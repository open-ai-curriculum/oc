## Profile Summary

Pennsylvania's official mathematics surface is presented through the Pennsylvania Core Standards for Mathematics, which include both Mathematical Content and Mathematical Practice. The official mathematics PDF is marked `January 2013` in the public stateboard document.

Pennsylvania also publishes related early-learning standards documents that use mathematics-oriented areas such as Numbers and Operations, Algebraic Concepts, Geometry, and Measurement, Data, and Probability.

For repository purposes, Pennsylvania is a baseline-derived profile with its own state-specific naming and adjacent early-learning standards surface.
