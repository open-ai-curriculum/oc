## Profile Summary

New Jersey's official standards surface identifies the `2023 New Jersey Student Learning Standards Mathematics` as the active mathematics content family. The state mathematics page describes K-8 organization into domains and high-school organization into conceptual categories, with explicit Standards for Mathematical Practice across all grades.

For repository purposes, New Jersey is a baseline-derived profile with a clear state-specific standards family identity.

## Relationship To Canonical Mathematics Content Baseline

- relationship type: `baseline-derived`
- core structure: `grade-level domains in K-8 and conceptual categories in high school, with explicit mathematical practices`
