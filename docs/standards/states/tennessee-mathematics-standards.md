## Profile Summary

The Tennessee Department of Education states that revised state math standards were adopted in February 2021 and implemented in classrooms during the 2023-2024 school year. The Tennessee State Board of Education standards review page further states that the current math standards will remain in effect until the 2031-2032 school year and that the next review begins in summer 2026.

Tennessee also elevates the eight Standards for Mathematical Practice and four literacy standards for mathematical proficiency as integral components of the K-12 mathematics standards.

For repository purposes, Tennessee is a baseline-derived profile with strong state-specific adjunct structures.
