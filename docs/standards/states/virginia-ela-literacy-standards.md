## Profile Summary

Virginia's official English standards surface is the `2024 Standards of Learning for English`. The Virginia Board of Education approved the 2024 English Standards of Learning on March 28, 2024, according to the official VDOE standards page.

Virginia also exposes supporting materials such as Understanding the Standards, instructional guides, assessment blueprints, and Literacy Act resources. These should be treated as adjacent implementation or interpretation surfaces rather than as replacements for the standards themselves.

For repository purposes, Virginia is a state-distinct literacy profile.
