## Profile Summary

New York uses the Next Generation English Language Arts Learning Standards as its current literacy standards family. Official NYSED guidance identifies the standards as revised in 2017, with implementation resources and a revised January 2023 timeline.

The state also publicly notes that the gradual removal of the earlier ELA Common Core standards began in 2022 and that the older Common Core surface remains relevant in specific transition contexts, including grades 11-12 references noted on the state standards page.

For repository purposes, New York is a baseline-derived literacy profile with explicit transition sensitivity.
