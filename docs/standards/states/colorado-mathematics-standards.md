## Profile Summary

Colorado's official mathematics standards surface presents the `2020 Mathematics Standards` online while indicating that the mathematics standards were adopted in 2018. The state provides standards by grade band, standards by grade, and adjacent support materials including Prepared Graduate Competencies, Mathematical Practices, Organizational Topics in Mathematics, and Extended Evidence Outcomes.

For repository purposes, Colorado is a state-distinct profile with a strong competency-and-outcome framing layered onto familiar mathematics content progressions.
