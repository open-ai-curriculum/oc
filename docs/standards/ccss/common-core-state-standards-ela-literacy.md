# Common Core State Standards For ELA And Literacy

## Purpose

This document codifies the Common Core State Standards for English Language Arts and Literacy in History/Social Studies, Science, and Technical Subjects.

## Public Source Surface

- CCSS ELA/literacy page: [corestandards.org/english-language-arts-standards/](https://corestandards.org/english-language-arts-standards/)

## Profile Summary

The official CCSS ELA/literacy page describes the standards family as including:

- English language arts standards
- literacy in history/social studies
- literacy in science
- literacy in technical subjects

The page also states that the College and Career Readiness Anchor Standards form the backbone of the ELA/literacy standards and that grade-specific standards add additional specificity. Beginning in grade 6, the literacy standards for history/social studies, science, and technical subjects are intended to supplement content standards in those areas rather than replace them.

## Structural Elements To Preserve

Future repository alignment to CCSS ELA/literacy should preserve:

- anchor standards
- strand
- grade or grade band
- literacy context
- content-area literacy designation for history/social studies, science, or technical subjects

## Relationship To NGSS

This artifact is especially relevant to later science standards work because the CCSS literacy standards in science and technical subjects are an explicit crosswalk surface for NGSS and science-domain artifacts.

## Claim Boundary

This document codifies the CCSS ELA/literacy family as a repository artifact. It does not replace subject-matter content standards in science, history, or technical subjects.
