# Social Studies Artifact Attachments

This directory contains machine-readable attachment registries that bind curriculum, assessment, intervention, and support artifacts to graph-native social studies entities.

## Purpose

These registries operationalize the [graph artifact attachment contract](../graph-artifact-attachment-contract.md) by giving the social studies graph a concrete attachment layer that can later drive curriculum compilation and adaptive navigation.

## Files

- `registry.schema.json`: canonical JSON Schema for social studies artifact attachment registries
- `inquiry-civics-entry-attachment-registry.yaml`: initial draft registry for the `Q1`, `C1`, and `H1` entry slice
- `geography-economics-systems-attachment-registry.yaml`: draft registry for the `G3`, `G4`, `E3`, and `E4` systems slice
- `capstone-public-argument-attachment-registry.yaml`: draft registry for the `Q6`, `H5`, `C5`, `G5`, `E5`, `A1`, and `A2` capstone slice

## Claim Boundary

The registry entries in this directory are draft attachment records. They define governed attachment intent and graph linkage. They do not by themselves prove that the referenced curriculum assets are authored, reviewed, or classroom-ready.
