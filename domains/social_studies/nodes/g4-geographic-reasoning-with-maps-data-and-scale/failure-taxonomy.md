# G4 Failure Taxonomy

### G4-FM-01 Map-Data Split

- description: learner reads the map and the data separately but does not integrate them into one geographic conclusion

### G4-FM-02 Scale Blindness

- description: learner ignores scale and overgeneralizes from a local or narrow representation

### G4-FM-03 Evidence-Claim Mismatch

- description: learner makes a geographic claim not supported by the map or data evidence

### G4-FM-04 Display Complexity Risk

- description: learner performance may be limited by multi-display complexity rather than by the geographic reasoning itself
