# A2 Failure Taxonomy

### A2-FM-01 Public Product Without Cross-Strand Integration

- description: learner produces a final public artifact that still behaves like a single-strand response rather than an integrated social studies capstone

### A2-FM-02 Medium Choice Without Message Fit

- description: learner selects a presentation medium or format that does not fit the intended audience claim structure or evidence load

### A2-FM-03 Model-Argument Misalignment

- description: learner's model argument and communication choices do not reinforce one another and the final product loses coherence

### A2-FM-04 Public-Communication Load Or Language Risk

- description: learner performance may be limited by public-communication demands medium complexity or language load rather than by capstone reasoning alone
