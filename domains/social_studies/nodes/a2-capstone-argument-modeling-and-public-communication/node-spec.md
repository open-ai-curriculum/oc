# A2 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `A2`
- name: `capstone_argument_modeling_and_public_communication`
- domain: `social_studies`
- support tier: `Incubation`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `social-studies-capstone-public-argument-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner integrates domain capstones into a final argument or model and communicates the result publicly with explicit audience, evidence, and medium choices.

## Dependencies

- `A1` interdisciplinary research and evidence synthesis
- `H6` historiography, public history, and capstone argument
- `C6` advanced civic argument, policy design, and participation
- `G6` geospatial analysis and policy application
- `E6` economic analysis, policy tradeoffs, and capstone modeling

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `A2` only when all of the following are true:

1. integrates the capstone work from multiple strands into one public-facing argument or model
2. chooses an appropriate communication medium and audience strategy
3. explains how evidence, modeling choices, and limitations shape the final public product
4. succeeds across changed capstone communication contexts
5. remains stable on delayed recheck

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.85`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `14`

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
