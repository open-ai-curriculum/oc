# A2 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `A2` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For A2-FM-01 Public Product Without Cross-Strand Integration

Primary interventions:

- require the final product to show explicit strand contributions
- use a cross-strand storyboard before final production
- compare integrated and pseudo-integrated public products

### For A2-FM-02 Medium Choice Without Message Fit

Primary interventions:

- compare the same argument in two media and justify one choice
- require a short rationale for audience medium and evidence fit
- contrast effective and overloaded communication formats

### For A2-FM-03 Model-Argument Misalignment

Primary interventions:

- use a coherence checklist linking model claim and communication choices
- compare aligned and misaligned capstone exemplars
- require revision notes that explain how the final format supports the argument

### For A2-FM-04 Public-Communication Load Or Language Risk

Primary interventions:

- reduce production load and preteach medium-specific communication terms
- stage the final product in outline storyboard and full form
- verify whether medium or language scaffolds materially change performance
