# G2 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `G2` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For G2-FM-01 Feature-Only Regioning

Primary interventions:

- compare two places and identify the shared regional feature
- sort places by one geographic criterion at a time
- use labeled region examples before fading supports

### For G2-FM-02 Human-Environment One-Way Reasoning

Primary interventions:

- ask how the environment affects people and how people affect the environment
- use paired cause-and-response examples
- map one interaction in both directions

### For G2-FM-03 Map-To-Region Disconnect

Primary interventions:

- point from a map feature to a region statement explicitly
- annotate the evidence needed for the claim
- compare justified and unjustified region groupings

### For G2-FM-04 Representation Or Vocabulary Risk

Primary interventions:

- simplify the map field and preteach key spatial terms
- use oral explanation plus pointing
- verify whether access adjustments change performance materially
