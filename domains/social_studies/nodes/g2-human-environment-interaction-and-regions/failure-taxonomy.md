# G2 Failure Taxonomy

### G2-FM-01 Feature-Only Regioning

- description: learner groups places by isolated features without a meaningful regional pattern

### G2-FM-02 Human-Environment One-Way Reasoning

- description: learner recognizes only one side of the interaction between people and environment

### G2-FM-03 Map-To-Region Disconnect

- description: learner can read a map feature but cannot use it to justify a region or interaction claim

### G2-FM-04 Representation Or Vocabulary Risk

- description: learner may be hindered by spatial vocabulary or display complexity rather than by geography reasoning alone
