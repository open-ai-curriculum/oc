# E2 Failure Taxonomy

### E2-FM-01 Producer-Consumer Role Swap

- description: learner confuses who produces and who consumes in the scenario

### E2-FM-02 Exchange-Chain Collapse

- description: learner identifies one role but cannot describe how goods services or money move through the market relationship

### E2-FM-03 Market-As-Place-Only

- description: learner treats the market only as a physical location rather than a relationship among participants and exchange

### E2-FM-04 Vocabulary Or Scenario-Familiarity Risk

- description: learner performance may be constrained by market vocabulary or unfamiliar scenario context rather than by economics reasoning alone
