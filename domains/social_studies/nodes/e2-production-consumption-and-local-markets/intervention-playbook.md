# E2 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `E2` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For E2-FM-01 Producer-Consumer Role Swap

Primary interventions:

- match market participants to their roles with concrete examples
- compare two exchanges with the roles labeled
- ask who makes, who buys, and who uses

### For E2-FM-02 Exchange-Chain Collapse

Primary interventions:

- trace one good or service through a short market chain
- use arrows to show what moves where
- narrate the exchange in sequence

### For E2-FM-03 Market-As-Place-Only

Primary interventions:

- compare a location with a market relationship
- focus on participants and exchange rather than the building
- use role-play to show the market interaction

### For E2-FM-04 Vocabulary Or Scenario-Familiarity Risk

Primary interventions:

- preteach producer consumer and market vocabulary
- start with familiar local examples
- allow oral explanation with visuals before written response
