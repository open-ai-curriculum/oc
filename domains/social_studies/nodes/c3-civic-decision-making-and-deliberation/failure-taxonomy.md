# C3 Failure Taxonomy

### C3-FM-01 Preference-Only Deliberation

- description: learner chooses an option based mainly on personal preference without civic reasons or evidence

### C3-FM-02 Option-Weighing Fragility

- description: learner can describe options but cannot compare tradeoffs or strengths meaningfully

### C3-FM-03 Norm-Evidence Disconnect

- description: learner cites a civic norm or a fact but does not connect it to the decision

### C3-FM-04 Discussion Or Language Constraint Risk

- description: learner may have a viable position but cannot express or sustain it in the current discussion mode
