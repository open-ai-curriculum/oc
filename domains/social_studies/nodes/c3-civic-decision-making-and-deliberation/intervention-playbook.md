# C3 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `C3` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For C3-FM-01 Preference-Only Deliberation

Primary interventions:

- separate what I like from what is fair or effective
- require one reason tied to evidence or civic norms
- compare a preference answer with a deliberative answer

### For C3-FM-02 Option-Weighing Fragility

Primary interventions:

- use a two-option compare chart
- identify one benefit and one drawback for each option
- narrow the choice set before widening it again

### For C3-FM-03 Norm-Evidence Disconnect

Primary interventions:

- link one civic norm to one piece of evidence explicitly
- use because therefore frames for decisions
- annotate where the supporting fact matters

### For C3-FM-04 Discussion Or Language Constraint Risk

Primary interventions:

- allow planning notes or sentence frames before discussion
- reduce turn complexity while preserving the same reasoning demand
- verify the position through brief structured response before full discussion
