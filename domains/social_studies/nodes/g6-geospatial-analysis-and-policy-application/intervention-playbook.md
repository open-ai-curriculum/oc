# G6 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `G6` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For G6-FM-01 Spatial Analysis Without Decision Link

Primary interventions:

- require every spatial claim to connect to a concrete planning or policy choice
- compare map analysis alone with map analysis tied to decision criteria
- use decision prompts that ask so what should happen here

### For G6-FM-02 Scale-Policy Mismatch

Primary interventions:

- compare local regional and national action options for the same case
- require one sentence on why the chosen scale fits the problem
- contrast scale-fit and scale-mismatch recommendations

### For G6-FM-03 Representation Trust Without Qualification

Primary interventions:

- require one limitation or uncertainty statement for the chosen representation
- compare strong and weak map-based evidence qualifications
- ask what the map cannot show before final recommendation drafting

### For G6-FM-04 Geospatial Tooling Or Vocabulary Risk

Primary interventions:

- reduce representation complexity and preteach geospatial terms
- provide a legend and layer-by-layer reading sequence before synthesis
- verify whether tooling or vocabulary scaffolds materially change performance
