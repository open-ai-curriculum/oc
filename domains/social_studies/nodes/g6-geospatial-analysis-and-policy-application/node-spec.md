# G6 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `G6`
- name: `geospatial_analysis_and_policy_application`
- domain: `social_studies`
- support tier: `Incubation`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `geospatial-policy-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner performs geospatial analysis, evaluates geographic evidence for policy use, and applies place-based reasoning to bounded public decisions.

## Dependencies

- `G5` global interdependence, migration, and sustainability
- `C4` constitutional principles and public policy
- `Q6` synthesis, argument, and public communication

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `G6` only when all of the following are true:

1. uses geospatial evidence to support a bounded policy or planning claim
2. explains how scale place and representation shape the decision
3. evaluates tradeoffs or risks in a spatially grounded policy context
4. succeeds in changed geospatial-analysis or policy-application contexts
5. remains stable on delayed recheck

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.85`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `14`
