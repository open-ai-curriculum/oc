# G6 Failure Taxonomy

### G6-FM-01 Spatial Analysis Without Decision Link

- description: learner analyzes spatial patterns correctly but does not connect them to the planning or policy choice that must be made

### G6-FM-02 Scale-Policy Mismatch

- description: learner recommends an action at the wrong scale or ignores how jurisdiction and scale shape the spatial decision

### G6-FM-03 Representation Trust Without Qualification

- description: learner treats a map model or spatial representation as decisive without naming its limits assumptions or uncertainty

### G6-FM-04 Geospatial Tooling Or Vocabulary Risk

- description: learner performance may be limited by geospatial vocabulary representation complexity or tooling conventions rather than by the underlying reasoning itself
