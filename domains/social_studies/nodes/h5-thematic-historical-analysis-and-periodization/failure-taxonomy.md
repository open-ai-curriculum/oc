# H5 Failure Taxonomy

### H5-FM-01 Theme Tracking Fragmentation

- description: learner notices events and examples but cannot track a theme across multiple eras or cases

### H5-FM-02 Period Boundary Arbitrary Or Unjustified

- description: learner divides history into periods using superficial dates or labels without defensible thematic justification

### H5-FM-03 Continuity-Change Imbalance

- description: learner overemphasizes either continuity or rupture and cannot explain how both shape the historical pattern

### H5-FM-04 Multi-Era Source Load Or Vocabulary Risk

- description: learner performance may be limited by multi-era evidence load or periodization vocabulary rather than by thematic historical reasoning alone
