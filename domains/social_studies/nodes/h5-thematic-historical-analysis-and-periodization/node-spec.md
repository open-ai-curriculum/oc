# H5 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `H5`
- name: `thematic_historical_analysis_and_periodization`
- domain: `social_studies`
- support tier: `Incubation`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `history-periodization-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner traces themes across eras, periodizes change over time, and explains how continuity and rupture shape historical interpretation.

## Dependencies

- `H4` sourcing, corroboration, and historical argument
- `Q5` evaluation of sources, models, and claims

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `H5` only when all of the following are true:

1. traces a theme or problem across multiple eras or periods
2. defends a period boundary or turning point using evidence
3. explains both continuity and change in the historical pattern
4. succeeds with changed era sets or thematic frames
5. remains stable on delayed recheck

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.85`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `14`
