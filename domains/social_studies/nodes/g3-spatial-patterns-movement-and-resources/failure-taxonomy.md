# G3 Failure Taxonomy

### G3-FM-01 Pattern-Spotting Without Explanation

- description: learner notices a spatial pattern but cannot explain what it suggests

### G3-FM-02 Movement-Path Fragility

- description: learner cannot trace or explain the movement of people goods or ideas across space coherently

### G3-FM-03 Resource Claim Without Map Support

- description: learner makes a resource-distribution claim without using representation evidence meaningfully

### G3-FM-04 Display Or Vocabulary Load Risk

- description: learner performance may be limited by map complexity or spatial vocabulary rather than by geography reasoning alone
