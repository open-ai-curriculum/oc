# G3 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `G3` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For G3-FM-01 Pattern-Spotting Without Explanation

Primary interventions:

- ask what the pattern suggests after each observation
- connect one map feature to one claim at a time
- compare explained and unexplained pattern observations

### For G3-FM-02 Movement-Path Fragility

Primary interventions:

- trace the path with arrows or sequencing supports
- narrate movement one step at a time
- compare two movement routes and identify the difference

### For G3-FM-03 Resource Claim Without Map Support

Primary interventions:

- require one map detail before making the claim
- annotate the resource evidence directly on the display
- contrast supported and unsupported claims

### For G3-FM-04 Display Or Vocabulary Load Risk

Primary interventions:

- simplify the display and preteach key spatial terms
- allow pointing and oral explanation
- verify whether access supports materially change performance
