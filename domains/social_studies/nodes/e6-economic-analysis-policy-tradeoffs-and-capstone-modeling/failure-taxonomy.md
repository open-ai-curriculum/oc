# E6 Failure Taxonomy

### E6-FM-01 Model Without Explicit Assumptions

- description: learner makes an economic recommendation but does not state the core assumptions or conditions that make the model plausible

### E6-FM-02 Tradeoff Compression

- description: learner collapses multiple economic tradeoffs into a single metric and misses important competing effects

### E6-FM-03 Policy Condition Omission

- description: learner gives an economic analysis that ignores the public-policy or institutional conditions that would shape the outcome

### E6-FM-04 Modeling-Language Or Representation Risk

- description: learner performance may be limited by modeling vocabulary symbolic representation or data-display complexity rather than by the underlying economic reasoning alone
