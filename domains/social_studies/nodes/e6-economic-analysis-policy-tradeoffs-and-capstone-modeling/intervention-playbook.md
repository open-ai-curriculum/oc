# E6 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `E6` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For E6-FM-01 Model Without Explicit Assumptions

Primary interventions:

- require the model to list assumptions before recommendation writing
- compare explicit-assumption and hidden-assumption analyses
- use prompts that ask what must be true for this model to hold

### For E6-FM-02 Tradeoff Compression

Primary interventions:

- require at least two competing metrics or stakeholder effects
- compare single-metric and multi-tradeoff recommendations
- use visible tradeoff matrices before final modeling

### For E6-FM-03 Policy Condition Omission

Primary interventions:

- require one public-policy or institutional condition in the explanation
- compare market-only and policy-conditioned analyses
- ask what rule subsidy tax or constraint changes the economic recommendation

### For E6-FM-04 Modeling-Language Or Representation Risk

Primary interventions:

- preteach modeling and tradeoff vocabulary
- simplify representations before full quantitative or qualitative comparison
- verify whether representation scaffolds materially change performance
