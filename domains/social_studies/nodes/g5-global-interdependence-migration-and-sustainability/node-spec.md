## Purpose

The learner explains how migration, interdependence, and sustainability pressures connect places across scales and shape geographic outcomes.

- `G4` geographic reasoning with maps, data, and scale
- `Q5` evaluation of sources, models, and claims

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `G5` only when all of the following are true:

1. explains how movement or interdependence connects places across scales
2. identifies interacting causes or constraints in migration or sustainability cases
3. reasons about environmental social and economic tradeoffs in a geographic system
4. succeeds in changed migration network or sustainability contexts
5. remains stable on delayed recheck
