# G5 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `G5` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For G5-FM-01 Local-Global Disconnect

Primary interventions:

- require one local effect and one wider system connection in the same response
- map flows between places before discussing outcomes
- compare isolated-place explanations with interdependence explanations

### For G5-FM-02 Migration Pattern Oversimplification

Primary interventions:

- require at least two interacting migration drivers or constraints
- compare push-pull explanations with more complex system explanations
- use case contrasts that show different migration pathways

### For G5-FM-03 Sustainability Tradeoff Blindness

Primary interventions:

- require one environmental one social and one economic consideration
- contrast single-goal solutions with tradeoff-aware responses
- use structured prompts for who benefits who bears costs and over what time scale

### For G5-FM-04 Systems-Language Or Multi-Scale Load Risk

Primary interventions:

- reduce representation complexity and preteach key terms
- sequence the case from local to regional to global before independent synthesis
- verify whether vocabulary or scale-reduction supports materially change performance
