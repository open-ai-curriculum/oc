# G5 Failure Taxonomy

### G5-FM-01 Local-Global Disconnect

- description: learner describes one place in isolation and misses how flows and interdependence connect outcomes across places and scales

### G5-FM-02 Migration Pattern Oversimplification

- description: learner explains migration or movement with a single cause and ignores interacting geographic drivers or constraints

### G5-FM-03 Sustainability Tradeoff Blindness

- description: learner treats sustainability as a slogan and does not reason through competing environmental social and economic constraints

### G5-FM-04 Systems-Language Or Multi-Scale Load Risk

- description: learner performance may be limited by systems vocabulary or multi-scale coordination demands rather than by geographic systems reasoning itself
