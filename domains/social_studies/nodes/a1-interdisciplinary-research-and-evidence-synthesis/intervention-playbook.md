# A1 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `A1` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For A1-FM-01 Single-Strand Dominance

Primary interventions:

- require evidence from at least two strands before a conclusion is accepted
- use a planning grid that assigns each strand a contribution to the question
- compare single-strand explanations with stronger interdisciplinary accounts

### For A1-FM-02 Cross-Domain Evidence Nonintegration

Primary interventions:

- require one sentence that explains how two strands connect or conflict
- sort evidence into grouped claims before drafting the synthesis
- model integration moves rather than parallel note taking

### For A1-FM-03 Research Question Drift

Primary interventions:

- freeze the research question in a visible planning frame
- check each piece of evidence against the stated question before use
- compare aligned and drifted syntheses before independent revision

### For A1-FM-04 Synthesis-Load Or Language Risk

Primary interventions:

- reduce source length and preteach key terms
- stage the synthesis one strand at a time before full integration
- verify whether vocabulary or synthesis-load supports materially change performance
