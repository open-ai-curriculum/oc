# A1 Failure Taxonomy

### A1-FM-01 Single-Strand Dominance

- description: learner relies on one disciplinary strand and does not integrate evidence from the other relevant strands

### A1-FM-02 Cross-Domain Evidence Nonintegration

- description: learner gathers evidence from multiple strands but keeps them parallel instead of synthesizing them into one account

### A1-FM-03 Research Question Drift

- description: learner shifts the research question midstream and loses alignment between the question evidence and conclusion

### A1-FM-04 Synthesis-Load Or Language Risk

- description: learner performance may be limited by dense language or cross-domain coordination demands rather than by synthesis reasoning alone
