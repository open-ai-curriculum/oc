# A1 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `A1`
- name: `interdisciplinary_research_and_evidence_synthesis`
- domain: `social_studies`
- support tier: `Incubation`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `social-studies-interdisciplinary-synthesis-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner frames a bounded interdisciplinary research problem, synthesizes evidence across history civics geography and economics, and produces a defensible cross-domain account.

## Dependencies

- `H5`
- `C5`
- `G5`
- `E5`
- `Q6` synthesis, argument, and public communication

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `A1` only when all of the following are true:

1. frames a bounded cross-domain question that can be investigated with available evidence
2. integrates evidence from multiple social studies strands into one coherent account
3. explains where disciplinary evidence fits together or remains in tension
4. succeeds across changed interdisciplinary problem contexts
5. remains stable on delayed recheck

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.85`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `14`

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
