# E5 Failure Taxonomy

### E5-FM-01 Indicator Reading Without System Meaning

- description: learner can read an indicator or trend but cannot explain what it suggests about the broader economic system

### E5-FM-02 Global Trade One-Way Thinking

- description: learner treats trade as automatically beneficial or harmful and ignores reciprocal effects or constraints

### E5-FM-03 Finance Detail Without Economic Judgment

- description: learner notices finance details like rates prices or exchange values but cannot use them to support a bounded economic judgment

### E5-FM-04 Macro-Language Or Representation Risk

- description: learner performance may be limited by macroeconomic vocabulary or chart-display complexity rather than by the underlying macroeconomic reasoning alone
