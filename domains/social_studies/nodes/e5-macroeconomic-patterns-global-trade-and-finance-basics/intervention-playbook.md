# E5 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `E5` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For E5-FM-01 Indicator Reading Without System Meaning

Primary interventions:

- connect each indicator to a short explanation of what it suggests about the broader economy
- compare trend description with economic interpretation
- use quick prompts asking what else would likely change if this pattern continues

### For E5-FM-02 Global Trade One-Way Thinking

Primary interventions:

- map reciprocal effects between trade partners before judgment
- require one benefit and one constraint for each side of the exchange
- compare one-way trade stories with interdependent trade explanations

### For E5-FM-03 Finance Detail Without Economic Judgment

Primary interventions:

- require the finance detail to change or sharpen the conclusion
- contrast named-but-unused finance facts with evidence that informs judgment
- use prompts that ask why this rate price or exchange value matters here

### For E5-FM-04 Macro-Language Or Representation Risk

Primary interventions:

- preteach core macroeconomic and trade terms
- simplify charts or tables before full interpretation
- verify whether vocabulary or representation supports materially change performance
