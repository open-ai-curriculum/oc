# H4 Failure Taxonomy

### H4-FM-01 Unsourced Argument

- description: learner makes a historical argument without attending to who produced the evidence or under what conditions

### H4-FM-02 Corroboration Blindness

- description: learner treats sources separately and does not compare them for agreement or tension

### H4-FM-03 Conflict-As-Error

- description: learner assumes conflicting sources mean one must simply be wrong instead of considering perspective, position, or limitation

### H4-FM-04 Source-Density Or Language Risk

- description: learner performance may be limited by the density or complexity of the source set rather than by historical reasoning alone
