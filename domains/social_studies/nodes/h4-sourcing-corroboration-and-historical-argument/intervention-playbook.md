# H4 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `H4` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For H4-FM-01 Unsourced Argument

Primary interventions:

- identify who, when, and why for each source before arguing
- use a sourcing header for every source
- compare an unsourced argument with a sourced one

### For H4-FM-02 Corroboration Blindness

Primary interventions:

- pair sources explicitly and ask what they agree or disagree on
- mark corroborating details in matching colors
- compare one-source and two-source arguments

### For H4-FM-03 Conflict-As-Error

Primary interventions:

- ask how perspective or position might shape the account
- compare conflict caused by bias, knowledge limits, or purpose
- model tension as evidence to interpret rather than to discard immediately

### For H4-FM-04 Source-Density Or Language Risk

Primary interventions:

- reduce the source set and preteach key terms
- allow annotation and oral reasoning before writing
- verify whether comprehension supports materially change performance
