# E6A Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `E6A` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For E6A-FM-01 Surface Recognition Without Structure

Primary interventions:

- model one correct example before independent work
- reduce the task to one structural move at a time
- require explicit attention to what counts in the node

### For E6A-FM-02 Organization Drift

Primary interventions:

- use an organizer that makes the node structure visible
- compare organized and disorganized responses
- prompt the learner to explain the sorting or sequencing rule

### For E6A-FM-03 Application Breakdown In Context

Primary interventions:

- rehearse transfer with one minimally changed context
- require a short why this still fits explanation
- compare original and changed task conditions

### For E6A-FM-04 Vocabulary Or Task-Load Risk

Primary interventions:

- preteach key terms and simplify directions
- reduce representation density before increasing complexity
- verify whether language or format supports materially change performance
