# H6 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `H6` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For H6-FM-01 Interpretation Comparison Collapse

Primary interventions:

- compare two interpretations with a shared evidence organizer
- require one claim about how the interpretations differ and why
- model interpretation-to-evidence matching before independent writing

### For H6-FM-02 Public-History Medium Blindness

Primary interventions:

- compare how the same historical claim changes across exhibit speech and essay formats
- require one sentence on audience and one on medium before drafting
- contrast effective and ineffective public-history framings of the same material

### For H6-FM-03 Capstone Claim Without Historiographic Position

Primary interventions:

- require the argument to name an interpretive position explicitly
- compare evidence summaries with arguments that take a historiographic stance
- use counterposition prompts before final claim writing

### For H6-FM-04 Interpretation-Density Or Public-History Vocabulary Risk

Primary interventions:

- reduce interpretive load and preteach public-history terms
- stage interpretation comparison before final public product design
- verify whether vocabulary or medium scaffolds materially change performance
