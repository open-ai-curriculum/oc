# C1 Failure Taxonomy

### C1-FM-01 Rule-Purpose Opacity

- description: learner can name a rule but cannot connect it to a shared purpose

### C1-FM-02 Role Confusion

- description: learner confuses who is responsible for what in the community setting

### C1-FM-03 Fairness-As-Preference

- description: learner treats fairness only as personal preference rather than as a shared norm or reasoned expectation

### C1-FM-04 Language Or Context Familiarity Risk

- description: learner may be hindered by unfamiliar community vocabulary or scenario context rather than by civic reasoning alone
