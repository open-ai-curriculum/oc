# H3 Failure Taxonomy

### H3-FM-01 Single-Cause Collapse

- description: learner reduces a historical event or change to one simplistic cause despite evidence of multiple influences

### H3-FM-02 Context Dropout

- description: learner makes a causal claim without using the relevant social, political, or material context

### H3-FM-03 Presentist Interpretation

- description: learner judges the past mainly through present assumptions rather than historically situated evidence

### H3-FM-04 Language Or Source-Load Risk

- description: learner performance may be limited by source density or vocabulary load rather than by historical reasoning alone
