# Q1 Failure Taxonomy

### Q1-FM-01 Observation Omission

- description: learner overlooks salient accessible features in the source or situation

### Q1-FM-02 Guess Over Observation

- description: learner reports unsupported assumptions as if they were directly observed

### Q1-FM-03 Question Disconnect

- description: learner asks a question unrelated to the source, task, or observed features

### Q1-FM-04 Access Or Language Barrier Risk

- description: learner response suggests the task may be limited by source access, response mode, or language demand rather than by inquiry reasoning alone
