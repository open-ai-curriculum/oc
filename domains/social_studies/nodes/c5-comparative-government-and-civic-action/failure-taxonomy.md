# C5 Failure Taxonomy

### C5-FM-01 Institution Label Without Comparison

- description: learner can identify institutions or system labels but cannot compare how they affect participation or power

### C5-FM-02 Action Choice Without System Fit

- description: learner selects a civic action without matching it to the relevant institutional pathway or context

### C5-FM-03 Comparative Pattern Overgeneralization

- description: learner overgeneralizes from one civic system and misses meaningful differences across cases

### C5-FM-04 Comparative-Civics Vocabulary Or Context Risk

- description: learner performance may be hindered by comparative-government language or unfamiliar civic context rather than by the underlying reasoning alone
