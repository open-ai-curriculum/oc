# C5 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `C5` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For C5-FM-01 Institution Label Without Comparison

Primary interventions:

- require one similarity and one difference across the compared systems
- use comparison matrices that focus on participation power and process
- model how labels alone differ from functional comparisons

### For C5-FM-02 Action Choice Without System Fit

Primary interventions:

- sort civic actions by which institution or process they target
- require a why this action fits here explanation before final choice
- contrast strong institutional matches with weak symbolic actions

### For C5-FM-03 Comparative Pattern Overgeneralization

Primary interventions:

- limit the comparison to a small number of variables first
- use counterexamples that show where the first pattern does not hold
- require evidence for each generalization across cases

### For C5-FM-04 Comparative-Civics Vocabulary Or Context Risk

Primary interventions:

- preteach core principle and policy terms
- anchor unfamiliar systems with a short glossary and case summary
- verify whether vocabulary or context supports materially change performance
