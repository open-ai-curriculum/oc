# C2 Failure Taxonomy

### C2-FM-01 Institution-Function Disconnect

- description: learner can name an institution but not connect it to its civic role or function

### C2-FM-02 Right-Responsibility Imbalance

- description: learner can name a right or a responsibility but not relate the two within the civic setting

### C2-FM-03 Process Sequence Fragility

- description: learner cannot maintain a basic sequence for how a civic process works

### C2-FM-04 Vocabulary Or Context-Load Risk

- description: learner may be hindered by institution-specific vocabulary or unfamiliar context rather than by civic reasoning alone
