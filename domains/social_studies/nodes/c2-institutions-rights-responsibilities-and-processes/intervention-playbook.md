# C2 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `C2` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For C2-FM-01 Institution-Function Disconnect

Primary interventions:

- match institutions to their basic functions
- compare two institutions with one clearly different role
- ask what problem this institution helps address

### For C2-FM-02 Right-Responsibility Imbalance

Primary interventions:

- pair each right with one matching responsibility
- compare examples where one is missing
- use sentence frames for with this right comes

### For C2-FM-03 Process Sequence Fragility

Primary interventions:

- sequence a simple civic process with cards or visuals
- narrate one step at a time
- compare correct and incorrect process orders

### For C2-FM-04 Vocabulary Or Context-Load Risk

Primary interventions:

- preteach essential civic vocabulary
- reduce the scenario to one institution and one process
- allow oral rehearsal before written response
