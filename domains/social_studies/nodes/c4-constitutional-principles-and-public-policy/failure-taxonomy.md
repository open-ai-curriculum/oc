# C4 Failure Taxonomy

### C4-FM-01 Principle-Name Without Use

- description: learner can name a civic or constitutional principle but cannot apply it to the policy question

### C4-FM-02 Policy Tradeoff Blindness

- description: learner favors one policy option without recognizing the civic tradeoff or constraint involved

### C4-FM-03 History-Policy Disconnect

- description: learner does not connect the relevant historical context to the policy reasoning where it matters

### C4-FM-04 Legal-Civic Vocabulary Risk

- description: learner performance may be hindered by abstract civic language rather than by the underlying reasoning alone
