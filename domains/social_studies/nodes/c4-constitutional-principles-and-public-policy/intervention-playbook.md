# C4 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `C4` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For C4-FM-01 Principle-Name Without Use

Primary interventions:

- pair one principle with one concrete policy implication
- compare correct and incorrect principle applications
- ask what this principle would require limit or protect here

### For C4-FM-02 Policy Tradeoff Blindness

Primary interventions:

- chart one benefit and one cost for each option
- require explanation of what is gained and what is constrained
- compare a one-sided and tradeoff-aware policy response

### For C4-FM-03 History-Policy Disconnect

Primary interventions:

- identify one historical example that clarifies the current issue
- anchor the policy question in the relevant historical context first
- compare decontextualized and historically grounded reasoning

### For C4-FM-04 Legal-Civic Vocabulary Risk

Primary interventions:

- preteach core principle and policy terms
- allow oral explanation with guided prompts
- simplify the scenario before increasing abstraction
