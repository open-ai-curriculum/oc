# Q2 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `Q2` status
- dependency posture for `Q1`
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner is comparing, grouping, and sequencing with a usable rule

Class-level view should highlight:

- who needs support with compare language
- who is sorting inconsistently
- who can perform with supports but cannot yet explain the rule
