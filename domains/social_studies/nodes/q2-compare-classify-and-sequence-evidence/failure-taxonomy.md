# Q2 Failure Taxonomy

### Q2-FM-01 Surface-Only Comparison

- description: learner notices isolated details but does not identify a meaningful similarity or difference

### Q2-FM-02 Unstable Classification Rule

- description: learner groups evidence inconsistently or cannot maintain a usable rule

### Q2-FM-03 Sequence Logic Drift

- description: learner orders events or evidence without a defensible temporal or logical basis

### Q2-FM-04 Explanation Or Language Compression Risk

- description: learner may have a workable comparison or classification idea but cannot express the rule clearly enough in the current response mode
