# Q4 Failure Taxonomy

### Q4-FM-01 Question-Representation Mismatch

- description: learner cannot connect the question to the most relevant representation or data source

### Q4-FM-02 Data Readout Without Use

- description: learner can read isolated details from the representation but does not use them to answer the investigation question

### Q4-FM-03 Investigation Scope Drift

- description: learner frames a question or conclusion that is too broad, unrelated, or unsupported by the available evidence

### Q4-FM-04 Representation Or Language Access Risk

- description: learner may be hindered by chart, table, map, or vocabulary load rather than by inquiry reasoning alone
