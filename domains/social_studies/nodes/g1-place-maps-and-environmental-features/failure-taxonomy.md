# G1 Failure Taxonomy

### G1-FM-01 Representation-Place Disconnect

- description: learner sees the map or image as isolated marks rather than a representation of a place

### G1-FM-02 Feature Identification Drift

- description: learner cannot identify salient environmental or place features consistently

### G1-FM-03 Scale Or Orientation Fragility

- description: learner loses meaning when the representation changes size orientation or perspective

### G1-FM-04 Access Risk In Spatial Display

- description: learner may be blocked by visual layout complexity or display format rather than by geographic reasoning alone
