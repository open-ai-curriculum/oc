# Q5 Failure Taxonomy

### Q5-FM-01 Face-Value Acceptance

- description: learner accepts a source, model, or claim without evaluating its support or limits

### Q5-FM-02 Feature-Notice Without Judgment

- description: learner notices source features but cannot use them to make an evaluative judgment

### Q5-FM-03 Stronger-Weaker Reversal

- description: learner misidentifies weaker support as stronger because of superficial features or confidence language

### Q5-FM-04 Source-Load Or Language Risk

- description: learner performance may be limited by dense language or complex source structure rather than by evaluation reasoning alone
