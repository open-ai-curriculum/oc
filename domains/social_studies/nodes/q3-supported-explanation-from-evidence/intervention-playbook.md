# Q3 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `Q3` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For Q3-FM-01 Claim Without Evidence

Primary interventions:

- require the learner to point to one supporting detail before stating the claim
- use claim-plus-because sentence frames
- compare supported and unsupported explanations side by side

### For Q3-FM-02 Evidence Without Connection

Primary interventions:

- ask how do you know after evidence selection
- model the bridge between evidence and explanation
- use one piece of evidence and one explanation at a time

### For Q3-FM-03 Source Drift

Primary interventions:

- mark what comes from the source and what comes from prior knowledge
- constrain the response to source-grounded details first
- compare grounded and ungrounded answers

### For Q3-FM-04 Expression Or Language Constraint Risk

Primary interventions:

- allow highlighting or pointing plus brief oral explanation
- reduce expressive load while keeping the same reasoning demand
- verify the evidence link through structured prompts before inferring conceptual absence
