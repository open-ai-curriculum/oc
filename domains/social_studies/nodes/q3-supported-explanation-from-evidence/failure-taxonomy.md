# Q3 Failure Taxonomy

### Q3-FM-01 Claim Without Evidence

- description: learner states an explanation or opinion but does not support it with relevant evidence

### Q3-FM-02 Evidence Without Connection

- description: learner identifies evidence but does not explain how it supports the claim

### Q3-FM-03 Source Drift

- description: learner pulls in irrelevant prior knowledge or opinion instead of staying grounded in the source

### Q3-FM-04 Expression Or Language Constraint Risk

- description: learner may have the relevant idea but cannot express the explanation-evidence link clearly enough in the current response mode
