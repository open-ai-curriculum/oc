## Purpose

The learner develops sourcing corroboration and historical argument through the transfer and contextual use slice of the governed social studies graph.

## Dependencies

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `H4D` only when all of the following are true:

1. completes the transfer and contextual use work with direct evidence
2. explains the structure or reasoning used in the node
3. applies the node in a bounded changed context
4. remains stable on delayed recheck

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.85`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `14`
