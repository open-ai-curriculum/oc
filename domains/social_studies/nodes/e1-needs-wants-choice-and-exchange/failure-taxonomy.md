# E1 Failure Taxonomy

### E1-FM-01 Need-Want Collapse

- description: learner treats all desired goods or actions as equivalent without distinguishing need from want

### E1-FM-02 Choice-Constraint Blindness

- description: learner does not recognize that choices are limited by available resources or options

### E1-FM-03 Exchange Meaning Drift

- description: learner can describe getting something but not the give-and-take structure of exchange

### E1-FM-04 Language Or Cultural-Context Risk

- description: learner performance may be distorted by vocabulary or unfamiliar scenario context rather than by economics reasoning alone
