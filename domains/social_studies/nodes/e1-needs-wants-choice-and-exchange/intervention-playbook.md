# E1 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `E1` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For E1-FM-01 Need-Want Collapse

Primary interventions:

- sort examples into need and want with explicit reasons
- compare the same item across different contexts
- ask what is necessary versus preferred

### For E1-FM-02 Choice-Constraint Blindness

Primary interventions:

- limit the available options and name the constraint
- use tokens or concrete resources to show tradeoffs
- ask what cannot also be chosen and why

### For E1-FM-03 Exchange Meaning Drift

Primary interventions:

- model simple exchanges with objects or role-play
- identify what each person gives and gets
- compare exchange with one-way receiving

### For E1-FM-04 Language Or Cultural-Context Risk

Primary interventions:

- use familiar and culturally legible scenarios
- preteach key vocabulary such as choice exchange need and want
- allow oral or acted response before written explanation
