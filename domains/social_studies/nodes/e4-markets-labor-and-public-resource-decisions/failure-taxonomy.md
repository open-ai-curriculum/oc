# E4 Failure Taxonomy

### E4-FM-01 Labor-Market Disconnect

- description: learner can identify labor or market actors but cannot explain how their choices interact

### E4-FM-02 Public-Resource Tradeoff Blindness

- description: learner favors one allocation choice without recognizing the competing uses or tradeoffs

### E4-FM-03 Civic-Economic Boundary Split

- description: learner treats public-resource decisions as purely economic or purely civic and misses the interaction between the two

### E4-FM-04 Abstractness Or Vocabulary Risk

- description: learner performance may be limited by abstract labor or resource-allocation language rather than by the underlying reasoning alone
