# E4 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `E4` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For E4-FM-01 Labor-Market Disconnect

Primary interventions:

- map the roles and choices of the labor-market participants
- trace one decision through to a market effect
- compare connected and disconnected labor scenarios

### For E4-FM-02 Public-Resource Tradeoff Blindness

Primary interventions:

- chart two competing uses for the same limited resource
- require one gain and one loss for the allocation choice
- compare a one-sided and tradeoff-aware response

### For E4-FM-03 Civic-Economic Boundary Split

Primary interventions:

- identify the economic and civic features of the same decision
- connect one institution to one resource-allocation effect
- compare a purely economic reading with an integrated one

### For E4-FM-04 Abstractness Or Vocabulary Risk

Primary interventions:

- preteach core labor and allocation terms
- use concrete diagrams or tokens before abstraction
- simplify the scenario before increasing complexity
