# Q6 Failure Taxonomy

### Q6-FM-01 Claim Assembly Without Coherent Support

- description: learner states a position but does not connect the claim to a coherent set of supporting evidence

### Q6-FM-02 Evidence Selection Without Synthesis

- description: learner gathers pieces of evidence but does not integrate them into a unified explanation or argument

### Q6-FM-03 Audience-Blind Communication

- description: learner communicates as if all audiences need the same framing and support regardless of purpose or context

### Q6-FM-04 Multi-Source Coordination Or Language Risk

- description: learner performance may be limited by dense language or multi-source coordination demands rather than by synthesis and communication reasoning alone
