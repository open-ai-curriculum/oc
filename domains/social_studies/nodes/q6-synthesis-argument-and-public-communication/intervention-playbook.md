# Q6 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `Q6` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For Q6-FM-01 Claim Assembly Without Coherent Support

Primary interventions:

- require a claim plus at least two linked pieces of support
- model evidence-to-claim sentence frames before independent production
- use a short coherence checklist for claim evidence and conclusion alignment

### For Q6-FM-02 Evidence Selection Without Synthesis

Primary interventions:

- sort evidence into groups before drafting the explanation
- require one sentence that explains how two sources fit together
- contrast a list of details with a synthesized explanation

### For Q6-FM-03 Audience-Blind Communication

Primary interventions:

- specify audience role and purpose before composing
- compare two versions of the same message for different audiences
- use prompts that ask what this audience needs to know first

### For Q6-FM-04 Multi-Source Coordination Or Language Risk

Primary interventions:

- reduce source length and preteach key terms
- use annotation plus oral rehearsal before final communication
- verify whether source-load reduction materially changes performance
