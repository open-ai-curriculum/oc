# H1 Failure Taxonomy

### H1-FM-01 Time-Order Instability

- description: learner cannot maintain a basic before-now-after sequence

### H1-FM-02 Source-Community Disconnect

- description: learner sees the source but does not connect it to people, place, or community memory

### H1-FM-03 Personal-Past Collapse

- description: learner collapses personal memory, shared history, and fiction into the same category without distinction

### H1-FM-04 Language Or Background-Access Risk

- description: learner performance may be limited by vocabulary or unfamiliar community context rather than chronology reasoning alone
