# E3 Failure Taxonomy

### E3-FM-01 Incentive-No-Behavior Link

- description: learner can name an incentive but cannot explain how it affects behavior or decisions

### E3-FM-02 Trade-As-One-Sided Gain

- description: learner sees trade only from one side and does not track the relationship across participants

### E3-FM-03 System-Connection Dropout

- description: learner understands isolated exchanges but not the simple system connecting roles and outcomes

### E3-FM-04 Vocabulary Or Abstractness Risk

- description: learner performance may be constrained by abstract economic language rather than by the underlying reasoning alone
