# E3 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `E3` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For E3-FM-01 Incentive-No-Behavior Link

Primary interventions:

- compare behavior before and after an incentive change
- ask what choice becomes more likely and why
- use one actor and one incentive at a time

### For E3-FM-02 Trade-As-One-Sided Gain

Primary interventions:

- identify what each side gains and gives
- compare one-sided taking with reciprocal trade
- use simple two-party trade diagrams

### For E3-FM-03 System-Connection Dropout

Primary interventions:

- map the participants and flows in a short system
- trace one decision through to an outcome
- compare disconnected and connected system diagrams

### For E3-FM-04 Vocabulary Or Abstractness Risk

Primary interventions:

- preteach key terms with concrete examples
- use oral explanation plus arrows or diagrams
- simplify the scenario before increasing abstraction
