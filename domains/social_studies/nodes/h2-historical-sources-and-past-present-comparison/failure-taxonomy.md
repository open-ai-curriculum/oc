# H2 Failure Taxonomy

### H2-FM-01 Source-As-Decoration

- description: learner notices the source but does not use it as evidence for the comparison

### H2-FM-02 Past-Present Blur

- description: learner cannot maintain the distinction between what belongs to the past and what belongs to the present

### H2-FM-03 Comparison Without Context

- description: learner states a difference or similarity but without a meaningful historical feature or context

### H2-FM-04 Background-Knowledge Or Language Risk

- description: learner performance may be limited by unfamiliar vocabulary or context rather than by source-comparison reasoning alone
