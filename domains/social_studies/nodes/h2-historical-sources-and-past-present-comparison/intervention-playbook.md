# H2 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `H2` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For H2-FM-01 Source-As-Decoration

Primary interventions:

- require pointing to or quoting one source detail before answering
- annotate the source together for usable evidence
- compare source-grounded and source-free answers

### For H2-FM-02 Past-Present Blur

Primary interventions:

- sort details into then and now columns
- connect each source detail to a timeline anchor
- revisit one feature across two time periods only

### For H2-FM-03 Comparison Without Context

Primary interventions:

- narrow the comparison focus to one feature
- use same because and different because frames
- supply candidate contexts and have the learner choose the best fit

### For H2-FM-04 Background-Knowledge Or Language Risk

Primary interventions:

- preteach essential vocabulary and source labels
- begin with familiar community contexts
- reduce expressive load while preserving source use
