# C6 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `C6`
- name: `advanced_civic_argument_policy_design_and_participation`
- domain: `social_studies`
- support tier: `Incubation`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `advanced-civic-design-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner builds advanced civic arguments, designs bounded policy responses, and matches participation strategies to institutional and public constraints.

## Dependencies

- `C5` comparative government and civic action
- `E5` macroeconomic patterns, global trade, and finance basics
- `Q6` synthesis, argument, and public communication

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `C6` only when all of the following are true:

1. develops a bounded civic or policy argument with explicit reasoning about tradeoffs and constraints
2. designs a plausible policy or participation pathway that fits the institutional setting
3. explains how economic or public conditions shape the civic recommendation
4. succeeds in changed policy-design or participation contexts
5. remains stable on delayed recheck

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.85`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `14`
