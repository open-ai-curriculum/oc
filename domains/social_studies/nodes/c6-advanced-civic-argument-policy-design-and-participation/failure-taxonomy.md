# C6 Failure Taxonomy

### C6-FM-01 Policy Proposal Without Tradeoff Discipline

- description: learner proposes a civic or policy response without making the relevant constraints tradeoffs or costs explicit

### C6-FM-02 Participation Pathway Mismatch

- description: learner recommends participation moves that do not fit the actual institutional decision pathway or public setting

### C6-FM-03 Civic-Economic Disconnect

- description: learner builds a civic recommendation without accounting for the relevant economic conditions or policy constraints

### C6-FM-04 Policy-Design Vocabulary Or Complexity Risk

- description: learner performance may be hindered by policy-design language or scenario complexity rather than by the underlying civic reasoning alone
