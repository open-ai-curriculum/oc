# C6 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `C6` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For C6-FM-01 Policy Proposal Without Tradeoff Discipline

Primary interventions:

- require one stated benefit one cost and one constraint for every proposal
- compare strong policy recommendations with slogan-level proposals
- use tradeoff grids before final recommendation writing

### For C6-FM-02 Participation Pathway Mismatch

Primary interventions:

- map the recommended action to the actual decision pathway
- require one sentence on who can act where and when
- compare institution-fit and institution-mismatch participation plans

### For C6-FM-03 Civic-Economic Disconnect

Primary interventions:

- require one economic condition that strengthens or weakens the civic recommendation
- compare civics-only and civic-economic explanations
- use prompts that ask what resource or cost condition changes the proposal

### For C6-FM-04 Policy-Design Vocabulary Or Complexity Risk

Primary interventions:

- preteach policy-design and participation terms
- simplify the case into actors options and constraints before argument writing
- verify whether vocabulary or case-structure scaffolds materially change performance
