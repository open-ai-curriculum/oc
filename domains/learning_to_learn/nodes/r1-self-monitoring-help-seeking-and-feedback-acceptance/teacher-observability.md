# R1 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `R1` status
- dependency posture for `A1`
- direct self-monitoring and help-seeking stability
- transfer and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who stays stuck without signaling
- who asks for help in a way that does not yield a usable next step
- who withdraws after feedback instead of attempting revision
