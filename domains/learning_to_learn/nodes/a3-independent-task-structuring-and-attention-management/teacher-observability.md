# A3 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `A3` status
- dependency posture for `A2` and `R3`
- whether the learner creates a usable task sequence before starting
- whether attention and pacing hold through checkpoints
- dominant failure mode
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who starts complex work without structure
- who loses the plan under attention load
- who abandons work instead of adapting the plan
