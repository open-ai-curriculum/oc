# M4 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: confirm consolidation, synthesis, and longer-horizon retrieval
- pass rule: consolidates and retrieves connected ideas across a longer interval in at least 4 of 5 opportunities

### G2. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm the routine across units or domains
- pass rule: one successful cross-unit or cross-domain consolidation routine

### G3. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 35 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct performance improves but remains inconsistent
- `provisional`: direct and transfer gates pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or regresses structurally
