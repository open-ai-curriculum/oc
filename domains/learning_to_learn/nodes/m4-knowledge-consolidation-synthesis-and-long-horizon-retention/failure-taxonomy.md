# M4 Failure Taxonomy

### M4-FM-01 Fragmented Retention Plan

- description: learner uses short-term study moves without a usable long-horizon consolidation or retrieval schedule

### M4-FM-02 Unsynthesized Knowledge

- description: learner remembers pieces in isolation but cannot connect them into a coherent structure

### M4-FM-03 Long-Delay Retrieval Collapse

- description: learner can perform after short spacing but cannot retrieve or apply the knowledge after a longer interval
