# M1 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `M1` status
- dependency posture for `A1` and `C1`
- direct encoding stability
- transfer and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who only receives information passively
- who cannot hold encoded information long enough for immediate reuse
- who uses a representation or chunking pattern that distorts the content structure
