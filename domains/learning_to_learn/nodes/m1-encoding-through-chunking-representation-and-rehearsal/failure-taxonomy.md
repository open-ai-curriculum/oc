# M1 Failure Taxonomy

### M1-FM-01 Passive Exposure Reliance

- description: learner watches or hears the information but does not perform an active encoding move

### M1-FM-02 Fragile Holding Of Information

- description: learner begins an encoding routine but cannot hold the information long enough for immediate reuse

### M1-FM-03 Mismatched Representation Or Chunking

- description: learner uses a representation or grouping that does not preserve the needed structure of the information
