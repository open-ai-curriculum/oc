# T1 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: confirm near-transfer use of a known learning strategy
- pass rule: near-transfer strategy use in at least 4 of 5 structured opportunities

### G2. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm use across more than one changed but related context
- pass rule: successful application across at least two changed but related contexts or representations

### G3. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 21 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct performance improves but remains inconsistent
- `provisional`: direct and transfer gates pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or regresses structurally
