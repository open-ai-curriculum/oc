# T1 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `T1` status
- dependency posture for `C2`, `M2`, `S2`, and `R2`
- direct near-transfer stability
- transfer and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who is locked to the original context
- who loses the strategy in a new representation
- who transfers without checking whether the move still fits
