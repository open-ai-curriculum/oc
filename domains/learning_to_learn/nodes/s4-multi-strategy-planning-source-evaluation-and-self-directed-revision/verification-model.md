# S4 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: confirm multi-strategy planning, source evaluation, and self-directed revision
- pass rule: compares strategies and revises the approach using evaluated sources in at least 4 of 5 opportunities

### G2. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm the routine across changed subject or task contexts
- pass rule: one successful changed-context self-directed revision cycle in at least two contexts

### G3. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 28 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct performance improves but remains inconsistent
- `provisional`: direct and transfer gates pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or regresses structurally
