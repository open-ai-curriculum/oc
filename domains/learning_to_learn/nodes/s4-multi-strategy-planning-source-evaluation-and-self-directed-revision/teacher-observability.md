# S4 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `S4` status
- dependency posture for `S3` and `R4`
- whether the learner compares more than one strategy
- whether source choices are justified by fit and credibility
- dominant failure mode
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who fixates on one strategy
- who cannot judge source quality
- who waits passively for correction rather than revising
