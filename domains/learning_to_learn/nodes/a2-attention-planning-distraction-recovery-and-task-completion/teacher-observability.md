# A2 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `A2` status
- dependency posture for `A1` and `R1`
- direct distraction-recovery stability
- transfer and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who starts without a usable distraction plan
- who drifts and does not recover
- who cannot complete the bounded work cycle
