# C3 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `C3` status
- dependency posture for `C2` and `R2`
- direct diagnosis-and-questioning stability
- transfer and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who labels confusion globally
- who misclassifies the type of misunderstanding
- who cannot ask a precise question after finding the breakdown
