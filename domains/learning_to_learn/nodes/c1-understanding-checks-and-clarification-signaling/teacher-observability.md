# C1 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `C1` status
- dependency posture for `A1`
- direct clarification-signaling stability
- transfer and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who remains silently confused
- who asks for help but cannot identify what is unclear
- who receives clarification but does not use it to reattempt the task
