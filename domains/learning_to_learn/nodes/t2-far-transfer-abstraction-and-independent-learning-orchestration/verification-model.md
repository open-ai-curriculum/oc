# T2 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: confirm far transfer, abstraction, and independent learning orchestration
- pass rule: orchestrates independent learning and far transfer in at least 4 of 5 substantially changed opportunities

### G2. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm the routine across clearly non-isomorphic contexts
- pass rule: one successful far-transfer routine in at least two distinctly changed contexts

### G3. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 35 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct performance improves but remains inconsistent
- `provisional`: direct and transfer gates pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or regresses structurally
