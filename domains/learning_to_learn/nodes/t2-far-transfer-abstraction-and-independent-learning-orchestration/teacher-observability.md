# T2 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `T2` status
- dependency posture for `T1`, `A3`, `M4`, `S4`, and `R4`
- whether the learner states the abstract principle behind transfer
- whether the learner can coordinate independent learning in a novel context
- dominant failure mode
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who transfers only when the surface stays similar
- who cannot abstract the reusable principle
- who cannot orchestrate the full routine independently under novelty
