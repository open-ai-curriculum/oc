# S1 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `S1` status
- dependency posture for `A1` and `C1`
- direct practice-routine stability
- transfer and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who guesses without checking
- who stops after the first error
- who uses a routine that does not fit the task well enough to support progress
