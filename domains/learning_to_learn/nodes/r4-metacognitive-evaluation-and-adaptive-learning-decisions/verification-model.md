# R4 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: confirm evidence-based evaluation of learning quality and adaptive decision making
- pass rule: evaluates learning quality and makes adaptive study decisions in at least 4 of 5 opportunities

### G2. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm the routine across changed learning contexts
- pass rule: one successful changed-context adaptive decision cycle in at least two contexts

### G3. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 28 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct performance improves but remains inconsistent
- `provisional`: direct and transfer gates pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or regresses structurally
