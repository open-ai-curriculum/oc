# R4 Intervention Playbook

## Purpose

Map `R4` failure modes to bounded intervention responses.

## Intervention Rules

### For R4-FM-01 Surface-Metric Misread

Primary interventions:

- compare feeling of fluency with actual retrieval evidence
- require one evidence statement before self-evaluation is accepted
- use delayed checks to challenge overconfidence

Expected effect:

- learner grounds evaluation in evidence rather than comfort

Reverification target:

- one evaluation-and-evidence cycle

### For R4-FM-02 No Adaptive Decision

Primary interventions:

- provide a short decision menu for pace strategy or help options
- require the learner to pick one adaptive move and justify it
- reinforce change based on evidence rather than passive persistence

Expected effect:

- learner makes an explicit adaptive decision when needed

Reverification target:

- one detect-and-adapt cycle

### For R4-FM-03 Overcorrection Or Instability

Primary interventions:

- limit changes to one variable at a time
- compare the new decision against the evidence that triggered it
- stabilize the routine before adding further adjustments

Expected effect:

- learner makes measured adaptive changes instead of impulsive ones

Reverification target:

- one bounded revision cycle

## Escalation Rule

If the same dominant failure mode remains blocking after two bounded intervention cycles, escalate to human review of anxiety load, executive demand, and available support clarity rather than only increasing reflective prompts.
