# R4 Failure Taxonomy

### R4-FM-01 Surface-Metric Misread

- description: learner judges learning quality from ease, speed, or familiarity instead of evidence of retention or transfer

### R4-FM-02 No Adaptive Decision

- description: learner notices struggle but does not change strategy, pacing, or support choices

### R4-FM-03 Overcorrection Or Instability

- description: learner changes plans impulsively or excessively without evidence, creating unstable learning routines
