# M2 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `M2` status
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner repeats one consistent unit without gaps or overlaps
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who switches units inside one measurement
- who leaves gaps or overlaps
- who can place units but does not connect them to the measured quantity
