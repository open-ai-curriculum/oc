# M2 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Measurement

- measure with repeated equal units
- identify valid versus invalid iterated-unit use
- compare quantities using the same repeated unit

### Family B. Explanation

- explain why units must be repeated consistently
- explain why gaps, overlaps, or unit switching are invalid

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify why a measurement attempt is invalid

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across length, area, and classroom object comparison contexts
