# T1 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `row-column language rehearsal`: Use explicit row and column naming while building and reading arrays.
- `array repair tasks`: Present broken arrays and require the learner to diagnose and correct unequal structure.
- `cover-and-reveal structure prompts`: Hide part of an array and ask the learner to infer totals from rows and columns.
