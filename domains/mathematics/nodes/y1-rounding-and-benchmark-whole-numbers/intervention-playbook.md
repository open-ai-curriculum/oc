# Y1 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `neighbor-benchmark placement`: Place exact numbers between adjacent benchmarks before rounding.
- `benchmark-choice contrasts`: Compare multiple plausible benchmark levels and justify the best fit for the task.
- `number-line approximation routines`: Use bounded lines to show why a rounded value is near the original number.
