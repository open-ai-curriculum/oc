# Y1 Failure Taxonomy

## Classified Failure Modes

- `benchmark_place_confusion`: Chooses the wrong place or benchmark level for the task.
- `rounding_rule_without_magnitude`: Applies a remembered procedure without interpreting the size relationship.
- `interval_anchor_loss`: Cannot locate the original number between neighboring benchmarks.
- `context_scale_mismatch`: Uses a benchmark that is inappropriate for the quantity or question scale.
