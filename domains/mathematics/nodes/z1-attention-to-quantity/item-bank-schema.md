# Z1 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Quantity Attention

- distinguish one from many
- notice added or removed items
- match broader or smaller quantity states

### Family B. Explanation

- explain how the learner knows the quantity changed or stayed the same

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when a claimed no-change state is wrong after an addition or removal

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across objects, pictures, and familiar daily-life quantity contexts
