# Z1 Failure Taxonomy

### Z1-FM-01 One-Many Instability

- description: learner does not reliably distinguish one from many

### Z1-FM-02 Quantity-Change Blindness

- description: learner does not notice when items were added or removed

### Z1-FM-03 Quantity-State Confusion

- description: learner confuses same, different, more, and less in broad quantity situations

### Z1-FM-04 Context Boundary

- description: learner attends to quantity in one familiar setting but not in another
