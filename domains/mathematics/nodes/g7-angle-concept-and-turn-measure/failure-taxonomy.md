# Failure Taxonomy

- `ray_length_bias`: Judges angle size by ray length rather than amount of turn.
- `turn_direction_confusion`: Confuses clockwise and counterclockwise or the direction of rotation.
- `benchmark_turn_instability`: Does not retain quarter-turn, half-turn, and full-turn benchmarks across contexts.
- `static_shape_only_reasoning`: Can recognize angle pictures but cannot connect them to movement or directional change.
