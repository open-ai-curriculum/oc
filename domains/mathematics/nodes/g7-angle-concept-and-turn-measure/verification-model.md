# Verification Model

`G7` is verified through four gate types.

## Gates

- `G1 direct performance`: learner meets at least 0.90 accuracy on direct tasks
- `G2 meaning`: learner explains the geometric concept in words, gestures, or supported notation
- `G3 transfer`: learner succeeds in at least one varied context beyond the most practiced format
- `G4 retention`: learner passes a delayed recheck after 14 days

## Item Families

- direct execution or classification
- explanation and justification
- error diagnosis
- transfer task in a new context
