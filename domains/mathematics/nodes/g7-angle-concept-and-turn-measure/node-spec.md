# G7 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `G7`
- name: `angle_concept_and_turn_measure`
- domain: `mathematics`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `upstream-readiness-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner interprets angles as turns, compares angle size, and connects directional change to formal angle language.

## Dependencies

- `G2`
- `G6`


## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `G7` only when all of the following are true:

1. identifies an angle as the amount of turn between two rays or directions
2. compares angles as larger, smaller, or equal based on turn size rather than side length
3. recognizes benchmark turns such as quarter-turn, half-turn, and full-turn in movement and diagrams
4. succeeds across body movement, benchmark-turn contexts, and drawn angle comparisons
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing


## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `14`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- show quarter-turn, half-turn, and full-turn actions and match them to diagrams
- compare two angles by amount of turn even when side lengths differ
- use spatial language to describe clockwise and counterclockwise turning situations
- connect angle size to changes in direction in familiar contexts, with clock-like turns used only as optional examples


## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
