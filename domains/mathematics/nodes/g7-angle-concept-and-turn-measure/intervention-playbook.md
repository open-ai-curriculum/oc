# Intervention Playbook

## `ray_length_bias`

- overlay angle comparisons with different ray lengths
- hinged-arm demonstrations showing fixed turn with variable arm length
- sort-by-turn-not-length tasks

## `turn_direction_confusion`

- body-turn rehearsal with verbal direction cues
- clockwise versus counterclockwise matching sets
- arrow-path tracing before angle comparison

## `benchmark_turn_instability`

- daily benchmark-turn warmups
- clock-face and navigation turn references
- movement-to-diagram matching routines

## `static_shape_only_reasoning`

- physical turning tasks before drawn representations
- map-route direction change stories
- teacher modeling of turn language linked to rays
