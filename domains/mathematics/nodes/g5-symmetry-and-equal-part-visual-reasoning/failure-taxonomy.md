# G5 Failure Taxonomy

### G5-FM-01 Symmetry Detection Weakness

- description: learner cannot reliably identify whether a figure has a symmetry line

### G5-FM-02 Fold-Line Misplacement

- description: learner proposes a fold or split that does not preserve matching structure

### G5-FM-03 Equal-Part Visual Misjudgment

- description: learner misjudges whether a partition creates equal parts

### G5-FM-04 Non-Structural Cue Bias

- description: learner uses color, decoration, or other non-structural features instead of geometric form
