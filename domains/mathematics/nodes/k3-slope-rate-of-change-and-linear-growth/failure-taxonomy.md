# Failure Taxonomy

- `change_ratio_breakdown`: Fails to coordinate change in output with change in input when computing rate.
- `steepness_without_rate_meaning`: Describes a graph as steep or flat without connecting that to quantitative rate of change.
- `sign_direction_confusion`: Misreads positive, negative, or zero slope in relation to graph direction or contextual change.
- `representation_growth_disconnect`: Can use one representation of linear growth but loses the same rate meaning in another.
