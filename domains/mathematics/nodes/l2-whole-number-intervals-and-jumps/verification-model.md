# L2 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

- `G1` direct performance gate
- `G2` meaning gate
- `G3` transfer gate
- `G4` retention gate

## Evidence Targets

- represents addition and subtraction moves as jumps on a line
- counts intervals rather than only reading landing labels
- explains distance between two positions as a measurable span
- matches story changes to number-line movement

## Minimum Verification Rule

A learner does not clear `L2` until direct accuracy, explanation, transfer, and delayed retention evidence all meet threshold.
