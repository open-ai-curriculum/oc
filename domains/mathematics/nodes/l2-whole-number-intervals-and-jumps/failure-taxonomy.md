# L2 Failure Taxonomy

## Classified Failure Modes

- `landing_label_only`: Reports the endpoint but cannot account for the interval traveled.
- `jump_count_drift`: Loses count or step size when representing repeated jumps.
- `distance_change_blend`: Confuses how far apart two numbers are with the act of moving between them.
- `step_size_instability`: Uses inconsistent jump sizes on the same line.
