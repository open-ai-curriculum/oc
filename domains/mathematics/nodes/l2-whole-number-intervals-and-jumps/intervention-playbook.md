# L2 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `trace-the-span routines`: Have learners mark and count the full interval between positions.
- `fixed-step jump rehearsals`: Constrain all jumps in a representation to one stated step size.
- `change-versus-distance sorts`: Sort tasks by whether they ask for movement, endpoint, or interval.
