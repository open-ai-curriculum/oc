# Failure Taxonomy

- `representation_selection_misalignment`: Chooses a representation or model family that does not fit the structure of the advanced-vector and parametric task.
- `parametric_polar_disconnect`: Treats parametric or polar forms as notation changes instead of structurally different ways of representing behavior and location.
- `trigonometric_modeling_fragmentation`: Applies periodic-function procedures without linking parameters, graphs, contexts, and repeated behavior.
- `local_global_behavior_confusion`: Notices isolated features but cannot distinguish which local or global behavior matters for the claim being made.
