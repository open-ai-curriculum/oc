# Teacher Observability

## Look-fors

- Chooses a familiar representation quickly without checking whether it actually preserves the structure of the advanced-vector and parametric task.
- Treats parametric or polar work as symbol translation rather than as a different way to express motion, angle, or location.
- Uses trigonometric parameters procedurally but cannot explain what is repeating, shifting, or scaling in context.
- Notices isolated graph features but cannot identify whether local or global behavior is what the conclusion depends on.
