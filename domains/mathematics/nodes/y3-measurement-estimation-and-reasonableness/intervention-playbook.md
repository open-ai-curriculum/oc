# Y3 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `benchmark-object matching`: Match familiar objects to plausible benchmark measurements.
- `reasonable-or-not sorts`: Sort reported measurements by plausibility and justify the decision.
- `estimate-before-measure routines`: Estimate first, then measure and compare to the estimate.
