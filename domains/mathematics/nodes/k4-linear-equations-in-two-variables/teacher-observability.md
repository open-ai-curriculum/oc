# Teacher Observability

## Look-fors

- Generates pairs or tables without checking whether they actually satisfy the equation.
- Talks about the equation as a symbolic object but not as a relationship among paired values.
- Reverses x- and y-values or substitutes them inconsistently when testing a pair.
- Can work in one representation of a two-variable linear equation but not preserve the same relation across table, graph, and verbal form.
