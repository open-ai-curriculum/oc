# Failure Taxonomy

- `pair_equation_mismatch`: Treats the equation and ordered pairs as disconnected objects and generates or accepts non-satisfying pairs.
- `symbol_relation_disconnect`: Reads the equation procedurally without understanding it as a rule for related pairs of values.
- `substitution_pair_breakdown`: Substitutes ordered-pair values inconsistently or into the wrong variable positions.
- `representation_relation_disconnect`: Can use one representation of the linear equation but loses the same relationship in another.
