# Intervention Playbook

## `additive_pattern_misclassification`

- contrast additive and proportional tables with same-looking sequences
- teacher modeling of same-factor checks
- sort tasks for proportional versus non-proportional relationships

## `scale_invariance_breakdown`

- equivalent-ratio build routines
- constant-factor rehearsal across several pairs
- ratio table row-to-row scaling annotations

## `table_extension_without_structure`

- require factor explanations for each extension step
- missing-value tasks with non-adjacent rows
- normalize-to-unit and rebuild routines

## `representation_split`

- translate one proportional relationship across word, table, graph-prep, and equation forms
- match cards across representation types
- teacher compare-and-connect routines
