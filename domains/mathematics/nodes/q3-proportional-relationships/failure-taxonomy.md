# Failure Taxonomy

- `additive_pattern_misclassification`: Treats repeated additive change as sufficient evidence of proportionality.
- `scale_invariance_breakdown`: Cannot preserve the same multiplicative relationship across multiple pairs.
- `table_extension_without_structure`: Extends tables by pattern imitation without understanding constant multiplicative comparison.
- `representation_split`: Can reason in one representation such as a table but loses the relationship in word or symbolic form.
