# M10 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

- `G1` direct performance gate
- `G2` meaning gate
- `G3` transfer gate
- `G4` retention gate

## Evidence Targets

- reads time on analog or digital clocks in bounded early increments
- orders events by start time and duration
- calculates simple elapsed intervals using count-on reasoning
- explains the difference between a clock reading and a span of time

## Minimum Verification Rule

A learner does not clear `M10` until direct accuracy, explanation, transfer, and delayed retention evidence all meet threshold.
