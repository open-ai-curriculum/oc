# S2 Failure Taxonomy

## Classified Failure Modes

- `sign_rule_instability`: Applies sign rules inconsistently or only by brittle memorization.
- `subtraction_rule_confusion`: Blends subtraction structure with multiplication or inverse-sign heuristics.
- `inverse_operation_gap`: Cannot use inverse relationships to check or explain signed computations.
- `magnitude_sign_mismatch`: Produces sign and magnitude combinations that conflict with the operation context.
