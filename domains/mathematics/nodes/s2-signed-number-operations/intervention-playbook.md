# S2 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `operation-family contrasts`: Contrast addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division cases that look superficially similar but have different structures.
- `signed-line composition routines`: Use directed jumps and cancellation pairs to ground additive operations.
- `inverse-check rehearsals`: Require each operation to be verified by an inverse or contextual reasonableness check.
