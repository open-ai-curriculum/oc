# T7 Failure Taxonomy

## Classified Failure Modes

- `quotient_remainder_role_confusion`: Cannot distinguish the quotient from the leftover remainder in interpretation.
- `partial_quotient_disconnect`: Uses or sees division steps without understanding the repeated decomposition structure.
- `remainder_context_misread`: Calculates a remainder but interprets it incorrectly for the context.
- `inverse_check_gap`: Does not verify the quotient and remainder against the original dividend and divisor.
