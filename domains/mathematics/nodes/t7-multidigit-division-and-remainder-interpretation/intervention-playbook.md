# T7 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `build-divide-reconstruct cycles`: Solve a division problem, then reconstruct the dividend from quotient, divisor, and remainder.
- `partial-quotient annotation`: Annotate each division step with the grouped quantity it removes.
- `remainder-meaning contrasts`: Compare contexts where a remainder is kept, redistributed, or rounded by interpretation.
