# Failure Taxonomy

- `sequence_series_process_blur`: Treats sequences, series, and partial sums as interchangeable procedures instead of distinct limiting structures.
- `convergence_reasoning_instability`: Applies convergence claims or tests mechanically without understanding why the structure supports the conclusion.
- `taylor_local_model_breakdown`: Uses polynomial approximation without tracking locality, accuracy, or what function behavior is actually being approximated.
- `advanced_change_context_drift`: Carries out BC-level procedures without identifying whether the claim depends on convergence, approximation quality, or another advanced calculus structure.
