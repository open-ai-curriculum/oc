# M3 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Comparison

- compare measurements taken with different-sized units
- identify which unit is larger or smaller from the count
- interpret same-quantity different-count scenarios

### Family B. Explanation

- explain why smaller units yield larger counts
- explain why count alone does not determine magnitude

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify why a count-only measurement conclusion is incorrect

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across length, area, amount, and equal-part contexts
