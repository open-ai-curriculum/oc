# T6 Failure Taxonomy

## Classified Failure Modes

- `partial_product_place_loss`: Computes subproducts without preserving their place-value meaning.
- `compression_without_structure`: Uses compressed written steps without being able to explain the decomposed structure.
- `area_model_disconnect`: Cannot relate rectangular or area structure to symbolic multiplication work.
- `recombination_error`: Calculates plausible partial products but combines them incorrectly.
