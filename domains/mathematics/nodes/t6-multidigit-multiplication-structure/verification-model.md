# T6 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

- `G1` direct performance gate
- `G2` meaning gate
- `G3` transfer gate
- `G4` retention gate

## Evidence Targets

- decomposes multidigit factors into place-value components
- computes partial products and recombines them coherently
- connects area or rectangular models to symbolic multiplication work
- explains why the multiplication method preserves total value and structure

## Minimum Verification Rule

A learner does not clear `T6` until direct accuracy, explanation, transfer, and delayed retention evidence all meet threshold.
