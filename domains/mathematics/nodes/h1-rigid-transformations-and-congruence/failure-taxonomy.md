# Failure Taxonomy

- `motion_rule_execution_breakdown`: Misapplies the direction, center, or magnitude of a rigid transformation in coordinate or grid work.
- `preservation_meaning_gap`: Names a transformation but cannot explain which properties remain unchanged and why congruence is preserved.
- `correspondence_label_drift`: Loses image/preimage point matching or mixes original and transformed labels during reasoning.
- `visual_congruence_overreach`: Declares figures congruent by appearance without establishing a valid rigid-motion relationship.
