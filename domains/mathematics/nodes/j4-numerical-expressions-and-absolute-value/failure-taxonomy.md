# Failure Taxonomy

- `structure_tracking_breakdown`: Loses grouping or operation structure when evaluating rational-number expressions.
- `absolute_value_as_sign_removal`: Treats absolute value as a mechanical sign change rather than as magnitude or distance from zero.
- `grouping_order_blindness`: Ignores parentheses, symbol role, or operation order in bounded expression cases.
- `context_expression_transfer_breakdown`: Can evaluate symbolically but loses expression or absolute-value meaning in verbal, number-line, or contextual forms.
