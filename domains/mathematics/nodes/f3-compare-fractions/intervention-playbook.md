# F3 Intervention Playbook

### For F3-FM-01 Whole Number Bias

- use same-whole visual comparisons
- require benchmark checks before final answer

### For F3-FM-02 Denominator Magnitude Error

- compare same-numerator fractions with drawings
- emphasize smaller pieces when denominator increases

### For F3-FM-03 Strategy Fragility

- rehearse multiple comparison routes
- require explanation of why each route works
