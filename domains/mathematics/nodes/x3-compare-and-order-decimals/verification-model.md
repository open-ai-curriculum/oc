# X3 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

- `G1` direct performance gate
- `G2` meaning gate
- `G3` transfer gate
- `G4` retention gate

## Evidence Targets

- compares decimals correctly using aligned place-value reasoning
- orders short decimal sets by magnitude
- uses benchmarks such as zero, one half, and one in bounded cases
- justifies comparisons with place or magnitude language

## Minimum Verification Rule

A learner does not clear `X3` until direct accuracy, explanation, transfer, and delayed retention evidence all meet threshold.
