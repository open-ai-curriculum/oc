# X3 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `aligned-place comparison routines`: Compare decimals only after aligning units by place.
- `benchmark sorting`: Sort decimals around anchor values such as 0, 0.5, and 1.
- `contrastive false-order tasks`: Repair incorrect orderings caused by whole-number bias.
