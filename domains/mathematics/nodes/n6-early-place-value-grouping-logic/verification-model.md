# N6 Verification Model

## Verification Intent

`N6` verifies whether the learner has generalized from teen numbers into early place-value reasoning with tens and ones.

## Gate Set

### G1 Direct Performance

- target: accurate tens-and-ones representation, interpretation, and comparison
- threshold: `>= 0.90`

### G2 Meaning

- target: explanation of why place changes value role
- threshold: successful explanation in at least `2 of 3` prompts

### G3 Transfer

- target: success across changed forms such as base-ten blocks, drawings, and numeral comparisons
- threshold: at least `1` successful transfer task

### G4 Retention

- target: durable grouped-place reasoning after spacing
- threshold: `>= 0.85` on delayed recheck after about `21` days

## Verification Notes

- direct symbol reading alone is insufficient
- comparison items should require tens-first reasoning
- at least one item must distinguish digit identity from place-based value
