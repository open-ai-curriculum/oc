# Failure Taxonomy

- `correspondence_ratio_drift`: Misaligns corresponding sides or angles, leading to invalid congruence or similarity claims.
- `scale_factor_meaning_gap`: Computes or cites scale factor mechanically without understanding what stays fixed and what changes.
- `congruence_similarity_conflation`: Treats congruent and similar figures as interchangeable or uses the wrong justification for each.
- `visual_match_overreliance`: Accepts or rejects figure relationships by appearance instead of transformation and proportional structure.
