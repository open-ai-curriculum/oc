# Y4 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `Y4`
- name: `fraction_benchmark_estimation`
- domain: `mathematics`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `upstream-readiness-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner uses fraction benchmarks to estimate magnitude and judge the reasonableness of fraction comparisons and operations.

## Dependencies

- `F3`
- `L4`

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `Y4` only when all of the following are true:

1. uses benchmark fractions to estimate and compare magnitude
2. applies benchmark reasoning to judge plausibility of fraction operations
3. connects fraction estimates to number-line position and whole-size reasoning
4. succeeds across symbolic, visual, and diagnostic transfer tasks
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.88`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `21`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- estimates fraction magnitude with benchmarks such as 0, 1/2, and 1
- judges whether fraction sums or differences are plausible relative to benchmarks
- uses number-line position to support fraction reasonableness judgments
- explains why a fraction is near, below, or above a benchmark

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
