# Y4 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

- `G1` direct performance gate
- `G2` meaning gate
- `G3` transfer gate
- `G4` retention gate

## Evidence Targets

- estimates fraction magnitude with benchmarks such as 0, 1/2, and 1
- judges whether fraction sums or differences are plausible relative to benchmarks
- uses number-line position to support fraction reasonableness judgments
- explains why a fraction is near, below, or above a benchmark

## Minimum Verification Rule

A learner does not clear `Y4` until direct accuracy, explanation, transfer, and delayed retention evidence all meet threshold.
