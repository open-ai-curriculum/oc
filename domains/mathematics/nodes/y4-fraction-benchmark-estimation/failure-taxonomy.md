# Y4 Failure Taxonomy

## Classified Failure Modes

- `benchmark_anchor_absence`: Cannot anchor fraction magnitude to familiar reference values.
- `operation_reasonableness_gap`: Computes or reads fraction results without checking plausibility.
- `whole_number_estimation_transfer_error`: Transfers whole-number estimation habits to fractions without respecting unit size.
- `number_line_benchmark_disconnect`: Cannot use fraction location on a line to support estimate quality.
