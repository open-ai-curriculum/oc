# Y4 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `benchmark placement routines`: Place key benchmark fractions before estimating target values.
- `plausible-or-not fraction results`: Sort candidate fraction results by reasonableness against benchmarks.
- `operation-estimate-first loops`: Estimate a fraction result before exact computation or verification.
