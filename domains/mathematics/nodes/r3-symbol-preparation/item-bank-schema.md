# R3 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Mapping

- match spoken part-whole language to early symbolic form
- match symbolic form to visual models
- distinguish selected-part count from whole-part count

### Family B. Explanation

- explain what each position in the notation is preparing to represent
- explain why swapping the positions changes the meaning

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify why a symbol swap or count-role confusion is incorrect

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across alternate visuals, oral prompts, and simple story contexts
