# R3 Failure Taxonomy

### R3-FM-01 Symbol Position Swap

- description: learner reverses selected-part count and whole-part count in early symbolic form

### R3-FM-02 Role Collapse

- description: learner does not understand that the two positions in the notation have different meanings

### R3-FM-03 Oral Symbol Disconnect

- description: learner can say the part-whole phrase orally but cannot map it reliably to written form

### R3-FM-04 Symbol Without Quantity

- description: learner uses the notation as a memorized pattern without connecting it to a visual or quantitative meaning
