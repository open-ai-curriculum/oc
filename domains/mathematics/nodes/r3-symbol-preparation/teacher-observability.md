# R3 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `R3` status
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner preserves top-role versus bottom-role meaning
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who still swaps selected-part count and whole-part count
- who can speak the phrase but not map it into notation
- who writes symbols without stable quantity meaning
