# R3 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `R3`
- name: `symbol_preparation`
- domain: `mathematics`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `upstream-readiness-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner prepares for formal fraction notation by connecting spoken part-whole language, equal-part visuals, and the positional logic that will become numerator and denominator meaning.

## Dependencies

- `R2`

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `R3` only when all of the following are true:

1. matches spoken phrases such as "one out of four equal parts" to an early symbolic form reliably
2. distinguishes the count of selected parts from the count of equal parts in the whole
3. explains that the two positions in the notation have different roles
4. succeeds across visual, oral, and symbolic-preparation prompts
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `14`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- match early fraction notation to a partitioned visual
- explain that the whole-part count belongs in a different role than the selected-part count
- avoid swapping the two roles when moving between oral and written forms
- carry the same meaning into changed contexts

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
