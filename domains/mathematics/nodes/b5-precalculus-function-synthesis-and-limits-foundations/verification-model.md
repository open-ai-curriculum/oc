# Verification Model

This node is verified through four gate types.

## Gates

- `G1 direct performance`: learner meets at least 0.80 accuracy on direct tasks
- `G2 meaning`: learner explains cross-family function synthesis and approaching-behavior meaning coherently
- `G3 transfer`: learner succeeds in at least one less-familiar precalculus synthesis context
- `G4 retention`: learner passes a delayed recheck after 21 days

## Item Families

- direct precalculus synthesis and limits-foundation tasks
- explanation and justification
- error diagnosis
- transfer in a varied precalculus context
