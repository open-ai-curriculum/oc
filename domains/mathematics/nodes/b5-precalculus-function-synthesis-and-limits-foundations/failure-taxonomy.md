# Failure Taxonomy

- `family_synthesis_fragmentation`: Chooses or combines function families without preserving the governing structure of the task.
- `approaching_behavior_opacity`: Talks about end behavior or continuity mechanically without understanding approaching behavior in a local neighborhood.
- `composition_representation_breakdown`: Loses coherence when a transformed, composed, or inverse function must be tracked across symbolic and graphical forms.
- `cross_family_feature_blindness`: Focuses on surface features and misses the dominant growth, oscillation, asymptotic, or local-behavior feature that controls the situation.
