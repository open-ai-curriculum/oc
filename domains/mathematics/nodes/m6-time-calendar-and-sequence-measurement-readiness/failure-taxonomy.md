# M6 Failure Taxonomy

### M6-FM-01 Sequence-Order Weakness

- description: learner cannot keep the ordered sequence stable

### M6-FM-02 Relative-Time Language Weakness

- description: learner cannot use before, after, yesterday, today, or tomorrow reliably

### M6-FM-03 Cycle-Structure Weakness

- description: learner does not recognize repeated daily or weekly cycles

### M6-FM-04 Context-Bounded Performance

- description: learner succeeds only in memorized contexts and fails when the sequence representation changes
