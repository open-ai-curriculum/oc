# M1 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Attribute Reasoning

- identify measurable attributes
- compare objects on one intended attribute
- distinguish relevant from irrelevant attributes

### Family B. Explanation

- explain which attribute is being compared
- explain why one comparison result does not automatically apply to another attribute

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when the wrong attribute is being used

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across length, area, amount, and classroom object contexts
