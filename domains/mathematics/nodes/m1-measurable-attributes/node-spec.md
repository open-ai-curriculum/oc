# M1 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `M1`
- name: `measurable_attributes`
- domain: `mathematics`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `upstream-readiness-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner recognizes that objects and quantities have attributes such as length, area, and amount that can be compared and later measured.

## Dependencies

- `C4`

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `M1` only when all of the following are true:

1. identifies measurable attributes in objects or quantities
2. compares attributes such as longer, shorter, more, less, larger area, or smaller area
3. explains which attribute is being compared
4. succeeds across more than one kind of attribute or context
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `14`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- identify when length, area, or amount is the relevant attribute
- compare two objects on the intended attribute
- explain that objects may differ on one attribute but not another
- prepare for formal measurement by recognizing what can be measured

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
