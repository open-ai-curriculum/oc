# M1 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `M1` status
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner identifies the intended measurable attribute
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who cannot identify the attribute being compared
- who uses one visible cue for every comparison
- who can compare length but not transfer to area or amount
