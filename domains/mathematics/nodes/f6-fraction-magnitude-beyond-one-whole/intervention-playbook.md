# Intervention Playbook

## `whole_number_override`

- whole-plus-fraction visual decompositions
- teacher modeling of numerator counting unit fractions across whole boundaries
- non-example analysis contrasting 5/4 with 5 and 4

## `mixed_improper_disconnect`

- equivalence tables between mixed and improper forms in bounded cases
- build-one-whole-then-extra routines
- match cards showing the same magnitude in both forms

## `one_whole_boundary_confusion`

- group unit fractions into wholes before naming the remainder
- shade-and-compose activities across multiple wholes
- count past one whole using unit-fraction language

## `comparison_reset_error`

- benchmark comparisons around one whole
- order sets containing values just below and above one
- number-line comparisons centered on the one-whole mark
