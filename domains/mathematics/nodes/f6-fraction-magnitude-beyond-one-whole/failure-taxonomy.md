# Failure Taxonomy

- `whole_number_override`: Treats the numerator and denominator as separate whole-number signals and misses the magnitude beyond one.
- `mixed_improper_disconnect`: Cannot connect mixed-number notation and improper-fraction notation as equivalent representations.
- `one_whole_boundary_confusion`: Loses track of when fractional parts have composed a full additional whole.
- `comparison_reset_error`: Resets comparison reasoning at one whole and mishandles magnitudes just above one.
