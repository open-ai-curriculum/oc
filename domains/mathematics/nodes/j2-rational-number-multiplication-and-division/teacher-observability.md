# Teacher Observability

## Look-fors

- Carries out multiplication or division steps without tracking what happened to the unit or scale.
- Chooses multiplication when division is needed, or division when multiplication is needed, in rational contexts.
- Applies sign rules procedurally without being able to explain the meaning of the result.
- Computes symbolically but cannot interpret the quotient or product in a rate, scaling, or grouping situation.
