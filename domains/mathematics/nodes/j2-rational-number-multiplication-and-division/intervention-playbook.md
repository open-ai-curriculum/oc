# Intervention Playbook

## `unit_scale_breakdown`

- use fraction-of-a-quantity and scale-model routines before symbolic compression
- annotate input unit, scale factor, and resulting unit in each task
- compare multiplication and division situations through explicit unit tracing

## `inverse_structure_confusion`

- sort situations into scale-up, scale-down, unit-rate, and unknown-factor structures
- rehearse inverse links between multiplication and division with rational numbers
- use missing-factor tables before free symbolic solving

## `sign_rule_without_meaning`

- use directed scaling and debt/gain contexts for signed products and quotients
- compare sign outcomes with concrete meaning before symbolic generalization
- teacher think-alouds separating magnitude reasoning from direction reasoning

## `rate_context_transfer_breakdown`

- translate among rate tables, scaling stories, group-size contexts, and symbolic forms
- pair each symbolic quotient or product with a unit interpretation question
- teacher compare-and-connect routines across symbolic, tabular, and contextual rational multiplicative situations
