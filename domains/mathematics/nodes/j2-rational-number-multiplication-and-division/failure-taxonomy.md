# Failure Taxonomy

- `unit_scale_breakdown`: Carries out multiplication or division without preserving unit and scale meaning.
- `inverse_structure_confusion`: Fails to distinguish when a rational situation calls for multiplication versus division or loses inverse-operation meaning.
- `sign_rule_without_meaning`: Produces signs procedurally for products or quotients without a coherent directional or scaling explanation.
- `rate_context_transfer_breakdown`: Can compute symbolically but loses meaning in rate, grouping, scaling, or contextual applications.
