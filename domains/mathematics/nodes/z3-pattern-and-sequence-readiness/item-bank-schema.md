# Z3 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Pattern And Sequence Reasoning

- identify repeating patterns
- continue a pattern
- identify the next element in an ordered sequence

### Family B. Explanation

- explain what repeats
- explain why a next element is correct

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify why a proposed next element breaks the pattern or sequence

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across blocks, pictures, sounds, or simple symbol-like contexts
