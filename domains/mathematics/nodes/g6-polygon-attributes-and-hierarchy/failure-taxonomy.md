# Failure Taxonomy

- `nondefining_attribute_bias`: Classifies shapes by size, color, or orientation instead of defining geometric attributes.
- `attribute_omission`: Misses one or more defining attributes when naming or sorting polygons.
- `hierarchy_reversal`: Fails to understand inclusive class relationships among polygon categories.
- `vocabulary_label_confusion`: Uses shape labels inconsistently or maps labels to the wrong attribute set.
