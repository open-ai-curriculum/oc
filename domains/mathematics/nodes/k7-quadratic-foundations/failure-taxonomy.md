# Failure Taxonomy

- `representation_structure_disconnect`: Can work in one quadratic representation but loses the same structure when moving among graph, table, expression, and area model.
- `feature_meaning_collapse`: Names vertex, symmetry, or zeros mechanically without understanding their relationship meaning.
- `zero_solution_confusion`: Confuses factors, zeros, x-intercepts, or verified solutions in simple quadratic solve situations.
- `linear_overgeneralization`: Treats quadratic growth as if it were linear or fails to notice curved/non-constant-rate structure.
