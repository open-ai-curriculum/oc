# K7 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `K7`
- name: `quadratic_foundations`
- domain: `mathematics`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `upstream-readiness-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner interprets quadratic relationships as structured non-linear patterns, connects quadratic expressions to tables and graphs, and reasons about zeros, symmetry, and simple factored forms without reducing the topic to disconnected procedures.

## Dependencies

- `A2`
- `K2`
- `T1`


## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on this node only when all of the following are true:

1. identifies quadratic growth and quadratic structure across tables, graphs, and symbolic forms in bounded cases
2. explains how quadratic expressions, area models, and parabolic graphs describe the same relationship
3. interprets vertex, symmetry, and zeros in introductory contextual and graphical settings
4. solves simple quadratic equations in factored or visualized forms and verifies solutions
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing


## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.82`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `21`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- recognize non-constant first differences and connect them to quadratic growth in bounded tables
- match quadratic expressions, area models, tables, and parabolic graphs representing the same relationship
- explain introductory vertex, symmetry, and zero meaning in graphical or contextual form
- solve simple quadratics in factored or area-linked forms and verify candidate solutions


## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
