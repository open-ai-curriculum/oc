# G3 Failure Taxonomy

### G3-FM-01 Composition Weakness

- description: learner cannot combine parts to form a valid target shape

### G3-FM-02 Decomposition Weakness

- description: learner cannot see or produce smaller component shapes within a whole

### G3-FM-03 Rotation-and-Movement Confusion

- description: learner treats moved or rotated parts as different shape identities or unusable pieces

### G3-FM-04 Part-Whole Explanation Weakness

- description: learner can manipulate pieces but cannot explain how the parts and whole are related
