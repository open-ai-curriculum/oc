# S1 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `reference-point number-line routines`: Use zero-centered lines to stabilize comparison and location reasoning.
- `context translation loops`: Translate among temperature, elevation, debt, and motion representations of the same signed value.
- `state-versus-change contrasts`: Compare tasks that ask for location, direction, or net change and require explicit classification.
