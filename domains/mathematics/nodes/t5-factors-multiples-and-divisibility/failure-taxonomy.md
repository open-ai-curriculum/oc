# T5 Failure Taxonomy

## Classified Failure Modes

- `factor_multiple_role_confusion`: Confuses the roles of factors and multiples in a multiplication relationship.
- `divisibility_as_rule_only`: Applies divisibility as an unsupported trick without group or factor meaning.
- `remainder_blindness`: Claims divisibility without checking whether equal grouping leaves a remainder.
- `family_structure_disconnect`: Knows isolated facts but cannot connect them into multiplication families.
