# T5 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `array-family reconstruction`: Build multiplication families with arrays to show factor-multiple structure.
- `divisible-or-not sort`: Sort numbers by whether they group evenly and justify each decision.
- `factor-pair listing routines`: List factor pairs systematically rather than guessing from isolated facts.
