# E2 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

- `G1` direct performance gate
- `G2` meaning gate
- `G3` transfer gate
- `G4` retention gate

## Evidence Targets

- solves equations requiring multiple inverse steps in a defensible sequence
- keeps sign, operation, and transcription accuracy across intermediate lines
- justifies why a chosen sequence of steps is valid
- checks completed solutions against the original equation

## Minimum Verification Rule

A learner does not clear `E2` until direct accuracy, explanation, transfer, and delayed retention evidence all meet threshold.
