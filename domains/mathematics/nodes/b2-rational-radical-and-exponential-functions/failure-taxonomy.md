# Failure Taxonomy

- `family_constraint_fragmentation`: Solves symbolically without preserving domain restrictions or family-specific structural constraints.
- `behavior_source_opacity`: Knows graph features such as asymptotes or growth direction but cannot explain what structural feature causes them.
- `extraneous_or_excluded_solution_acceptance`: Accepts solutions that violate excluded values, radical-domain limits, or original-equation conditions.
- `representation_family_disconnect`: Can work in one representation but loses the connection between symbolic structure and advanced-function behavior.
