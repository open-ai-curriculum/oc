# U3 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Fair-Share Reasoning

- identify fair and unfair shares
- distribute objects or portions fairly
- critique candidate sharing situations

### Family B. Explanation

- explain why equal amount matters for fairness
- explain why same count alone may not be enough

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify why an unfair sharing situation is incorrect

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across objects, food-sharing, and partitioned-whole contexts
