# U3 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `U3` status
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner uses equal amount rather than equal piece count
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who treats same number of pieces as sufficient for fairness
- who can describe fairness but not execute it
- who cannot transfer fairness from sets to wholes
