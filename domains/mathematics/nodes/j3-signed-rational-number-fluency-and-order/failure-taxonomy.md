# Failure Taxonomy

- `reference_point_instability`: Does not use zero, benchmark values, or consistent number-line structure when comparing signed rational numbers.
- `sign_magnitude_collapse`: Treats sign and magnitude as one blur and misreads which value is greater or farther from zero.
- `mixed_form_ordering_breakdown`: Cannot compare or order signed fractions and decimals when the forms differ.
- `operation_order_context_breakdown`: Can compute some signed rational operations but loses meaning in contextual, table, or number-line forms.
