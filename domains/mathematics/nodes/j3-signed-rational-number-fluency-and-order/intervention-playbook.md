# Intervention Playbook

## `reference_point_instability`

- anchor comparisons to zero, one-half, one, and other benchmark reference points
- require number-line placement before symbolic ordering in mixed-form tasks
- use distance-from-zero rehearsals alongside left-right ordering routines

## `sign_magnitude_collapse`

- separate sign language from absolute-size language in teacher think-alouds
- compare pairs of values that isolate sign versus magnitude effects
- rehearse statements such as "greater value" versus "greater distance from zero"

## `mixed_form_ordering_breakdown`

- convert or benchmark mixed fraction-decimal forms before ordering
- use matched-value card sorts across decimal and fraction notation
- require justification by benchmark or number-line location rather than intuition alone

## `operation_order_context_breakdown`

- translate among symbolic expressions, movement contexts, tables, and number-line representations
- pair each operation with a reasonableness and direction check
- teacher compare-and-connect routines across symbolic, visual, and contextual signed rational situations
