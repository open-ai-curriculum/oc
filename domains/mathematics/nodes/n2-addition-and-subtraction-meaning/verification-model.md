# N2 Verification Model

## Verification Intent

`N2` verifies whether the learner interprets addition and subtraction as quantity actions and chooses operations for reasons tied to the situation.

## Gate Set

### G1 Direct Performance

- target: correct representation of joining, separating, and compare situations
- threshold: `>= 0.90`

### G2 Meaning

- target: explanation of why an operation fits the situation
- threshold: successful explanation in at least `2 of 3` prompts

### G3 Transfer

- target: success when the same meaning appears in a changed story frame or a missing-part form
- threshold: at least `1` successful transfer task

### G4 Retention

- target: durable interpretation after spacing
- threshold: `>= 0.85` on delayed recheck after about `14` days

## Verification Notes

- direct correctness without operation meaning is insufficient
- item families must include both result-unknown and part-unknown situations
- compare situations should be included to prevent overfitting to simple join/take-away forms
