# N2 Intervention Playbook

### N2-FM-01 Action-Quantity Disconnect

- use concrete join and separate actions with immediate recounting
- require the learner to narrate what changed and what stayed the same

### N2-FM-02 Operation Reversal

- contrast paired stories where one quantity grows and the other shrinks
- sort story cards into "put together" and "take away" categories before solving

### N2-FM-03 Story-to-Symbol Drift

- move from manipulatives to drawings to equations in a fixed sequence
- require a spoken explanation for each symbol in the equation

### N2-FM-04 Missing-Part Blindness

- use covered-part tasks where the learner must infer the unseen quantity
- rehearse compare stories with bar drawings or matched sets
