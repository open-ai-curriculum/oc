# N2 Failure Taxonomy

### N2-FM-01 Action-Quantity Disconnect

- description: learner cannot map a quantity action to the resulting change in the whole

### N2-FM-02 Operation Reversal

- description: learner selects addition where subtraction is required or subtraction where addition is required

### N2-FM-03 Story-to-Symbol Drift

- description: learner can act out the story but cannot produce or interpret a matching equation

### N2-FM-04 Missing-Part Blindness

- description: learner handles result-unknown tasks but breaks when the unknown is a starting part or a compared amount
