# N2 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `N2`
- name: `addition_and_subtraction_meaning`
- domain: `mathematics`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `upstream-readiness-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner understands addition and subtraction as meaningful actions on quantities and can connect those actions to stories, objects, drawings, and symbols.

## Dependencies

- `N1`

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `N2` only when all of the following are true:

1. identifies whether a situation involves joining, separating, or comparing quantities
2. models addition and subtraction situations with objects, drawings, or equations
3. explains why an operation matches the situation rather than choosing a symbol by guesswork
4. succeeds across story, object, and symbolic forms
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `14`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- represent a joining story with addition
- represent a taking-away or missing-part story with subtraction
- explain how the total changes when parts are added or removed
- connect concrete actions to symbolic expressions such as `4 + 3` or `7 - 2`

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
