# T3 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `unknown-role comparison tasks`: Pair share-unknown and group-unknown problems and require explicit contrast.
- `build-and-check with groups`: Use counters or drawings to build quotient proposals and verify against the original total.
- `inverse equation rehearsals`: Translate division results back into multiplication statements to verify fit.
