# T3 Failure Taxonomy

## Classified Failure Modes

- `unknown_role_confusion`: Cannot determine whether the missing value is number of groups or size of share.
- `remainder_structure_blindness`: Produces or accepts leftover quantities without interpreting what they mean in context.
- `inverse_link_gap`: Cannot connect division outcomes back to multiplication structure.
- `share_group_mixup`: Treats fair-share and measurement division situations as interchangeable without analysis.
