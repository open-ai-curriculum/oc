# L1 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `equal-interval rebuilds`: Reconstruct lines from matched unit intervals before placing labels.
- `direction contrast tasks`: Explicitly compare correct and reversed greater-than movement on a line.
- `blank-line placement routines`: Move from fully labeled strips to minimally labeled lines with anchor points.
