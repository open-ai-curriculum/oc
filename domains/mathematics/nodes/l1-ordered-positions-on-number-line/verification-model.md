# L1 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

- `G1` direct performance gate
- `G2` meaning gate
- `G3` transfer gate
- `G4` retention gate

## Evidence Targets

- places bounded whole numbers in correct order on a line
- uses left-right direction consistently to interpret less and greater
- explains that steps on the line represent equal intervals
- matches spoken, symbolic, and visual number-line positions

## Minimum Verification Rule

A learner does not clear `L1` until direct accuracy, explanation, transfer, and delayed retention evidence all meet threshold.
