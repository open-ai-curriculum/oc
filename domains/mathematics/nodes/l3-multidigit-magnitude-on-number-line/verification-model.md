# L3 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

- `G1` direct performance gate
- `G2` meaning gate
- `G3` transfer gate
- `G4` retention gate

## Evidence Targets

- places multidigit numbers within bounded intervals on a line
- uses benchmark anchors such as tens or hundreds to estimate position
- compares multidigit magnitudes through relative location on a line
- explains why closer position reflects smaller difference

## Minimum Verification Rule

A learner does not clear `L3` until direct accuracy, explanation, transfer, and delayed retention evidence all meet threshold.
