# G2 Failure Taxonomy

### G2-FM-01 Position-Term Confusion

- description: learner confuses spatial terms such as above/below or in front/behind

### G2-FM-02 Left-Right Instability

- description: learner cannot reliably interpret or produce left/right distinctions

### G2-FM-03 Multi-Step Direction Drift

- description: learner loses one part of a directional sequence or scrambles the order

### G2-FM-04 Representation Context Dependence

- description: learner succeeds in one context such as body movement but fails in pictures or object layouts
