# M4 Verification Model

## Verification Intent

`M4` verifies whether the learner can compare and order measurable quantities while preserving attribute focus and correct alignment.

## Gate Set

### G1 Direct Performance

- target: accurate direct comparison and ordering
- threshold: `>= 0.90`

### G2 Meaning

- target: explanation of same-attribute focus and alignment
- threshold: successful explanation in at least `2 of 3` prompts

### G3 Transfer

- target: success in changed representational or attribute contexts
- threshold: at least `1` successful transfer task

### G4 Retention

- target: durable comparison reasoning after spacing
- threshold: `>= 0.85` on delayed recheck after about `14` days
