# Failure Taxonomy

- `representation_alignment_breakdown`: Cannot align fractions, decimals, or signed forms into a coherent common quantity basis before operating.
- `signed_magnitude_confusion`: Loses magnitude meaning when positive and negative rational values are combined.
- `whole_number_procedure_intrusion`: Applies whole-number operation habits directly to rational-number forms without respecting units or magnitude.
- `context_sign_transfer_breakdown`: Can compute symbolically but loses direction, change, or context meaning in verbal and number-line cases.
