# Intervention Playbook

## `representation_alignment_breakdown`

- align fraction and decimal forms through benchmark or equivalent-form conversion routines
- require common-unit or common-form annotation before computation
- use number-line overlays to compare the same quantity in multiple representations

## `signed_magnitude_confusion`

- use directed-movement number-line tasks with rational increments
- compare additive combination to distance or temperature-change contexts
- rehearse sign and magnitude separately before recombining the result

## `whole_number_procedure_intrusion`

- contrast rational-operation tasks with tempting whole-number shortcuts
- use unit-language prompts that force attention to denominator, decimal place, or signed value
- teacher error analysis highlighting why the procedure breaks quantity meaning

## `context_sign_transfer_breakdown`

- translate among number-line movement, change situations, and symbolic expressions
- use paired symbolic and contextual tasks with explicit reasonableness checks
- teacher compare-and-connect routines across symbolic, visual, and verbal rational-number situations
