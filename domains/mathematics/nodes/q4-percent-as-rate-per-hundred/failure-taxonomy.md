# Failure Taxonomy

- `symbol_without_quantity`: Treats the percent sign as a procedure marker rather than as rate per hundred.
- `equivalent_form_disconnect`: Cannot coordinate percent with equivalent fractions, decimals, or hundred models.
- `base_whole_confusion`: Computes percent without preserving what whole the percent refers to.
- `context_transfer_breakdown`: Can handle one familiar percent setting but fails when the context or representation changes.
