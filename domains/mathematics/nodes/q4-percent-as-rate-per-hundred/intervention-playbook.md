# Intervention Playbook

## `symbol_without_quantity`

- hundred-grid shading and labeling routines
- verbal rehearsal of "out of one hundred" language before symbolic notation
- benchmark percent card sorts anchored to familiar fractions and decimals

## `equivalent_form_disconnect`

- convert among percent, fraction, and decimal using matched visual models
- benchmark percent families such as 50%, 25%, 10%, and 1%
- teacher compare-and-connect routines across equivalent forms

## `base_whole_confusion`

- explicitly name the whole before computing or comparing percent
- contrast percent of different wholes with the same numerical percent
- annotate percent statements with part and whole quantities

## `context_transfer_breakdown`

- translate one percent situation across words, visuals, tables, and simple numeric forms
- vary contexts across discounts, survey results, and hundred-grid models
- use error-analysis tasks that require identifying the whole and the percent relationship
