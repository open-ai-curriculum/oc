# M5 Failure Taxonomy

### M5-FM-01 Transitive Chain Breakdown

- description: learner cannot preserve the logical comparison chain across three or more quantities

### M5-FM-02 Inference Justification Weakness

- description: learner states a comparison conclusion without explaining why it follows from the known relations

### M5-FM-03 Shared-Reference Confusion

- description: learner cannot use a common reference object to compare two other quantities indirectly

### M5-FM-04 Language-Chain Drift

- description: learner loses the comparison logic when it is expressed verbally rather than directly shown
