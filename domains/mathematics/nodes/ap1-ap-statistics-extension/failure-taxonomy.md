# Failure Taxonomy

- `evidence_chain_fragmentation`: Treats design, probability, and inference steps separately without integrating them into one argument.
- `chance_evidence_opacity`: Uses significance or probability language mechanically without understanding evidence strength under chance variation.
- `inferential_overreach_from_weak_design`: Accepts a strong conclusion from a weak or biased study design.
- `model_claim_misalignment`: Chooses or interprets a statistical model in a way that does not support the claim being made.
