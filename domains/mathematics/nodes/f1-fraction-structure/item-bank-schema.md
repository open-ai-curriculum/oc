# F1 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Interpretation

- identify numerator and denominator
- match symbol to diagram
- match diagram to symbol

### Family B. Explanation

- explain what the numerator counts
- explain what the denominator names

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify why a whole-number reading of a fraction is incorrect

### Family D. Transfer

- interpret fractions in sharing, partitioning, or alternate visual contexts
