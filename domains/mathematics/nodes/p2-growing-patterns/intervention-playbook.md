# P2 Intervention Playbook

### P2-FM-01 Growth-Rule Misidentification

- compare consecutive steps side by side and count the added amount
- mark the changed part explicitly before extending

### P2-FM-02 Extension Drift

- extend one step at a time with a check after each step
- rehearse "how much more?" before "what comes next?"

### P2-FM-03 Repeating-vs-Growing Confusion

- contrast matched repeating and growing patterns
- ask whether the whole pattern repeats or whether each step gets bigger

### P2-FM-04 Change-Language Weakness

- use sentence frames such as "each time it adds __"
- require explanation of the change before extension
