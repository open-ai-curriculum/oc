# Failure Taxonomy

- `association_fit_fragmentation`: Reads individual points correctly but fails to integrate them into a coherent association or fit judgment.
- `causation_overreach`: Treats observed association as proof of causation or mechanism without supporting design evidence.
- `outlier_trend_erasure`: Ignores how outliers or clusters affect apparent direction, strength, or fit.
- `prediction_context_disconnect`: Makes or accepts predictions beyond what the trend or data context reasonably supports.
