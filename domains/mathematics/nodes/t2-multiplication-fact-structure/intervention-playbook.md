# T2 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `fact family structure sort`: Sort related facts by shared array or equal-group structure.
- `derive-from-known routines`: Prompt learners to build unknown facts from doubles, fives, tens, or near neighbors.
- `array rotation explanation`: Use rotated arrays to ground commutative reasoning in visible structure.
