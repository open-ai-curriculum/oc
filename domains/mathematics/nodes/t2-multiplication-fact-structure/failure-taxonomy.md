# T2 Failure Taxonomy

## Classified Failure Modes

- `fact_recall_without_meaning`: Recalls or attempts facts without understanding the represented structure.
- `factor_role_confusion`: Confuses number of groups with group size or swaps them without structural explanation.
- `derived_fact_breakdown`: Cannot use a known fact to infer a nearby related fact.
- `commutativity_as_rule_only`: States commutativity as a rule but cannot justify it with arrays or groups.
