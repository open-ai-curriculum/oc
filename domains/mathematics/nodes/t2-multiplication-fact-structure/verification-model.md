# T2 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

- `G1` direct performance gate
- `G2` meaning gate
- `G3` transfer gate
- `G4` retention gate

## Evidence Targets

- interprets a multiplication expression as groups of equal size
- uses known structures to derive related multiplication facts
- explains commutative pairings through array rotation or grouping logic
- connects multiplication facts to repeated addition where appropriate

## Minimum Verification Rule

A learner does not clear `T2` until direct accuracy, explanation, transfer, and delayed retention evidence all meet threshold.
