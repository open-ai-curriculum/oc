# R1 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Recognition

- identify equal versus unequal partitions
- identify the whole associated with a part
- match parts to the correct whole

### Family B. Explanation

- explain why equal parts are required
- explain why the same-looking piece can mean different amounts in different wholes

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify why an unequal partition or wrong-whole interpretation is invalid

### Family D. Transfer

- reason about equal parts across area models, set models, and classroom sharing contexts
