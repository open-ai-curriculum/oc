# M14 Failure Taxonomy

## Classified Failure Modes

- `unit_size_direction_error`: Reverses the relationship between larger units and smaller units during conversion.
- `formula_without_quantity_meaning`: Applies a remembered conversion step without understanding the quantity relationship.
- `equivalent_measure_disconnect`: Cannot recognize two differently labeled measurements as the same quantity.
- `reasonableness_check_absent`: Accepts conversion outputs that contradict obvious magnitude expectations.
