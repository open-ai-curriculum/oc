# C1 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `C1` status
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner preserves count-word order and can continue from bounded entry points
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who skips or substitutes count words
- who can count only from one
- who recites sequence patterns without understanding order stability
