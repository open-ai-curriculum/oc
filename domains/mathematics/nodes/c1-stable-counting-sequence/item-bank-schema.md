# C1 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Sequence Production

- recite the count sequence in order
- identify the next number
- continue from a bounded point

### Family B. Explanation

- explain why the count words must stay in the same order
- explain what is wrong with an out-of-order sequence

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify skipped or misplaced count words

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across oral prompts, picture-number supports, and start-stop counting contexts
