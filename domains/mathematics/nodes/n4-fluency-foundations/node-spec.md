# N4 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `N4`
- name: `fluency_foundations`
- domain: `mathematics`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `upstream-readiness-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner solves small addition and subtraction facts with stable, efficient strategies and increasing automaticity while preserving quantity meaning.

## Dependencies

- `N2`
- `N3`

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `N4` only when all of the following are true:

1. solves small addition and subtraction combinations accurately with efficient strategies
2. does not depend on rebuilding each problem from zero
3. explains at least one efficient strategy family such as make-a-number, count on, or use a known fact
4. succeeds in timed-light and untimed transfer contexts without major accuracy collapse
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.92`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `21`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- solve small facts within an expected range without repeated recounting
- choose an efficient strategy appropriate to the numbers
- explain how a known combination helps solve a related combination
- maintain accuracy when problems are mixed rather than blocked by type

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
