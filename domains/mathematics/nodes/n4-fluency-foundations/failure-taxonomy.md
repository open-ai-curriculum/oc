# N4 Failure Taxonomy

### N4-FM-01 Fact Instability

- description: learner remains inaccurate across mixed small addition and subtraction combinations

### N4-FM-02 Recount Dependence

- description: learner arrives at answers only through slow full recounting instead of efficient strategy use

### N4-FM-03 Mixed-Set Collapse

- description: learner performs in blocked practice but accuracy or strategy stability collapses when item types are mixed

### N4-FM-04 Strategy Inarticulation

- description: learner may answer correctly but cannot explain or intentionally choose an efficient strategy
