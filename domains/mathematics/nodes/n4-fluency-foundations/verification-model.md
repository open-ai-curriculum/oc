# N4 Verification Model

## Verification Intent

`N4` verifies whether the learner has begun to stabilize efficient small-number addition and subtraction strategies into early fluency without losing operation meaning.

## Gate Set

### G1 Direct Performance

- target: accurate mixed fact solving with efficient strategy use
- threshold: `>= 0.92`

### G2 Meaning

- target: explanation of strategy choice and quantity logic
- threshold: successful explanation in at least `2 of 3` prompts

### G3 Transfer

- target: success in mixed-format or light-efficiency contexts
- threshold: at least `1` successful transfer task

### G4 Retention

- target: durable fact stability after spacing
- threshold: `>= 0.88` on delayed recheck after about `21` days

## Verification Notes

- fluency should not be inferred from a single timed sheet
- efficient strategy evidence matters because raw correctness can hide recount dependence
- mixed-format tasks are required to distinguish memorized sequences from stable flexible fluency
