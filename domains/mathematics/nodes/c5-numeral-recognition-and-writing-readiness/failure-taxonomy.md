# Failure Taxonomy

- `symbol_name_instability`: Does not reliably associate written numerals with their spoken names.
- `orientation_reversal`: Reverses or distorts numerals so identity becomes unstable.
- `symbol_set_confusion`: Confuses numerals with letters, symbols, or visually similar marks.
- `rote_sequence_without_symbol_access`: Can recite count words but cannot access the matching numeral when needed.
