# T4 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

- `G1` direct performance gate
- `G2` meaning gate
- `G3` transfer gate
- `G4` retention gate

## Evidence Targets

- interprets statements such as twice as many or three times as long
- distinguishes multiplicative comparison from additive difference
- solves scale-up and scale-down situations using known factor structure
- explains why repeated equal-size change represents multiplicative growth

## Minimum Verification Rule

A learner does not clear `T4` until direct accuracy, explanation, transfer, and delayed retention evidence all meet threshold.
