# T4 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `compare additive versus multiplicative sets`: Contrast side-by-side examples that look similar but require different structures.
- `scale strip diagrams`: Use strip or bar models to show repeated referent units inside the compared quantity.
- `forward-backward scale routines`: Pair scale-up and scale-down tasks to stabilize inverse reasoning.
