# T4 Failure Taxonomy

## Classified Failure Modes

- `additive_substitution`: Uses additive comparison where multiplicative reasoning is required.
- `scale_factor_loss`: Cannot preserve the same scale factor across a representation or context change.
- `comparison_reference_confusion`: Loses track of which quantity is the referent and which is the scaled quantity.
- `inverse_scale_breakdown`: Can scale up but not reason backward through scale down.
