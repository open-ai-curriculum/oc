# Teacher Observability

## Look-fors

- Treats terms, partial sums, and infinite series as the same object.
- Applies convergence language or tests mechanically without explaining why the series structure supports the conclusion.
- Uses Taylor or local approximation procedures without saying what behavior is being preserved or where the approximation is valid.
- Chooses advanced calculus procedures before identifying whether the task is fundamentally about convergence, approximation, or another BC-level structure.
