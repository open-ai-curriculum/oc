# Failure Taxonomy

- `operation_structure_fragmentation`: Performs polynomial operations mechanically without preserving or interpreting the resulting structure.
- `factor_zero_opacity`: Uses factoring or roots procedurally without understanding how factors connect to zeros and intercept behavior.
- `form_purpose_mismatch`: Chooses or stays in a polynomial form that obscures the goal of the task, such as finding zeros or reading behavior.
- `graph_expression_disconnect`: Can work symbolically or graphically, but cannot connect degree and coefficient structure to graph behavior.
