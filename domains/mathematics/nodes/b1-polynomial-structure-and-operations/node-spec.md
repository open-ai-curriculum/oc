# B1 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `B1`
- name: `polynomial_structure_and_operations`
- domain: `mathematics`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `upstream-readiness-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner interprets polynomials as structured expressions and functions, connects operations to algebraic and graphical meaning, and reasons about zeros, factors, and multiplicity rather than treating polynomial work as isolated symbol pushing.

## Dependencies

- `A2`
- `K1`
- `T6`


## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on this node only when all of the following are true:

1. identifies degree, leading term behavior, and structural parts of polynomials in bounded algebraic and graphical settings
2. performs polynomial addition, subtraction, multiplication, and introductory division with coherent structural reasoning
3. connects factors, zeros, and x-intercepts in bounded cases and interprets multiplicity in introductory form
4. rewrites or compares polynomial forms to reveal useful structure for solving, graphing, or interpretation
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing


## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.83`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `21`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- identify degree, leading coefficient effects, and term structure in bounded polynomial expressions and graphs
- perform polynomial operations and explain how the structure changes
- connect factors and zeros to graphical intercept behavior in bounded cases
- choose or compare polynomial forms for a stated purpose such as finding zeros or reading end behavior


## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
