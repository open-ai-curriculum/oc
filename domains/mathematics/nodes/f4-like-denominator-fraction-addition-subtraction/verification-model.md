# F4 Verification Model

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- pass rule: at least 90% accuracy across like-denominator addition and subtraction items

### G2. Meaning Gate

- pass rule: correct explanation of combining or separating same-size parts

### G3. Transfer Gate

- pass rule: one successful contextual or visual task

### G4. Retention Gate

- pass rule: at least 85% delayed recheck accuracy after about 14 days
