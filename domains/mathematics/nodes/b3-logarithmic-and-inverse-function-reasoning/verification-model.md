# Verification Model

This node is verified through four gate types.

## Gates

- `G1 direct performance`: learner meets at least 0.82 accuracy on direct tasks
- `G2 meaning`: learner explains logarithmic and inverse-function structure coherently
- `G3 transfer`: learner succeeds in at least one less-familiar logarithmic or inverse-function context
- `G4 retention`: learner passes a delayed recheck after 21 days

## Item Families

- direct logarithmic and inverse-function tasks
- explanation and justification
- error diagnosis
- transfer in a varied logarithmic or inverse context
