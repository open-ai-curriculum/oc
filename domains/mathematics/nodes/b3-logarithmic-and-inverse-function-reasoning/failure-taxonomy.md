# Failure Taxonomy

- `inverse_log_equivalence_fragmentation`: Manipulates exponential and logarithmic forms without preserving their inverse relationship.
- `inverse_structure_opacity`: Applies logarithmic rules mechanically without understanding inverse-function meaning, domain, or range effects.
- `log_rule_without_domain_check`: Uses a logarithmic transformation or rule without checking whether the expression is defined.
- `context_inverse_disconnect`: Can solve symbolically but loses the meaning of logarithmic or inverse relationships in graphical or contextual settings.
