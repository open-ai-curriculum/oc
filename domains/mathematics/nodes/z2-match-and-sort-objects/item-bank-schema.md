# Z2 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Matching And Sorting

- match same objects or sets
- sort by one shared attribute
- identify the odd item out

### Family B. Explanation

- explain what makes a match or category
- explain the shared attribute in a sorted group

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify why an item does not belong in a group

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across concrete materials, pictures, and mixed-category contexts
