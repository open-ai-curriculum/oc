# M9 Failure Taxonomy

## Classified Failure Modes

- `perimeter_area_blend`: Treats perimeter and area as the same quantity or uses one procedure for both.
- `boundary_unit_loss`: Counts side labels or vertices instead of iterated boundary units.
- `square_cover_gap_overlap`: Miscounts area because unit squares leave gaps, overlap, or are inconsistently treated.
- `same_number_false_equivalence`: Assumes equal numerical results imply equal measurement type or equal shape structure.
