# M9 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

- `G1` direct performance gate
- `G2` meaning gate
- `G3` transfer gate
- `G4` retention gate

## Evidence Targets

- identifies whether a task asks for boundary or covered region
- finds perimeter by iterating boundary units
- finds area by counting or structuring unit-square coverage
- explains why identical shapes can share one measure while differing on the other

## Minimum Verification Rule

A learner does not clear `M9` until direct accuracy, explanation, transfer, and delayed retention evidence all meet threshold.
