# M9 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `boundary-versus-cover sort`: Sort tasks and diagrams by whether they quantify edge length or covered region.
- `trace-and-tile routines`: Separate tracing the outside from filling the inside before assigning measures.
- `structured-unit-square arrays`: Use rectangular arrays of unit squares to stabilize area structure.
