# M12 Failure Taxonomy

## Classified Failure Modes

- `attribute_type_confusion`: Confuses mass or volume with length, size, or count.
- `visual_size_overgeneralization`: Assumes larger-looking objects always have more mass or volume.
- `container_shape_bias`: Judges volume by container shape without reasoning about contained amount.
- `comparison_language_instability`: Uses heavier/lighter or more/less-full language inconsistently.
