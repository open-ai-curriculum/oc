# M12 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `M12`
- name: `mass_and_volume_attributes`
- domain: `mathematics`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `upstream-readiness-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner recognizes mass and volume as measurable attributes distinct from length and reasons about them in bounded comparison contexts.

## Dependencies

- `M1`

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `M12` only when all of the following are true:

1. recognizes mass and volume as legitimate measurement attributes
2. compares mass and volume in bounded direct and indirect situations
3. distinguishes attribute type from number of items or visible size alone
4. succeeds across objects, containers, pictures, and verbal comparison tasks
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `14`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- identifies mass and volume as measurable attributes of objects or containers
- compares heavier/lighter and more/less-full situations meaningfully
- distinguishes these attributes from length or count
- explains comparison judgments in familiar physical contexts

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
