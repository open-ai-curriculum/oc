# M12 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

- `G1` direct performance gate
- `G2` meaning gate
- `G3` transfer gate
- `G4` retention gate

## Evidence Targets

- identifies mass and volume as measurable attributes of objects or containers
- compares heavier/lighter and more/less-full situations meaningfully
- distinguishes these attributes from length or count
- explains comparison judgments in familiar physical contexts

## Minimum Verification Rule

A learner does not clear `M12` until direct accuracy, explanation, transfer, and delayed retention evidence all meet threshold.
