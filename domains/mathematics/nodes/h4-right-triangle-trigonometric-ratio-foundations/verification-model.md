# Verification Model

This node is verified through four gate types.

## Gates

- `G1 direct performance`: learner meets at least 0.83 accuracy on direct tasks
- `G2 meaning`: learner explains trigonometric-ratio meaning coherently
- `G3 transfer`: learner succeeds in at least one less-familiar right-triangle context
- `G4 retention`: learner passes a delayed recheck after 21 days

## Item Families

- direct trigonometric-ratio tasks
- explanation and justification
- error diagnosis
- transfer in a varied right-triangle context
