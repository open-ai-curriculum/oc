# U4 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Partitioning

- identify equal and unequal partitions
- create equal partitions of a whole
- critique candidate partitions

### Family B. Explanation

- explain why equal parts are required
- explain why same count of pieces does not guarantee equal partitioning

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify why an unequal partition is invalid

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across area models, set sharing, and classroom fair-share contexts
