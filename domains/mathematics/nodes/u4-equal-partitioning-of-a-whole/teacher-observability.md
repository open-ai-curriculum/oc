# U4 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `U4` status
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner can produce equal partitions or only recognize them
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who confuses requested piece count with equal share structure
- who can discuss fairness but not connect it to equal measure
- who still produces unequal partitions
