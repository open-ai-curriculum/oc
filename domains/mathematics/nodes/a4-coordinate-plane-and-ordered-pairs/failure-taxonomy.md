# Failure Taxonomy

- `axis_order_reversal`: Reverses x- and y-coordinate order or confuses horizontal and vertical movement.
- `origin_reference_instability`: Does not use the origin and axes consistently as a location reference system.
- `quadrant_sign_confusion`: Misinterprets the effect of positive and negative values on plane location.
- `representation_mapping_breakdown`: Can plot in one format but loses location meaning when coordinates are given in tables, words, or simple maps.
