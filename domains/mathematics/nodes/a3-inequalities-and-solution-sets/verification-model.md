# Verification Model

This node is verified through four gate types.

## Gates

- `G1 direct performance`: learner meets at least 0.88 accuracy on direct tasks
- `G2 meaning`: learner explains inequality, comparison, and solution-set structure coherently
- `G3 transfer`: learner succeeds in at least one less-familiar inequality context
- `G4 retention`: learner passes a delayed recheck after 21 days

## Item Families

- direct inequality-solving or solution-set representation tasks
- explanation and justification
- error diagnosis
- transfer in a varied inequality context
