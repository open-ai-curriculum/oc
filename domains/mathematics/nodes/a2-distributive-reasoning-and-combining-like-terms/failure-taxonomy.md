# Failure Taxonomy

- `distributive_structure_breakdown`: Applies distribution inconsistently or breaks expression equivalence during rewriting.
- `like_term_identity_confusion`: Combines unlike terms or fails to recognize which terms share the same unit structure.
- `factor_structure_blindness`: Cannot detect a common factor or recognize compact distributed structure.
- `structure_model_disconnect`: Can manipulate symbols but cannot connect them to area or grouped-quantity structure.
