# Intervention Playbook

## `distributive_structure_breakdown`

- area-model expansion and compression routines
- annotate each factor and distributed product before rewriting
- equivalence checks with substitution after each rewrite

## `like_term_identity_confusion`

- sort term cards into like and unlike families before combining
- use unit-language rehearsal such as "x plus x makes 2x, but x plus x-squared does not"
- coefficient-and-variable decomposition routines

## `factor_structure_blindness`

- identify common factors in grouped-quantity or array contexts first
- reverse-distribution tasks from expanded to factored form
- compare multiple equivalent forms and highlight shared structure

## `structure_model_disconnect`

- match symbolic expressions to area or rectangle models
- rebuild symbolic rewrites from partitioned diagrams
- teacher compare-and-connect routines across symbolic and model-based forms
