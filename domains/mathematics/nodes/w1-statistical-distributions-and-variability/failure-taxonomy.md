# Failure Taxonomy

- `summary_selection_fragmentation`: Chooses mean, median, or spread measures mechanically without regard to distribution shape or outliers.
- `distribution_feature_opacity`: Names a statistic correctly but cannot explain how center, spread, clusters, gaps, or outliers shape the full distribution.
- `outlier_summary_blindness`: Fails to notice how extreme values affect different summaries and comparisons.
- `graph_claim_disconnect`: Reads a graph or summary but makes claims not actually supported by the distribution shown.
