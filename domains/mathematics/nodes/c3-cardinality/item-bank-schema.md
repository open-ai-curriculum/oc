# C3 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Counting

- count a set and state how many
- identify the total after counting
- maintain total when the set is rearranged

### Family B. Explanation

- explain what the final count word means
- explain why the set still has the same total after rearrangement

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify why recounting or changing the total after rearrangement is incorrect

### Family D. Transfer

- reason about counted totals in picture and short story contexts
