# U6 Failure Taxonomy

### U6-FM-01 Distribution Drift

- description: learner loses track of the quantity while distributing shares

### U6-FM-02 Fairness Criterion Weakness

- description: learner does not treat equal share as the non-negotiable rule of fair sharing

### U6-FM-03 Unequal-Share Tolerance

- description: learner accepts visibly unequal shares as valid or fails to repair them

### U6-FM-04 Share-Amount Inference Weakness

- description: learner can distribute objects but cannot infer or explain how many belong in each equal share
