# U6 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `U6`
- name: `equal_share_division_readiness`
- domain: `mathematics`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `upstream-readiness-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner interprets fair-sharing situations as equal shares distributed across a fixed number of recipients or groups.

## Dependencies

- `U3`

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `U6` only when all of the following are true:

1. distributes a quantity into equal shares across recipients or groups
2. identifies when a share is not equal and corrects the distribution
3. explains why fair sharing requires equal shares
4. succeeds across objects, drawings, stories, and simple table-like representations
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `14`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- share 12 items equally among 3 recipients
- determine the amount each group gets in a fair-sharing situation
- reject a distribution where one recipient gets more than another
- connect equal-share situations to missing-share or how-many-in-each reasoning

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
