# U6 Intervention Playbook

### U6-FM-01 Distribution Drift

- distribute one item at a time in a round-robin pattern
- use physical placeholders for each recipient to stabilize tracking

### U6-FM-02 Fairness Criterion Weakness

- contrast fair and unfair sharing examples and require justification
- ask the learner to check whether everyone received the same amount after each distribution

### U6-FM-03 Unequal-Share Tolerance

- present near-fair examples and require correction
- use peer-check routines where the learner verifies every share before closure

### U6-FM-04 Share-Amount Inference Weakness

- move from completed equal-share pictures to "how many in each?" prompts
- use sentence frames such as "__ shared among __ means each gets __"
