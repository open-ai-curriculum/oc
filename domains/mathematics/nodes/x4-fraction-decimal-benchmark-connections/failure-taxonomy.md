# X4 Failure Taxonomy

## Classified Failure Modes

- `representation_split`: Treats fraction and decimal forms as unrelated systems.
- `benchmark_mismatch`: Pairs familiar benchmark fractions and decimals incorrectly.
- `exact_vs_approx_blur`: Confuses exact equivalence with near-benchmark approximation.
- `cross_representation_ordering_drift`: Can compare within one representation but not across fraction and decimal forms.
