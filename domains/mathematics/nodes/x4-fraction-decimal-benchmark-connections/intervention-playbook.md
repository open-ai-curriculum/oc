# X4 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `benchmark match sets`: Match common fraction and decimal benchmarks through shared visual anchors.
- `same-magnitude number-line placement`: Place equivalent fraction and decimal labels at the same point on a line.
- `exact-versus-near sorts`: Sort representation pairs into exact matches and near-benchmark neighbors.
