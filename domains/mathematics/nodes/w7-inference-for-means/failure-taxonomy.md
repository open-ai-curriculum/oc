# Failure Taxonomy

- `inference_design_fragmentation`: Makes an inference claim without aligning it to the sampling and design conditions that justify it.
- `representativeness_opacity`: Talks about a sample as if it automatically represents a population without considering bias or sampling method.
- `causal_claim_leap`: Treats non-experimental or weakly designed evidence as sufficient for causal conclusions.
- `bias_source_blindness`: Misses obvious sources of sampling bias, confounding, or measurement weakness in a bounded study.
