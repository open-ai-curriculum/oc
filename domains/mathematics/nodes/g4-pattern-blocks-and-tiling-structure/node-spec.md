# G4 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `G4`
- name: `pattern_blocks_and_tiling_structure`
- domain: `mathematics`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `upstream-readiness-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner uses shapes to tile or cover a region and notices fit, repetition, and gap-free structure.

## Dependencies

- `G3`

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `G4` only when all of the following are true:

1. covers a simple region with shapes without gaps or overlaps
2. notices and explains when shapes fit together in a repeating or structured way
3. distinguishes valid tilings from invalid placements
4. succeeds across physical blocks, drawings, and simple outline tasks
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `14`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- fill a simple outline with repeated or combined shapes
- identify gaps and overlaps in an attempted tiling
- explain why certain shapes fit together to cover a region
- connect tiling structure to repeated composition and spatial organization

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
