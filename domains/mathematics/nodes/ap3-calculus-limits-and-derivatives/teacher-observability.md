# Teacher Observability

## Look-fors

- Treats a limit as plugging in a value instead of reasoning about nearby behavior.
- Can differentiate symbolically but cannot explain what the derivative means in the graph, motion, or context.
- Produces derivative-based claims that conflict with the graph or the described motion and does not notice the mismatch.
- Uses critical-point routines mechanically without checking endpoints, interval meaning, or contextual constraints.
