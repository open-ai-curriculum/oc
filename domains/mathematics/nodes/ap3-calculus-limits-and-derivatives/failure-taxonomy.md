# Failure Taxonomy

- `limit_process_static_view`: Treats a limit as direct substitution or a frozen endpoint rather than as approaching behavior.
- `derivative_meaning_fragmentation`: Can perform derivative procedures but cannot connect them to slope, rate, motion, or local linearization.
- `symbolic_graphical_motion_disconnect`: Gets symbolic derivative information that does not match the graph or motion interpretation and cannot detect the mismatch.
- `optimization_local_global_confusion`: Uses derivative cues mechanically without identifying whether the claim depends on local behavior, global behavior, or contextual constraints.
