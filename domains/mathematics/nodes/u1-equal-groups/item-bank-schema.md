# U1 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Equal-Group Reasoning

- identify equal and unequal groups
- make equal groups
- critique mismatched groups

### Family B. Explanation

- explain why groups are equal or unequal
- explain what must stay the same across groups

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify why a mismatched grouping is invalid

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across objects, pictures, and simple grouping stories
