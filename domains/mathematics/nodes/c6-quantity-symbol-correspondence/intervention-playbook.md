# Intervention Playbook

## `count_symbol_disconnect`

- count-then-label routines with immediate numeral placement
- teacher modeling of final-count-to-symbol linkage
- match counted sets to numeral cards after each count

## `symbol_quantity_guessing`

- mixed non-sequential numeral-choice tasks
- justify-before-place prompts
- small contrast sets where visual familiarity is misleading

## `final_count_loss`

- last-number emphasis during counting
- cover-and-recall numeral placement after counting
- count-stop-label routines

## `label_acceptance_error`

- error-detection tasks with intentionally mislabeled sets
- check-the-label prompts before approval
- peer-label correction routines
