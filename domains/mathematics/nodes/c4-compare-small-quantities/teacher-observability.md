# C4 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `C4` status
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner preserves more-less-same meaning across rearrangements and contexts
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who reverses more and less
- who mistakes spread for quantity
- who can compare direct sets but not story or picture contexts
