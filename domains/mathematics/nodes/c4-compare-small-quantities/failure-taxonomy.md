# C4 Failure Taxonomy

### C4-FM-01 Comparison Without Stable Count Basis

- description: learner cannot ground the more-less-same judgment in stable quantity evidence

### C4-FM-02 More-Less Reversal

- description: learner reverses which set has more or less

### C4-FM-03 Spread-Out-Means-More

- description: learner treats arrangement or visible spread as quantity change

### C4-FM-04 Context Boundary

- description: learner compares direct sets correctly but loses the meaning in pictured or story contexts
