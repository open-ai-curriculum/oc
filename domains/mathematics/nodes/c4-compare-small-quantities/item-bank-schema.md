# C4 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Quantity Comparison

- identify more, less, and same
- compare bounded sets
- preserve comparison across rearrangement

### Family B. Explanation

- explain how the comparison was determined
- explain why rearrangement does not change which set has more or less

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify why a mistaken more-less-same claim is incorrect

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across concrete sets, pictured sets, and simple story contexts
