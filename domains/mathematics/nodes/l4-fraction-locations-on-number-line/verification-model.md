# L4 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

- `G1` direct performance gate
- `G2` meaning gate
- `G3` transfer gate
- `G4` retention gate

## Evidence Targets

- places unit fractions and simple non-unit fractions on bounded lines
- partitions a whole interval into equal fractional intervals
- compares fractions using relative position and benchmark locations
- explains why equal partitions are required for valid fraction placement

## Minimum Verification Rule

A learner does not clear `L4` until direct accuracy, explanation, transfer, and delayed retention evidence all meet threshold.
