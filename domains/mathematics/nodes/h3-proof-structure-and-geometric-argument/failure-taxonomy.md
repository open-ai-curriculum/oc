# Failure Taxonomy

- `reason_claim_mismatch`: Supplies a reason that does not actually support the stated claim or conclusion.
- `proof_structure_opacity`: Can copy proof steps but cannot explain the role of givens, subclaims, or theorem sequence.
- `visual_assertion_substitution`: Replaces justified reasoning with diagram-based appearance claims.
- `algebra_geometry_link_breakdown`: Loses coherence when algebraic manipulation and geometric properties must be coordinated in one argument.
