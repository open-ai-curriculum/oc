# Failure Taxonomy

- `accumulation_without_rate_link`: Treats an integral as isolated area output without connecting it to the underlying rate or change process.
- `fundamental_theorem_connection_breakdown`: Can compute pieces separately but cannot explain how rate, accumulation, and antiderivative ideas connect.
- `signed_area_orientation_confusion`: Loses sign, direction, or region meaning when interpreting definite integrals.
- `antiderivative_procedure_without_context`: Executes antiderivative procedures mechanically without deciding whether the context asks for net change, total amount, or an accumulation function.
