# Teacher Observability

## Look-fors

- Treats integrals as shaded-area calculations without saying what quantity is accumulating.
- Computes antiderivatives but cannot explain why they connect to rate and accumulation.
- Loses sign and direction when interpreting regions below the axis or negative rates.
- Chooses an integral procedure before deciding whether the task is about net change, total amount, or an accumulation function.
