# N1 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Composition

- build a whole from given parts
- break a whole into parts
- identify missing part configurations

### Family B. Explanation

- explain how different part sets can make the same whole
- explain why the whole stays constant when grouping changes

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify why an incorrect part-whole relation does not preserve the whole

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across manipulatives, pictures, and short joining or separating stories
