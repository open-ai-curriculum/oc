# N1 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `N1` status
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner preserves the whole while changing parts
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who knows only one decomposition of a number
- who loses total quantity while splitting or combining
- who does not yet understand that regrouping preserves the whole
