# N1 Failure Taxonomy

### N1-FM-01 Whole-Part Instability

- description: learner does not preserve the whole when reasoning about parts

### N1-FM-02 Single-Decomposition Lock

- description: learner knows one composition of a number but cannot generate alternate decompositions

### N1-FM-03 Counting Drift

- description: learner miscounts or loses quantity while composing or decomposing

### N1-FM-04 Rearrangement Misunderstanding

- description: learner treats regrouping as changing the total rather than reorganizing the same whole
