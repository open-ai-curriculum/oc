# Y2 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

- `G1` direct performance gate
- `G2` meaning gate
- `G3` transfer gate
- `G4` retention gate

## Evidence Targets

- estimates addition and subtraction results with reasonable benchmark strategies
- uses multiplication and division approximations in bounded cases
- judges whether exact answers are plausible relative to benchmark estimates
- explains the estimation method used and its limits

## Minimum Verification Rule

A learner does not clear `Y2` until direct accuracy, explanation, transfer, and delayed retention evidence all meet threshold.
