# Y2 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `estimate-then-exact routines`: Require an estimate before exact computation and a comparison after.
- `operation-specific benchmark rehearsal`: Contrast benchmark estimation for addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.
- `implausible-answer critiques`: Diagnose exact answers that contradict obvious benchmark expectations.
