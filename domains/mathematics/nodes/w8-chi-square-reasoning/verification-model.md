# Verification Model

This node is verified through four gate types.

## Gates

- `G1 direct performance`: learner meets at least 0.84 accuracy on direct tasks
- `G2 meaning`: learner explains probability-model and event-structure meaning coherently
- `G3 transfer`: learner succeeds in at least one less-familiar compound-event context
- `G4 retention`: learner passes a delayed recheck after 21 days

## Item Families

- direct probability-model and compound-event tasks
- explanation and justification
- error diagnosis
- transfer in a varied probability context
