# R2 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Interpretation

- identify one equal part of a whole
- match unit-part language to visuals
- compare one equal part across different partition counts

### Family B. Explanation

- explain why one out of four is not the same size as one out of two in the same whole
- explain what determines the size of one part

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify why a count-only or whole-number reading of one part is incorrect

### Family D. Transfer

- reason about one equal part in sharing, set, and measurement-adjacent contexts
