# R2 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `R2`
- name: `unit_fraction_readiness`
- domain: `mathematics`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `upstream-readiness-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner interprets one equal part of a whole as a meaningful unit and can connect "one out of n equal parts" reasoning across visuals, sets, and concrete sharing situations.

## Dependencies

- `R1`

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `R2` only when all of the following are true:

1. identifies one equal part of a partitioned whole reliably
2. explains that the named part depends on how many equal parts make the whole
3. distinguishes unit-fraction reasoning from counting pieces without regard to unit size
4. succeeds in at least one changed visual or contextual form
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `14`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- identify one part when a whole is partitioned into `2`, `3`, `4`, or more equal parts
- explain that one out of four equal parts is smaller than one out of two equal parts of the same whole
- connect unit-fraction language to fair-sharing situations
- preserve the whole reference when the same count of parts appears in different wholes

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
