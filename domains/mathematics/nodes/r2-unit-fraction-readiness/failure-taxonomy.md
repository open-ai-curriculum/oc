# R2 Failure Taxonomy

### R2-FM-01 Part Selection Drift

- description: learner cannot reliably identify one equal part of the whole

### R2-FM-02 Whole Count Substitution

- description: learner counts pieces but does not connect one part to the whole relationship

### R2-FM-03 Denominator Magnitude Reversal

- description: learner assumes one out of more equal parts must be larger because the whole-number count is larger

### R2-FM-04 Fair Share Without Unit Meaning

- description: learner can identify a fair share informally but cannot explain it as one equal part of a whole
