# N5 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `N5`
- name: `teen_numbers_and_base_ten_composition_readiness`
- domain: `mathematics`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `upstream-readiness-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner understands that teen numbers are composed of one ten and additional ones and can represent that structure across objects, drawings, spoken forms, and symbols.

## Dependencies

- `N1`
- `C6`

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `N5` only when all of the following are true:

1. composes teen numbers as `10 and some ones`
2. decomposes teen numbers into one ten and leftover ones
3. explains that the ten remains a single bundled unit while the ones remain separate
4. succeeds across concrete, pictorial, spoken, and symbolic forms
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `14`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- show `14` as one group of ten and four ones
- explain why `17` is not just `1 and 7`
- move between teen number words, numerals, and grouped representations
- compare two teen numbers by reasoning about the ten and the extra ones

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
