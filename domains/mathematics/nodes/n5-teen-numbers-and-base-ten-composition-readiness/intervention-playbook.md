# N5 Intervention Playbook

### N5-FM-01 Unbundled Teen Interpretation

- use fixed ten-frames or bundled-stick models to show one full ten plus extras
- contrast teen numbers with single-digit numbers explicitly

### N5-FM-02 Number Word-Symbol Drift

- cycle among spoken number, numeral, and concrete model in one routine
- rehearse teen number dictation with immediate build-and-label steps

### N5-FM-03 Ten-Unit Instability

- require the learner to keep the ten intact while adding or removing ones
- use sentence frames such as "one ten and __ ones"

### N5-FM-04 Comparison Weakness

- compare two teen numbers using common ten plus different ones
- use side-by-side grouped models before symbolic comparison
