# J5 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `J5`
- name: `percent_change_discount_tax_and_markup`
- domain: `mathematics`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `upstream-readiness-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner applies percent to change situations such as discount, tax, markup, and percent increase or decrease using rate-per-hundred and multiplicative structure rather than one-off shopping tricks.

## Dependencies

- `Q4`
- `J2`
- `M14`


## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on this node only when all of the following are true:

1. solves bounded percent change, discount, tax, and markup situations accurately
2. distinguishes percent of a quantity from additive difference and from unrelated whole-number operations
3. explains how original value, percent rate, and resulting value are multiplicatively related
4. succeeds across symbolic, table, verbal, and simple contextual representations
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing


## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.88`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `21`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- compute a percent amount and combine it correctly with an original quantity when the context requires increase or decrease
- distinguish original amount, percent amount, and resulting amount in bounded change problems
- explain why discount and tax are structurally similar but directionally different applications of percent
- judge whether a resulting price, quantity, or measurement is reasonable relative to the original amount


## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
