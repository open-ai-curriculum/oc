# Failure Taxonomy

- `mark_position_error`: Places data marks at the wrong numerical or fractional location on the line plot.
- `frequency_tracking_loss`: Loses count of repeated values or misreads stacked marks.
- `measurement_scale_disconnect`: Cannot connect measurement units or fractional tick marks to the plotted values.
- `distribution_question_confusion`: Reads individual points but fails to answer summary or comparison questions from the full data set.
