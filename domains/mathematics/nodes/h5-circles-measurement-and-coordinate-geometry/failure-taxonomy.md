# Failure Taxonomy

- `formula_structure_fragmentation`: Treats circle and coordinate formulas as unrelated snippets and selects them without structural fit to the problem.
- `arc_angle_meaning_gap`: Computes with arc or angle quantities mechanically without understanding how the measures are related.
- `coordinate_measure_substitution_error`: Substitutes coordinates inconsistently or loses geometric meaning in midpoint, distance, or circle-form work.
- `measurement_context_disconnect`: Produces a numerical answer without checking whether the measure is plausible for the diagram or context.
