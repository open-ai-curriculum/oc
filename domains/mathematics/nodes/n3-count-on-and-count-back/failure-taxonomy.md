# N3 Failure Taxonomy

### N3-FM-01 Restart-From-One Dependence

- description: learner recounts from `1` instead of starting from the known quantity

### N3-FM-02 Directional Reversal

- description: learner loses direction when counting back or counts the wrong number of steps

### N3-FM-03 Jump Tracking Drift

- description: learner starts correctly but skips, repeats, or loses a count during the sequence of jumps

### N3-FM-04 Strategy Meaning Weakness

- description: learner can imitate the procedure but cannot explain why the strategy is efficient or appropriate
