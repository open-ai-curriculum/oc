# N3 Verification Model

## Verification Intent

`N3` verifies whether the learner can use the counting sequence strategically from a known quantity rather than reconstructing quantities from zero each time.

## Gate Set

### G1 Direct Performance

- target: accurate count-on and count-back execution
- threshold: `>= 0.90`

### G2 Meaning

- target: explanation of why counting begins at the known amount
- threshold: successful explanation in at least `2 of 3` prompts

### G3 Transfer

- target: success in changed representation such as spoken jumps or a simple number line
- threshold: at least `1` successful transfer task

### G4 Retention

- target: durable strategic counting after spacing
- threshold: `>= 0.85` on delayed recheck after about `14` days

## Verification Notes

- counting all from `1` should not pass as secure strategy use
- subtraction items must check whether backward movement is preserved accurately
- strategy explanations should capture efficiency, not only correctness
