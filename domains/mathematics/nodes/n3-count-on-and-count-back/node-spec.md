# N3 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `N3`
- name: `count_on_and_count_back`
- domain: `mathematics`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `upstream-readiness-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner starts from a known quantity and counts on or counts back accurately to solve small addition and subtraction situations.

## Dependencies

- `N2`

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `N3` only when all of the following are true:

1. starts from the known quantity instead of restarting from `1`
2. counts on or back accurately by a small number of steps
3. explains why the counting move matches the operation meaning
4. succeeds across objects, spoken counts, and simple number-line or jump representations
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `14`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- solve `6 + 3` by starting at `6` and counting on `3`
- solve `9 - 2` by starting at `9` and counting back `2`
- represent count-on or count-back moves with verbal jumps, fingers, or simple marks
- distinguish efficient counting from recounting all objects from the beginning

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
