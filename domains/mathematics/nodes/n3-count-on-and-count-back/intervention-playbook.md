# N3 Intervention Playbook

### N3-FM-01 Restart-From-One Dependence

- rehearse "keep the first number in your head" routines with a visible starting marker
- compare full recounting against count-on on matched problems

### N3-FM-02 Directional Reversal

- use floor or tabletop number paths with explicit forward and backward movement
- pair subtraction language with physical backward jumps

### N3-FM-03 Jump Tracking Drift

- reduce step size and add touch or mark support for each jump
- require one count word per move to stabilize tracking

### N3-FM-04 Strategy Meaning Weakness

- ask the learner to explain why starting from the larger or known quantity saves work
- model the same problem with two strategies and compare them explicitly
