# M13 Failure Taxonomy

## Classified Failure Modes

- `unit_label_drop`: Compares or records measurement numbers without preserving the unit.
- `cross_unit_false_equivalence`: Treats equal numerals in different units as automatically equivalent quantities.
- `object_measure_mismatch`: Accepts implausible unit-object pairings.
- `mass_volume_unit_blur`: Confuses which units belong to mass versus volume contexts.
