# M13 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `number-plus-unit matching`: Require every measured value to stay paired with its unit.
- `plausible-unit sorts`: Sort object and container measures by plausible unit choice.
- `same-number-different-unit contrasts`: Compare equal numerals with different units to surface meaning differences.
