# Q2 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `Q2`
- name: `unit_rate_reasoning`
- domain: `mathematics`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `upstream-readiness-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner interprets and computes unit rates as the amount for one unit and uses them to compare, scale, and explain quantities coherently.

## Dependencies

- `Q1`
- `M14`


## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on this node only when all of the following are true:

1. interprets a unit rate as the amount for one unit of measure, item, or time
2. computes bounded unit rates from simple ratio or measurement contexts
3. uses unit rates to compare situations with different totals or scales
4. succeeds across tables, words, diagrams, and measurement contexts without collapsing unit rate into raw totals
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing


## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.88`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `21`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- explain why miles per hour or cost per item describes an amount for one unit
- find unit rate in bounded ratio and measurement tasks
- compare two situations by coordinating the same unit basis
- translate between a composite ratio and its associated one-unit rate in bounded cases


## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
