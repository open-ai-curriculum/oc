# Intervention Playbook

## `total_quantity_fixation`

- compare same-context situations where larger totals hide lower unit rates
- teacher modeling of normalize-to-one routines
- rate tables that highlight one-unit rows

## `division_structure_breakdown`

- explicit what-is-being-measured versus what-is-one-unit prompts
- bounded ratio-to-unit-rate rehearsal
- table-based partitioning before symbolic division

## `unit_label_loss`

- always-name-the-unit response frames
- unit-tag matching exercises
- non-example review of numerically correct but semantically unlabeled rates

## `cross_context_transfer_failure`

- parallel tasks across money, speed, and measurement contexts
- representation translation sets
- teacher compare-and-generalize routines
