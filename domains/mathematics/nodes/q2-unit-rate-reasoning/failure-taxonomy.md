# Failure Taxonomy

- `total_quantity_fixation`: Uses total quantities directly instead of normalizing to one unit.
- `division_structure_breakdown`: Cannot determine which quantity should be divided by which to form the unit rate.
- `unit_label_loss`: Computes a number but drops or confuses the meaning of the unit in the result.
- `cross_context_transfer_failure`: Can compute in one familiar context but cannot apply the same structure in another context.
