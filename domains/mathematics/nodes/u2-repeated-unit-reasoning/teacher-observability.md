# U2 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `U2` status
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner preserves the repeated unit across contexts
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who accepts unequal groups as if they matched
- who sees collections but not repeatable units
- who can reason with objects but not tiled or strip contexts
