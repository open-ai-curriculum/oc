# U2 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Repeated-Unit Reasoning

- identify repeated equal groups
- count or describe repeated same-size units
- critique nonmatching groups

### Family B. Explanation

- explain what makes the repeated unit the same each time
- explain why changing one group breaks the pattern

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify why a non-equal group does not fit the repeated-unit structure

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across object groups, strips, and simple measurement-adjacent contexts
