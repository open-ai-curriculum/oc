# C2 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Counting Control

- count objects one by one
- identify skipped or double-counted sets
- count across different layouts

### Family B. Explanation

- explain why each object needs one count word
- explain how to keep track while counting

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify where skipping or double-counting happened

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across concrete sets, pictures, and moved objects
