# M11 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `same-value sort`: Sort different coin combinations that represent the same total.
- `exchange match tasks`: Match purchase or trade scenarios to equivalent replacement sets.
- `value-versus-piece-count contrast`: Compare sets with different counts of pieces but reversed total value.
