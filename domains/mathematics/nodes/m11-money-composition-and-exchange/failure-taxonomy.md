# M11 Failure Taxonomy

## Classified Failure Modes

- `coin_count_over_value`: Treats more coins or pieces as automatically greater value.
- `exchange_equivalence_gap`: Cannot determine whether an exchange preserves total value.
- `tens_ones_value_drift`: Loses composed-value structure when amounts move beyond the smallest units.
- `enough_not_enough_miscalibration`: Misjudges whether an amount covers a given bounded price.
