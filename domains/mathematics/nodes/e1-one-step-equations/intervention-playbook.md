# E1 Intervention Playbook

## Intervention Principles

- intervention must match the dominant failure mode
- corrective work must preserve the structural meaning of the node
- progression resumes only after local re-verification

## Bounded Interventions

- `balance-model rehearsal`: Use concrete or visual balance representations to reinforce same-operation-both-sides logic.
- `inverse-pair matching`: Match equations to the inverse operations needed to isolate the unknown.
- `substitution-check routines`: Require every solved equation to be checked by substitution before closure.
