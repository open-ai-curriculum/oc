# E1 Failure Taxonomy

## Classified Failure Modes

- `equality_preservation_failure`: Changes only one side or applies mismatched operations across the equation.
- `inverse_logic_gap`: Cannot identify or justify the inverse operation needed to isolate the variable.
- `no_solution_check`: Produces an answer but does not verify whether it satisfies the equation.
- `variable_as_label_only`: Treats the variable as a decorative symbol rather than as an unknown quantity.
