# Downstream Expansion Plan

## Purpose

Document the downstream work needed after the current ELA/literacy graph and node contracts are in place.

## Downstream Targets

- curriculum artifact authoring
- verification surface authoring
- intervention asset authoring
- teacher decision supports
- accessibility and multilingual support layers

## Dependency Rule

Downstream artifact work should attach to the graph rather than bypass it with free-floating content libraries.

## Immediate Downstream Priority

The first downstream priority is to expand artifact-attachment registries from the initial early-literacy slice into later reading, writing, and advanced-analysis slices.
