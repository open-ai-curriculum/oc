# FR5 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: confirm stable automatic recognition of known words or patterns
- pass rule: successful performance in at least 9 of 10 structured opportunities

### G2. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm automatic recognition survives mixed-list or spaced conditions
- pass rule: success across one changed-set, spaced, or mixed-list task

### G3. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 21 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: accuracy improves but recognition remains effortful or unstable
- `provisional`: direct and transfer pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all three gates pass
- `unstable`: previously secure automatic recognition regresses on later checks
