# FR5 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `FR5` status
- dependency posture for `FR4`
- direct recognition accuracy
- automaticity, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner is still decoding effortfully on familiar items
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who is accurate but still slow on known items
- who collapses in mixed or spaced lists
- who trades accuracy for speed
