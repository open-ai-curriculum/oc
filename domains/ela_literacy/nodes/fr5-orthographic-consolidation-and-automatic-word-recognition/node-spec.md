# FR5 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `FR5`
- name: `orthographic_consolidation_and_automatic_word_recognition`
- domain: `ela_literacy`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `foundational-reading-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner recognizes known words and patterns with growing automaticity and orthographic consolidation, reducing the need for full effortful decoding on every encounter.

## Dependencies

- `FR4` word-level decoding generalization

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `FR5` only when all of the following are true:

1. recognizes known words and patterns with stable accuracy
2. shows reduced effort compared with earlier controlled decoding
3. maintains control across mixed or spaced lists
4. remains stable on delayed recheck

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.9`
- explanation required: `false`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `21`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- identify familiar words and patterns with growing automaticity
- reserve effortful decoding for less consolidated items
- support the move into connected-text fluency without conflating the two demands

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
