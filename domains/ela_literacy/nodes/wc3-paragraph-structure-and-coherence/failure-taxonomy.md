# WC3 Failure Taxonomy

### WC3-FM-01 Sentence-List Without Unity

- description: learner lists sentences without a coherent paragraph center

### WC3-FM-02 Order Drift

- description: learner loses logical sequence across the paragraph

### WC3-FM-03 Main-Idea Fragility

- description: learner cannot sustain a controlling idea

### WC3-FM-04 Scaffold Dependence

- description: learner succeeds only with one heavy paragraph organizer
