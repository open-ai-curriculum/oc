# WC5 Failure Taxonomy

### WC5-FM-01 Paragraph-Island Writing

- description: learner writes separate paragraphs that do not build together

### WC5-FM-02 Development Thinness

- description: learner cannot sustain sufficient development across sections

### WC5-FM-03 Structural Drift

- description: learner loses the larger plan across the composition

### WC5-FM-04 Revision Fragility

- description: learner cannot stabilize coherence after edits or expansion
