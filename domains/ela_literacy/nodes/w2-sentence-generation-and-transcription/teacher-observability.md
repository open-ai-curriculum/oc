# W2 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `W2` status
- dependency posture for `FR4`, `L1`, and `W1`
- direct sentence-writing accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether sentence failure is conceptual or transcription-driven
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who can say a sentence but not get it onto the page
- who lose sentence control across multiple trials
- who only succeed on one familiar writing prompt
