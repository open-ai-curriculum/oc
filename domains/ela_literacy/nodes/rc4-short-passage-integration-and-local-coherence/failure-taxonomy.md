# RC4 Failure Taxonomy

### RC4-FM-01 Sentence-Island Reading

- description: learner understands individual sentences but not their relation

### RC4-FM-02 Local Coherence Drift

- description: learner loses track of how ideas connect across the passage

### RC4-FM-03 Fluency-Comprehension Confound

- description: passage integration fails because connected-text control is still unstable

### RC4-FM-04 Detail Overload

- description: learner cannot hold enough relevant details to maintain passage meaning
