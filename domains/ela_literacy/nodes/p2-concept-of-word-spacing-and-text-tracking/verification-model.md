# P2 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: track print at the word level with usable spacing awareness
- pass rule: successful supported text tracking in at least 5 of 6 opportunities

### G2. Meaning Gate

- purpose: confirm that the learner recognizes word units as meaningful print structure
- pass rule: correct concept-of-word response in at least 4 of 5 prompts

### G3. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm performance across changed texts, layouts, or shared-reading formats
- pass rule: one successful changed-format text-tracking task

### G4. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 14 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct accuracy improves but meaning or transfer still fails
- `provisional`: direct, meaning, and transfer pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all four gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or later regresses structurally
