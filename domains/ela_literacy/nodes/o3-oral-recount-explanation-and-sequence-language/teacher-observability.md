# O3 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `O3` status
- dependency posture for `O2`
- direct oral recount accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether oral language is coherent enough to support later text response and writing
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who loses event order in retell
- who gives unclear oral references
- who can recount but not explain
