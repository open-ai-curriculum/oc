# W3 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `W3` status
- dependency posture for `W2` and `RC6`
- direct paragraph-organization accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether source-based support is relevant and coherent
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who write sentence piles without paragraph structure
- who omit relevant source support
- who lose coherence when prompt or source changes
