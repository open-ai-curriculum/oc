# W4 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `W4` status
- dependency posture for `W3`, `R4`, and `R5`
- direct sustained-writing accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the response holds together across paragraphs
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who lose coherence across multiple paragraphs
- who can organize but not develop support sufficiently
- who only succeed in one genre or prompt condition
