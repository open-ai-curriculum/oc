# W4 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `W4`
- name: `multi_paragraph_explanation_narrative_and_argument`
- domain: `ela_literacy`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `multi-paragraph-writing-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner composes sustained multi-paragraph explanation, narrative, and argument well enough to support secondary writing demands and later research synthesis.

## Dependencies

- `W3` paragraph organization and source-based response
- `R4` literary structure, theme, and character analysis
- `R5` informational structure, author claim, and source use

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `W4` only when all of the following are true:

1. organizes writing across multiple paragraphs with recognizable coherence
2. sustains explanation, narrative, or argument over more than one paragraph
3. integrates relevant support, detail, or source material where required
4. succeeds across changed genres or prompt conditions
5. remains stable on delayed recheck

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.85`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `21`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- compose a coherent multi-paragraph response
- sustain development of an idea, story, or argument
- use organizational structure that remains recoverable to a reader
- support later disciplinary writing and research work

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
