# W4 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: compose coherent multi-paragraph explanation, narrative, or argument with sustained development
- pass rule: successful multi-paragraph writing in at least 5 of 6 structured opportunities

### G2. Meaning Gate

- purpose: confirm that organization, development, and support remain recoverable to a reader across the full response
- pass rule: at least 4 of 5 responses sustain a recognizable line of development across paragraphs

### G3. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm performance across changed genres or prompt conditions
- pass rule: one successful changed-genre or changed-prompt multi-paragraph writing task

### G4. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 21 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct accuracy improves but meaning or transfer still fails
- `provisional`: direct, meaning, and transfer pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all four gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or multi-paragraph coherence collapses materially
