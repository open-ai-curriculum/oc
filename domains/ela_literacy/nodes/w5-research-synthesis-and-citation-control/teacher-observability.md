# W5 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `W5` status
- dependency posture for `W4`, `R6`, and `L3`
- direct research-writing accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner is synthesizing or merely accumulating sources
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who stack source notes without synthesis
- who lose attribution or citation control
- who rely on copying or unstable paraphrase instead of synthesis
