# W5 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: synthesize multiple sources into a coherent written response with usable attribution or citation control
- pass rule: successful research writing in at least 5 of 6 structured opportunities

### G2. Meaning Gate

- purpose: confirm that the learner explains how sources are integrated and attributed rather than copied or accumulated
- pass rule: correct synthesis-and-attribution rationale in at least 4 of 5 prompts

### G3. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm performance across changed research questions or source sets
- pass rule: one successful changed-question or changed-source-set research-writing task

### G4. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 21 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct accuracy improves but meaning or transfer still fails
- `provisional`: direct, meaning, and transfer pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all four gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or reverts to disconnected source accumulation
