# W5 Failure Taxonomy

### W5-FM-01 Source-Accumulation Without Synthesis

- description: learner gathers or restates source information but does not integrate it into a coherent line of written reasoning

### W5-FM-02 Attribution-Or-Citation Drift

- description: learner uses source material but loses control of attribution, citation, or source boundary clarity

### W5-FM-03 Copying-Or-Paraphrase Instability

- description: learner shifts between copying, patchwriting, and weak paraphrase instead of controlled synthesis

### W5-FM-04 Question-And-Source Transfer Fragility

- description: learner succeeds on one familiar research question or source set but performance collapses on changed questions or sources
