# W5 Agent Behavior

## Purpose

Define how an instructional agent should behave when supporting `W5`.

## Guidance

- keep source sets bounded enough that synthesis and attribution remain inspectable
- require a genuine synthesis move before treating performance as successful
- distinguish citation-control weakness from synthesis weakness
- vary research questions and source sets only after one stable research-writing routine exists
- escalate to human review when research-writing failure cannot be separated from sustained-writing, cross-text reading, or revision weaknesses
