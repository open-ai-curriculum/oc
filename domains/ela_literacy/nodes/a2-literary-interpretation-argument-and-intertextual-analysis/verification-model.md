# A2 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: construct sustained literary interpretation and intertextual argument with relevant textual grounding
- pass rule: successful advanced literary analysis in at least 5 of 6 structured opportunities

### G2. Meaning Gate

- purpose: confirm that the learner explains how textual evidence and intertextual comparison support the interpretation
- pass rule: correct interpretation-and-comparison explanation in at least 4 of 5 prompts

### G3. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm performance across changed literary texts or interpretive tasks
- pass rule: one successful changed-text or changed-task advanced literary-analysis prompt

### G4. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 21 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct accuracy improves but meaning or transfer still fails
- `provisional`: direct, meaning, and transfer pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all four gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or reverts to summary-only literary response
