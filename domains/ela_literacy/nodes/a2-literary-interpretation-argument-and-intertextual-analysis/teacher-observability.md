# A2 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `A2` status
- dependency posture for `W4`, `R4`, and `L3`
- direct advanced literary-analysis accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner is building interpretation rather than summary
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who summarize instead of interpreting
- who cannot connect literary evidence to sustained argument
- who lose precision when comparing texts or interpretive frames
