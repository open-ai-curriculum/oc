# A2 Failure Taxonomy

### A2-FM-01 Summary-For-Interpretation Substitution

- description: learner recounts literary content or reactions instead of building a sustained interpretive argument

### A2-FM-02 Evidence-And-Interpretation Link Weakness

- description: learner offers an interpretive claim but cannot connect it clearly to textual evidence or literary reasoning

### A2-FM-03 Intertextual Comparison Blur

- description: learner brings multiple texts or interpretations together but loses distinctions, precision, or argumentative control

### A2-FM-04 Literary-Task Transfer Fragility

- description: learner succeeds on one familiar literary text or interpretive frame but performance collapses on changed literary tasks or text sets
