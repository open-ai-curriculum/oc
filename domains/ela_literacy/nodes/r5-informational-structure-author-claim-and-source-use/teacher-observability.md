# R5 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `R5` status
- dependency posture for `RC6`
- direct informational-analysis accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner is analyzing claims and sources or only summarizing
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who stay at topic summary
- who can name a claim or source but cannot explain its role
- who lose performance when text, source set, or prompt changes
