# P4 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `P4` status
- dependency posture for `P1` and `P3`
- direct letter-knowledge accuracy
- transfer and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner distinguishes and forms letters as literacy symbols
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who confuses visually similar letters
- who recognizes letters but cannot form them
- who loses performance when the format changes
