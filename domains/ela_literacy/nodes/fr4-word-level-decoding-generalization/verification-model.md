# FR4 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: confirm accurate decoding and encoding across changed simple-word sets
- pass rule: successful application in at least 5 of 6 structured opportunities

### G2. Meaning Gate

- purpose: confirm the learner is still using explicit pattern reasoning
- pass rule: correct explanation or demonstration in at least 4 of 5 prompted trials

### G3. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm generalization beyond one practiced set
- pass rule: success across at least two changed sets or task surfaces

### G4. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 14 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct gate improves but generalization remains weak
- `provisional`: direct, meaning, and transfer pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all four gates pass
- `unstable`: previously secure generalized word control regresses on later checks
