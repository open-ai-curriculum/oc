# O1 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `O1` status
- direct interaction stability
- transfer and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether shared attention holds long enough for instruction to start
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who cannot sustain shared attention long enough for brief routines
- who breaks down on reciprocal turns
- who responds but not contingently to the interaction cue
