# O1 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: sustain shared attention and basic contingent turn-taking
- pass rule: successful participation in at least 5 of 6 structured interaction opportunities

### G2. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm that the behavior survives a changed partner, routine, or interaction format
- pass rule: one successful changed-context interaction

### G3. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 14 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct interaction improves but remains inconsistent
- `provisional`: direct and transfer gates pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or regresses structurally
