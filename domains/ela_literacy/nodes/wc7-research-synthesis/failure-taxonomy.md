# WC7 Failure Taxonomy

### WC7-FM-01 Source-List Without Synthesis

- description: learner reports sources separately without integrating them

### WC7-FM-02 Line-Of-Reasoning Collapse

- description: learner cannot maintain a coherent synthesis claim

### WC7-FM-03 Source-Selection Overload

- description: learner cannot manage multiple sources effectively

### WC7-FM-04 Research-Question Transfer Fragility

- description: learner succeeds on one source set but not changed research situations
