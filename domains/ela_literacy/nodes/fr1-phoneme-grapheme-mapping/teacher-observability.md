# FR1 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `FR1` status
- dependency posture for `P3` and `P4`
- direct correspondence accuracy
- transfer and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner is mapping in both directions
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who can name but not map
- who maps in only one direction
- who loses control when a small target-set change is introduced
