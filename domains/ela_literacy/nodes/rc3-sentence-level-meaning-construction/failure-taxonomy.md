# RC3 Failure Taxonomy

### RC3-FM-01 Word-Calling Without Meaning

- description: learner reads words but does not construct sentence meaning

### RC3-FM-02 Referent Tracking Loss

- description: learner cannot keep track of who or what the sentence is about

### RC3-FM-03 Syntax Interpretation Fragility

- description: learner loses meaning when sentence structure shifts

### RC3-FM-04 Decoding-Load Confound

- description: learner meaning collapses when the sentence exceeds supported decoding load
