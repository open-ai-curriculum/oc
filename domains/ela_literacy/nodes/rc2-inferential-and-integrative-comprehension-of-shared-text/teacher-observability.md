# RC2 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `RC2` status
- dependency posture for `RC1`
- direct comprehension accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether inference is anchored in multiple relevant text details
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who are blocked by the dominant failure modes on `RC2`
- who improve on supported texts but lose control on changed surfaces
- who appear ready for the next comprehension boundary
