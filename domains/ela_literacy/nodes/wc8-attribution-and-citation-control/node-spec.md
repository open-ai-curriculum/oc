## Purpose

The learner manages attribution and citation in sourced writing after synthesis itself is secure.

## Dependencies

- `WC7`
- `L3`

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `WC8` only when all of the following are true:

1. attributes source material appropriately in research writing
2. maintains usable citation control without obscuring the writing
3. succeeds across changed source sets or citation surfaces
4. remains stable on delayed recheck

- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `21`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- separate citation control from source synthesis quality
- maintain usable attribution in research writing
- support advanced analysis and capstone writing
