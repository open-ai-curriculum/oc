# W1 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: capture ideas through drawing, dictation, labeling, or early marks
- pass rule: successful idea capture in at least 5 of 6 supported production opportunities

### G2. Meaning Gate

- purpose: confirm that the produced representation carries intended meaning
- pass rule: another observer can recover the basic intended message in at least 4 of 5 prompts

### G3. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm performance across changed topics or production formats
- pass rule: one successful changed-format idea-capture task

### G4. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 14 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct accuracy improves but meaning or transfer still fails
- `provisional`: direct, meaning, and transfer pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all four gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or later regresses structurally
