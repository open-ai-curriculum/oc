# W1 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `W1` status
- dependency posture for `O3` and `P4`
- direct idea-capture accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner can externalize oral meaning into a representation
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who can talk about an idea but not capture it
- who produces marks without recoverable meaning
- who succeeds only in one representation format
