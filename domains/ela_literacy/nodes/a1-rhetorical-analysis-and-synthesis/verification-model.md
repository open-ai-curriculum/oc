# A1 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: analyze rhetoric and synthesize claims across complex sources
- pass rule: successful advanced rhetorical analysis in at least 5 of 6 structured opportunities

### G2. Meaning Gate

- purpose: confirm that the learner explains how rhetorical choices and cross-source evidence support the synthesis
- pass rule: correct rhetorical-analysis explanation in at least 4 of 5 prompts

### G3. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm performance across changed rhetorical situations or complex source sets
- pass rule: one successful changed-context or changed-source-set rhetorical-analysis task

### G4. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 21 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct accuracy improves but meaning or transfer still fails
- `provisional`: direct, meaning, and transfer pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all four gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or reverts to disconnected summary and assertion
