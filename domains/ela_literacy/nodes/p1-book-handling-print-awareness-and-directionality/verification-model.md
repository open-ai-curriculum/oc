# P1 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: handle books and orient to print meaningfully
- pass rule: successful purposeful book and page handling in at least 5 of 6 structured opportunities

### G2. Meaning Gate

- purpose: distinguish print from pictures and demonstrate basic directionality
- pass rule: correct print-awareness response in at least 4 of 5 prompts

### G3. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm that the skill survives changed books, layouts, or print formats
- pass rule: one successful changed-format print-awareness task

### G4. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 14 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct handling improves but meaning or transfer still fails
- `provisional`: direct, meaning, and transfer pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all four gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or later regresses structurally
