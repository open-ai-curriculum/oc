# FR2 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: confirm that the learner uses print as an encoding of spoken sounds
- pass rule: successful demonstration in at least 5 of 6 structured opportunities

### G2. Meaning Gate

- purpose: confirm explicit understanding of the print-speech relationship
- pass rule: correct explanation or demonstration in at least 4 of 5 prompts

### G3. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm bidirectional application in decoding-like and encoding-like tasks
- pass rule: one successful paired changed-set task across both directions

### G4. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 14 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct gate improves but meaning or transfer still fails
- `provisional`: direct, meaning, and transfer pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all four gates pass
- `unstable`: previously secure print-encoding logic regresses on later checks
