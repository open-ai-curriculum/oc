# FR6 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `FR6` status
- dependency posture for `FR5` and `P2`
- direct connected-text accuracy
- fluency, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether difficulty is primarily tracking, word integrity, or pace
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who loses place in connected text
- who is accurate on isolated words but not in passage reading
- who remains too labored for usable connected-text flow
