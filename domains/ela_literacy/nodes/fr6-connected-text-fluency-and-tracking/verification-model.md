# FR6 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: confirm accurate connected-text reading with stable tracking and usable pacing
- pass rule: successful performance in at least 9 of 10 structured opportunities

### G2. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm connected-text control across a changed passage or text surface
- pass rule: one successful changed-passage or changed-surface reading task

### G3. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 21 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: accuracy improves but tracking or pacing remains weak
- `provisional`: direct and transfer pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all three gates pass
- `unstable`: previously secure connected-text control regresses on later checks
