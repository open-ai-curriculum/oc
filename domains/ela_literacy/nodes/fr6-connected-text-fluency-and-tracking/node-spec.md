# FR6 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `FR6`
- name: `connected_text_fluency_and_tracking`
- domain: `ela_literacy`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `foundational-reading-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner reads short connected text with enough accuracy, tracking stability, and usable pacing to support passage-level literacy work.

## Dependencies

- `FR5` orthographic consolidation and automatic word recognition
- `P2` concept of word, spacing, and text tracking

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `FR6` only when all of the following are true:

1. tracks connected text accurately without losing place or skipping lines
2. maintains word-level integrity while reading across short connected passages
3. reads with usable pacing rather than laborious word-by-word breakdown alone
4. carries control into a modestly changed text surface
5. remains stable on delayed recheck

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.9`
- explanation required: `false`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `21`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- sustain accurate movement through connected text
- coordinate tracking and word recognition together
- support later passage-level comprehension demands without conflating them with decoding alone

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
