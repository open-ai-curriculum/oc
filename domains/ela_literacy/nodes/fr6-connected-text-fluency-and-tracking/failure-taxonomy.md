# FR6 Failure Taxonomy

### FR6-FM-01 Tracking Loss

- description: learner loses place, skips lines, or misaligns to the text stream even when many words are known

### FR6-FM-02 Word-Integrity Breakdown In Passage Reading

- description: learner distorts or omits words while moving through connected text

### FR6-FM-03 Labored Pace Without Flow

- description: learner maintains some accuracy but the pacing remains too effortful to support usable connected-text reading

### FR6-FM-04 Surface-Change Instability

- description: learner reads one familiar connected-text surface but loses control on a changed passage or layout
