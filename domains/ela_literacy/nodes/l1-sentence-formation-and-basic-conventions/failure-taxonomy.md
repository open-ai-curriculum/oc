# L1 Failure Taxonomy

### L1-FM-01 Fragment-Dominant Output

- description: learner produces labels, fragments, or loosely connected words instead of complete simple sentences

### L1-FM-02 Oral-Written Transfer Split

- description: learner can say a complete sentence orally but cannot carry comparable structure into supported written production

### L1-FM-03 Convention Load Collapse

- description: learner sentence meaning becomes unclear because capitalization, spacing, or end-mark control collapses

### L1-FM-04 Prompt-Bound Sentence Production

- description: learner can form one rehearsed sentence frame but does not generalize sentence formation across changed prompts
