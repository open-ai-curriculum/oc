# L3 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `L3` status
- dependency posture for `L1` and `L2`
- direct revision accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether revision improves meaning or only fixes surface errors
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who only proofread without revising meaningfully
- who ignore audience or purpose in revision
- who lose revision control beyond one isolated sentence
