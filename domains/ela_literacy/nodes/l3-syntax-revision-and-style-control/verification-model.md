# L3 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: revise syntax and style to improve clarity, coherence, or rhetorical effect
- pass rule: successful revision performance in at least 5 of 6 structured opportunities

### G2. Meaning Gate

- purpose: confirm that the learner can explain why the revision improves meaning, flow, or audience fit
- pass rule: correct revision rationale in at least 4 of 5 prompts

### G3. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm performance across changed writing contexts or revision prompts
- pass rule: one successful changed-context syntax or style revision task

### G4. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 21 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct accuracy improves but meaning or transfer still fails
- `provisional`: direct, meaning, and transfer pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all four gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or returns to error-correction-only editing
