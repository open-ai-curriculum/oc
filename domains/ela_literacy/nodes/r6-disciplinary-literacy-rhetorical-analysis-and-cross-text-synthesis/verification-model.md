# R6 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: analyze rhetoric and synthesize across multiple texts or disciplinary sources
- pass rule: successful cross-text or disciplinary analysis in at least 5 of 6 structured opportunities

### G2. Meaning Gate

- purpose: confirm that the learner explains how cross-text evidence or rhetorical features support the synthesis
- pass rule: correct synthesis-to-evidence explanation in at least 4 of 5 prompts

### G3. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm performance across changed source sets, disciplines, or rhetorical situations
- pass rule: one successful changed-source, changed-discipline, or changed-rhetorical-context task

### G4. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 21 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct accuracy improves but meaning or transfer still fails
- `provisional`: direct, meaning, and transfer pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all four gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or reverts to disconnected single-text treatment
