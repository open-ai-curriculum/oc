# R6 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `R6` status
- dependency posture for `R4` and `R5`
- direct cross-text analysis accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner truly synthesizes across sources
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who stay inside one text at a time
- who gather details from multiple sources without coherent synthesis
- who lose performance when source sets or disciplinary frames change
