# R6 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `R6`
- name: `disciplinary_literacy_rhetorical_analysis_and_cross_text_synthesis`
- domain: `ela_literacy`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `disciplinary-literacy-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner analyzes rhetoric, synthesizes across texts, and performs disciplinary literacy work well enough to support upper-grade reading demands and research writing.

## Dependencies

- `R4` literary structure, theme, and character analysis
- `R5` informational structure, author claim, and source use

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `R6` only when all of the following are true:

1. compares or synthesizes meaning across more than one text or source
2. analyzes rhetoric, argument, or disciplinary structure with textual grounding
3. succeeds in at least one non-generic disciplinary or cross-text setting
4. maintains coherence when multiple sources or perspectives are in play
5. remains stable on delayed recheck

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.85`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `21`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- synthesize across texts or sources
- analyze rhetoric or discipline-specific text features
- support later research synthesis and advanced rhetorical analysis
- operate beyond single-text literal comprehension

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
