# FR3 Failure Taxonomy

### FR3-FM-01 Guessing Over Application

- description: learner appears to read or spell by habit or partial cueing rather than full simple-pattern reasoning

### FR3-FM-02 Decoding-Encoding Split

- description: learner can decode supported simple words or encode them, but not both with usable control

### FR3-FM-03 Memorized-List Dependence

- description: learner succeeds on practiced items but loses control on changed simple-word sets

### FR3-FM-04 Pattern-Element Omission

- description: learner drops or distorts one part of the controlled word pattern during decoding or encoding
