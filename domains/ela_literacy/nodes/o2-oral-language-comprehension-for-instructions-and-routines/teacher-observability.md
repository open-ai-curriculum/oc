# O2 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `O2` status
- dependency posture for `O1`
- direct comprehension accuracy
- transfer and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether success reflects actual comprehension or imitation-only responding
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who only succeeds on familiar routine wording
- who breaks down on multi-part oral directions
- who loses comprehension when speaker or setting changes
