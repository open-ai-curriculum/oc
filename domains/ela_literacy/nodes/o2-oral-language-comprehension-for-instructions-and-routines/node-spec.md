# O2 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `O2`
- name: `oral_language_comprehension_for_instructions_and_routines`
- domain: `ela_literacy`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `upstream-readiness-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner comprehends routine spoken directions, prompts, and teacher language well enough to access early literacy instruction and interaction.

## Dependencies

- `O1` joint attention, turn-taking, and response readiness

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `O2` only when all of the following are true:

1. follows common spoken directions in routine contexts
2. distinguishes relevant oral-language meaning from unrelated environmental cues
3. succeeds with changed wording, speaker, or routine surface
4. demonstrates comprehension through action, selection, or response
5. remains stable on delayed recheck

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.85`
- explanation required: `false`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `14`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- follow routine oral instructions
- act on teacher language without heavy imitation-only dependence
- respond appropriately to changed oral prompts
- support later speaking, listening, print, and phonological readiness work

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
