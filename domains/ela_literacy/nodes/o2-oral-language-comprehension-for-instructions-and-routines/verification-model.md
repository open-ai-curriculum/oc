# O2 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: follow spoken directions and routine oral prompts
- pass rule: successful comprehension in at least 5 of 6 supported oral-language opportunities

### G2. Meaning Gate

- purpose: confirm that success is driven by comprehension rather than imitation-only responding
- pass rule: correct action, selection, or response in at least 4 of 5 changed prompts

### G3. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm comprehension across changed speakers, wording, or routine settings
- pass rule: one successful changed-context comprehension task

### G4. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 14 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct accuracy improves but meaning or transfer still fails
- `provisional`: direct, meaning, and transfer pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all four gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or later shows comprehension regression
