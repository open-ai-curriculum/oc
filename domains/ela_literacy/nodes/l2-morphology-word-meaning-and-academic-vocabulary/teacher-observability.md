# L2 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `L2` status
- dependency posture for `FR4` and `RC3`
- direct vocabulary and morphology accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether meaning is context-grounded
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who guess word meaning without context
- who miss usable morphology clues
- who understand vocabulary receptively but cannot use it accurately
