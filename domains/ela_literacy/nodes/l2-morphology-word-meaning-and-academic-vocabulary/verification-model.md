# L2 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: interpret or use targeted words and morphology accurately in supported contexts
- pass rule: successful vocabulary and morphology performance in at least 5 of 6 structured opportunities

### G2. Meaning Gate

- purpose: confirm that the learner explains or demonstrates why the word or word-part meaning fits the context
- pass rule: correct contextual explanation in at least 4 of 5 prompts

### G3. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm application across changed texts, tasks, or production contexts
- pass rule: one successful changed-context vocabulary or morphology task

### G4. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 14 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct accuracy improves but meaning or transfer still fails
- `provisional`: direct, meaning, and transfer pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all four gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or word-meaning application regresses materially
