# P3 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: detect or manipulate spoken-language sound structures
- pass rule: successful phonological-awareness performance in at least 5 of 6 structured oral tasks

### G2. Meaning Gate

- purpose: confirm that the learner is attending to sound structure rather than semantic or visual cues
- pass rule: correct sound-focused response in at least 4 of 5 changed prompts

### G3. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm that the skill survives changed words, prompts, or oral task formats
- pass rule: one successful changed-format phonological task

### G4. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 14 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct accuracy improves but meaning or transfer still fails
- `provisional`: direct, meaning, and transfer pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all four gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or later regresses structurally
