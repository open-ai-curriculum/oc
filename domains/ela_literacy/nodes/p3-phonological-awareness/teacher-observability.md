# P3 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `P3` status
- dependency posture for `O2`
- direct phonological-awareness accuracy
- transfer and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner is attending to sound structure rather than meaning
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who confuses sound work with meaning-based guessing
- who breaks on segmentation or blending
- who succeeds only in one prompt format
