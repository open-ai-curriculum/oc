# F2 Failure Taxonomy

### F2-FM-01 Guessing-Over-Decoding

- description: learner identifies words by partial cues, memory, or context rather than decoding through the full sequence

### F2-FM-02 Encoding-Transfer Weakness

- description: learner can decode some simple words but cannot map the same sound sequence back into spelling reliably

### F2-FM-03 Word-Set Fragility

- description: learner succeeds on rehearsed items but performance collapses on changed simple-word sets

### F2-FM-04 Sequence-Tracking Breakdown

- description: learner knows some individual sound-symbol correspondences but loses control over ordered blending or segmenting across the whole word
