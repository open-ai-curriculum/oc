# F2 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: decode and encode supported simple words with applied sound-symbol control
- pass rule: successful decoding and encoding in at least 5 of 6 structured opportunities

### G2. Meaning Gate

- purpose: confirm that the learner uses sound-symbol reasoning rather than whole-word guessing
- pass rule: correct explanation or demonstration of how at least 4 of 5 words were worked out

### G3. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm performance on changed word sets or changed task surfaces
- pass rule: one successful changed-set reading task and one successful changed-set encoding task

### G4. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 14 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct accuracy improves but explanation or transfer still fails
- `provisional`: direct, meaning, and transfer pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all four gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or regresses into guessing-dominant decoding
