# F1 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `F1`
- name: `alphabetic_principle_and_sound_symbol_mapping`
- domain: `ela_literacy`
- support tier: `Core release`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `literacy-entry-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner understands and uses the alphabetic principle by linking phonemes and graphemes with enough control to support early decoding and encoding.

## Dependencies

- `P3` phonological awareness
- `P4` alphabet knowledge and letter formation

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `F1` only when all of the following are true:

1. maps supported sounds to letters and letters to sounds with usable consistency
2. explains or demonstrates that print encodes spoken sounds
3. applies sound-symbol knowledge in both reading-like and writing-like tasks
4. succeeds across changed word sets or task formats
5. remains stable on delayed recheck

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.85`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `14`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- connect letters to their associated sounds in supported sets
- use sound-symbol knowledge to support early decoding and encoding
- distinguish genuine mapping knowledge from rote visual memorization
- provide the formal bridge into independent word reading

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
