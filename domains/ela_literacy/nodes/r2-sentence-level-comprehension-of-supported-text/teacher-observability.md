# R2 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `R2` status
- dependency posture for `FR4` and `R1`
- direct sentence-comprehension accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether decoding is being connected to meaning
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who can read sentences aloud without understanding them
- who breaks down when sentence structure changes
- who appears blocked by decoding load rather than comprehension alone
