# WC6 Failure Taxonomy

### WC6-FM-01 Genre-Blurring

- description: learner uses one generic structure regardless of writing purpose

### WC6-FM-02 Reasoning-Structure Mismatch

- description: learner cannot match the structure to the explanatory or argumentative demand

### WC6-FM-03 Narrative-Control Instability

- description: learner loses control of sequence, perspective, or development in narrative work

### WC6-FM-04 Prompt-Genre Transfer Fragility

- description: learner succeeds in one genre but not changed writing situations
