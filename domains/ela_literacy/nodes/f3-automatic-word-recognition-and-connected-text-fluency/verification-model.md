# F3 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: read supported connected text with sufficient automaticity, accuracy, and tracking
- pass rule: successful connected-text reading in at least 5 of 6 structured opportunities

### G2. Meaning Gate

- purpose: confirm that fluency preserves meaning rather than becoming pace-only performance
- pass rule: correct meaning checks on at least 4 of 5 connected-text prompts

### G3. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm performance across changed text sets or layouts
- pass rule: one successful changed-text or changed-layout connected-reading task

### G4. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 14 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct accuracy improves but meaning or transfer still fails
- `provisional`: direct, meaning, and transfer pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all four gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or connected-text control regresses materially
