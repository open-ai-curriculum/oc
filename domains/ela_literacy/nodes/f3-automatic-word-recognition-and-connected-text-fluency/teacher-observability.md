# F3 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `F3` status
- dependency posture for `F2` and `P2`
- direct connected-text reading accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether fluency preserves meaning
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who read word-by-word with excessive effort
- who lose place or line progression in connected text
- who appear fluent only on one memorized passage
