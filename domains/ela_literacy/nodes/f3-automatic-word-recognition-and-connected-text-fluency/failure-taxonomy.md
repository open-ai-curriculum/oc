# F3 Failure Taxonomy

### F3-FM-01 Labored-Word-By-Word Reading

- description: learner can decode many words but connected-text reading remains too effortful to sustain fluency

### F3-FM-02 Tracking Instability

- description: learner loses line progression, spacing control, or place in text during connected reading

### F3-FM-03 Rate-Over-Meaning Tradeoff

- description: learner speeds up or performs fluency-like reading while comprehension or accuracy drops

### F3-FM-04 Text-Set Fragility

- description: learner succeeds on one familiar passage type but performance collapses on changed texts or layouts
