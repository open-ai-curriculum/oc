# R1 Verification Model

## Gate Set

### G1. Direct Performance Gate

- purpose: derive literal meaning from shared or read-aloud text
- pass rule: successful literal comprehension in at least 5 of 6 structured opportunities

### G2. Meaning Gate

- purpose: confirm that recalled or selected responses are actually text-relevant
- pass rule: correct text-grounded response in at least 4 of 5 prompts

### G3. Transfer Gate

- purpose: confirm performance across changed texts, genres, or oral response formats
- pass rule: one successful changed-text comprehension task

### G4. Retention Gate

- purpose: confirm persistence after spacing
- pass rule: stable delayed recheck after about 14 days

## Status Logic

- `blocked`: direct gate fails
- `emerging`: direct accuracy improves but meaning or transfer still fails
- `provisional`: direct, meaning, and transfer pass while retention is pending
- `secure`: all four gates pass
- `unstable`: a previously secure learner fails delayed recheck or later regresses structurally
