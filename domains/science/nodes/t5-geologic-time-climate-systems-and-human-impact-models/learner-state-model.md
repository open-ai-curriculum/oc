# T5 Learner State Model

The learner-state for `T5` tracks:

- dependency security on `T4`, `M4`, and `E3`
- direct accuracy on geologic-time, climate-system, and human-impact tasks
- explanation quality for advanced Earth-system modeling
- transfer performance across unfamiliar Earth-system contexts
- delayed retention outcome

`secure` requires all verification gates to pass. Any delayed failure or dependency instability should move the learner to `unstable`.
