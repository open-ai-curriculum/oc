# T5 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`T5` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across geologic-time, climate-system, and human-impact model tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains long-term Earth-system change or bounded human impact in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar advanced Earth-system context.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `42` days.

## Verification Notes

- Timescale discipline is essential; short-term events cannot stand in for long-term system claims.
- Human-impact reasoning must remain evidence-based and system-aware.
- Synthetic evidence supports package inspection only and cannot authorize final learner claims.
