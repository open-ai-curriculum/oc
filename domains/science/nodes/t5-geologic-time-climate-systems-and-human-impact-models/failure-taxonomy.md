# T5 Failure Taxonomy

- `T5-FM-01` timescale collapse: learner uses short-term or local observations to justify long-term Earth-history or climate claims.
- `T5-FM-02` single-variable climate simplification: learner ignores interacting Earth-system components and feedbacks.
- `T5-FM-03` overtotalized human-impact claim: learner attributes all Earth-system change to human action without bounded evidence.
- `T5-FM-04` Earth-system evidence mismatch: learner makes advanced Earth claims without aligning them to the actual data or model.
