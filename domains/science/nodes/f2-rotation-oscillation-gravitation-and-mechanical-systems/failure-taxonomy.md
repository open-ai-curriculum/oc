# F2 Failure Taxonomy

- `F2-FM-01` rotation-linear collapse: learner treats rotational relations as identical to linear ones without bounded interpretation.
- `F2-FM-02` oscillation-pattern only: learner describes periodicity without explaining restoring dynamics.
- `F2-FM-03` gravitation-formula isolation: learner recalls gravitation formulas without model-based orbital reasoning.
- `F2-FM-04` mechanical-system overgeneralization: learner extends one mechanics model to incompatible rotational or orbital cases.
