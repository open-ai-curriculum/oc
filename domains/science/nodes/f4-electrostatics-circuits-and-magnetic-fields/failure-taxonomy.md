# F4 Failure Taxonomy

- `F4-FM-01` current-only electricity collapse: learner explains all electric behavior through current without field or potential reasoning.
- `F4-FM-02` component-island circuits: learner describes circuit parts without system-level relation among them.
- `F4-FM-03` magnetism-rule memorization: learner uses direction rules without interaction-model explanation.
- `F4-FM-04` electromagnetic-context overgeneralization: learner applies one electric or magnetic relation beyond its bounded case.
