# B5 Failure Taxonomy

- `B5-FM-01` single-variable ecology collapse: learner explains ecosystem outcomes with one factor only when interactions matter.
- `B5-FM-02` field-evidence overreach: learner generalizes beyond what the sampling design supports.
- `B5-FM-03` biotic-abiotic disconnect: learner separates living-system reasoning from environmental constraints.
- `B5-FM-04` impact-claim inflation: learner overstates human or environmental impact without enough evidence scale.
