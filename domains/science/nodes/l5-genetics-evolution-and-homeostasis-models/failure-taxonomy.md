# L5 Failure Taxonomy

- `L5-FM-01` need-based inheritance drift: learner explains inherited traits as acquired because they were needed.
- `L5-FM-02` teleological evolution error: learner treats evolution as intentional improvement by individual organisms.
- `L5-FM-03` static-homeostasis misconception: learner treats regulation as no-change rather than bounded dynamic stability.
- `L5-FM-04` gene-trait oversimplification: learner treats genes, traits, and expression as one-step deterministic equivalence without context.
