# I5 Intervention Playbook

- For `I5-FM-01`, use question-to-measurement matching tasks that force explicit justification of what must be measured and why.
- For `I5-FM-02`, use claim-calibration prompts that connect uncertainty patterns to what can still be supported.
- For `I5-FM-03`, use side-by-side analyses separating precision, validity, and representativeness.
- For `I5-FM-04`, use method-critique routines that begin with the investigation purpose before evaluating the design.
