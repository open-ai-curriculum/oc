# I5 Learner State Model

The learner-state for `I5` tracks:

- dependency security on `I4`
- direct accuracy on quantitative design, uncertainty, and method-critique tasks
- explanation quality for methodological adequacy and inference calibration
- transfer performance across advanced investigation contexts
- delayed retention outcome

`secure` requires all verification gates to pass. Any delayed failure or dependency instability should move the learner to `unstable`.
