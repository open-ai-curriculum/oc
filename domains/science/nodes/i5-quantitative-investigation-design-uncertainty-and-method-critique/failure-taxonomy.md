# I5 Failure Taxonomy

- `I5-FM-01` quantitative-design mismatch: learner selects measurements that do not fit the scientific question.
- `I5-FM-02` uncertainty absolutism: learner treats uncertainty as proving either nothing can be known or everything is the same.
- `I5-FM-03` precision-validity confusion: learner mistakes precise tools for overall methodological strength.
- `I5-FM-04` critique without purpose alignment: learner critiques methods generically instead of against the actual question and inference goal.
