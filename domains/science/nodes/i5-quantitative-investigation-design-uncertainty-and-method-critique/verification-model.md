# I5 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`I5` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across quantitative design, uncertainty interpretation, and method-critique tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains methodological adequacy or quantitative uncertainty in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar advanced investigation context.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `35` days.

## Verification Notes

- Numeric language without disciplined interpretation is insufficient.
- Method critique must remain tied to the actual scientific purpose and available evidence.
- Synthetic evidence supports structural testing only and cannot authorize final learner claims.
