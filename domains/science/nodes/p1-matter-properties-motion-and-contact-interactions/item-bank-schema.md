# P1 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Properties Motion And Interactions

- identify an observable material property
- describe a motion change
- connect a push or pull to an outcome

### Family B. Explanation

- explain how the observation supports the property or interaction claim

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when an explanation uses unsupported hidden causes

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across balls, ramps, blocks, fabrics, liquids, and other familiar materials
