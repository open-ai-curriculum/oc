# P1 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `P1` status
- dependency posture for `Q1` and `E1`
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner grounds property and motion claims in observation
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who misses observable material properties
- who misreads motion changes
- who uses fantasy or hidden causes instead of direct evidence
