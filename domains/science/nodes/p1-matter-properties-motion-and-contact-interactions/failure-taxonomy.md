# P1 Failure Taxonomy

### P1-FM-01 Property Observation Instability

- description: learner cannot reliably identify observable material properties from direct evidence

### P1-FM-02 Motion Change Misread

- description: learner misidentifies what changed in the object's motion

### P1-FM-03 Interaction-Cause Disconnect

- description: learner notices movement but cannot connect the contact interaction to the outcome

### P1-FM-04 Hidden-Cause Or Fantasy Drift

- description: learner explains physical events using unsupported hidden causes, intentions, or fantasy
