# I4 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`I4` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across multivariable design, sampling, and error-awareness tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains methodological tradeoffs or limitations in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar scientific investigation context.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `35` days.

## Verification Notes

- Naming variables without controlling or prioritizing them is insufficient.
- Sampling and error explanations must remain bounded to the evidence and design provided.
- Synthetic evidence supports package inspection only and cannot authorize learner-impacting claims.
