# C3 Failure Taxonomy

- `C3-FM-01` equation-procedure detachment: learner runs symbolic routines without matter-conservation meaning.
- `C3-FM-02` mole-ratio instability: learner misuses or misinterprets proportional reaction relationships.
- `C3-FM-03` solution-unit collapse: learner confuses amount, concentration, and volume roles.
- `C3-FM-04` measurement-claim mismatch: learner overstates quantitative certainty beyond measurement quality.
