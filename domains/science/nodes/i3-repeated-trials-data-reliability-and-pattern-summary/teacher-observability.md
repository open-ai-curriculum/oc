# I3 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `I3` status
- dependency posture for `I2`
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner can use repeated trials to judge reliability and summarize patterns
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who undervalues repeated trials
- who misjudges reliability
- who states stronger conclusions than the data supports
