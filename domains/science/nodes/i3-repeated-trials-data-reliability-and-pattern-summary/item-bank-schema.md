# I3 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Trials Reliability And Patterns

- identify why more than one trial helps
- judge whether results are consistent
- summarize a data pattern

### Family B. Explanation

- explain what repeated trials or a pattern summary shows

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when a pattern claim is too strong for the available data

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across plant, motion, materials, and environmental investigations
