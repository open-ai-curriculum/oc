# L1 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `L1` status
- dependency posture for `Q1` and `E1`
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner connects organism needs and habitats using evidence
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who misclassifies organism needs
- who cannot describe observable traits reliably
- who uses fantasy or personification instead of evidence
