# L1 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Needs Traits And Habitats

- identify a basic organism need
- describe an observable trait
- match an organism to a habitat feature

### Family B. Explanation

- explain how an observation supports a needs, traits, or habitat claim

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when a claim about an organism is unsupported or anthropomorphic

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across plants, insects, birds, pets, and familiar wild organisms
