# L1 Failure Taxonomy

### L1-FM-01 Needs Misclassification

- description: learner misidentifies what an organism needs to live or grow

### L1-FM-02 Trait Observation Instability

- description: learner cannot reliably describe observable organism traits from direct evidence

### L1-FM-03 Habitat-Function Disconnect

- description: learner names a habitat but cannot connect it to how a need is met

### L1-FM-04 Anthropomorphic Or Fantasy Drift

- description: learner explains organism needs or traits using personification, fantasy, or unsupported human-like assumptions
