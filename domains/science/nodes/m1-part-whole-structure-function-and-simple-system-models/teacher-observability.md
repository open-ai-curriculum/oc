# M1 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `M1` status
- dependency posture for `Q3`
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner can map simple models to parts and functions
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who cannot identify meaningful parts within a whole
- who confuses the model with the real system
- who notices parts but cannot explain their function
