# P2 Failure Taxonomy

### P2-FM-01 Matter-Change Misread

- description: learner cannot reliably identify what changed in the material or system

### P2-FM-02 Energy-Evidence Disconnect

- description: learner cannot connect an observed effect to evidence of energy transfer

### P2-FM-03 Force-Effect Disconnect

- description: learner notices movement or change but cannot connect it to a force effect coherently

### P2-FM-04 Hidden-Cause Or Fantasy Drift

- description: learner explains physical changes using unsupported hidden causes or fantasy
