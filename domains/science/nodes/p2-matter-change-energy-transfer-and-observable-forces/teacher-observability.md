# P2 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `P2` status
- dependency posture for `P1` and `I1`
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner uses evidence to explain matter change, energy transfer, and force effects
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who misreads material change
- who cannot connect energy effects to observable evidence
- who notices motion but not the force-effect relationship
