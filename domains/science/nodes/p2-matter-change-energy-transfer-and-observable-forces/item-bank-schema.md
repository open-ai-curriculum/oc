# P2 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Matter Energy And Forces

- identify an observable material change
- identify evidence of energy transfer
- describe a visible force effect

### Family B. Explanation

- explain how the observation supports the physical-science claim

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when a physical explanation ignores the observed change or evidence

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across heating, lighting, sound, ramps, magnets, and related elementary contexts
