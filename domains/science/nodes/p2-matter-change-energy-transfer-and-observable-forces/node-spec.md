# P2 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `P2`
- name: `matter_change_energy_transfer_and_observable_forces`
- domain: `science`
- support tier: `Incubation`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `science-bridge-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner explains observable changes in matter, identifies simple evidence of energy transfer such as heat or light effects, and reasons about visible force effects beyond a single push-pull event.

## Dependencies

- `P1` matter properties, motion, and contact interactions
- `I1` fair tests, tool use, and measurement routines

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `P2` only when all of the following are true:

1. identifies an observable change in matter or material state
2. describes a simple energy transfer effect using direct evidence
3. explains a visible force effect in a bounded physical context
4. succeeds across more than one materials or motion context
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `21`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- describe what changed in a material or system
- identify evidence that heat, light, sound, or motion energy was transferred
- explain visible force outcomes using observed interactions
- avoid relying on unsupported hidden causes

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
