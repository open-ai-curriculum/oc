# D1 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Problems Criteria Constraints And Solutions

- define the problem
- identify criteria and constraints
- sketch or choose a fitting solution

### Family B. Explanation

- explain why the proposed solution fits the design conditions

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when a solution ignores a criterion or constraint

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across structures, water movement, transport, shelter, and similar early design tasks
