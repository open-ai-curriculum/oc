# D1 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `D1` status
- dependency posture for `Q2` and `E1`
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner can distinguish criteria from constraints and sketch fitting solutions
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who cannot state the design problem clearly
- who confuses criteria and constraints
- who proposes solutions that ignore problem conditions
