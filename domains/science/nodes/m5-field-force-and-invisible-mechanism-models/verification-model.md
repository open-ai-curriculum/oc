# M5 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`M5` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across field, force, and invisible-mechanism model tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains model usefulness and limits in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar advanced scientific system.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `35` days.

## Verification Notes

- The learner must link models to observable evidence, not simply recite invisible entities.
- Analogies are useful only when their limits are also controlled.
- Synthetic evidence supports package inspection only and cannot authorize final learner claims.
