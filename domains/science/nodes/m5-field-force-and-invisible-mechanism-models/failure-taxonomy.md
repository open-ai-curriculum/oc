# M5 Failure Taxonomy

- `M5-FM-01` model-literalism error: learner treats the field or invisible-mechanism model as direct literal ontology.
- `M5-FM-02` evidence-detached mechanism claim: learner proposes invisible causes without tying them to observable effects.
- `M5-FM-03` uncontrolled analogy transfer: learner imports analogy features beyond their useful limits.
- `M5-FM-04` model-competition neglect: learner cannot compare alternative unobservable-mechanism explanations.
