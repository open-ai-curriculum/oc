# P3 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`P3` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across particle, wave, and force-interaction tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains a phenomenon using an appropriate model in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar physical-system context.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `28` days.

## Verification Notes

- Memorized vocabulary without model use is insufficient.
- Explanations must stay within the limits of the taught model and observable evidence.
- Synthetic evidence supports package structure only and cannot replace human review.
