# P3 Failure Taxonomy

- `P3-FM-01` literal particle picture error: learner treats the particle model as a direct visible picture rather than a useful explanatory model.
- `P3-FM-02` wave transport confusion: learner treats waves as chunks of matter moving wholesale from source to receiver.
- `P3-FM-03` one-sided force reasoning: learner explains interactions as if only one object exerts force.
- `P3-FM-04` model-boundary drift: learner extends the model beyond what the evidence or instruction supports.
