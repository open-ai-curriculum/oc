# P3 Intervention Playbook

- For `P3-FM-01`, use compare-and-bound tasks that ask what the particle model helps explain and what it does not show directly.
- For `P3-FM-02`, use disturbance demonstrations with explicit tracing of energy movement versus matter location.
- For `P3-FM-03`, use paired-object interaction diagrams that require naming forces on both objects.
- For `P3-FM-04`, use model-boundary checklists before explanations are accepted.
