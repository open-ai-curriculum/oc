# P3 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `P3`
- name: `particle_models_waves_and_force_interactions`
- domain: `science`
- support tier: `Incubation`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `science-middle-grade-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner uses bounded particle, wave, and force-interaction models to explain observable physical phenomena without treating models as literal pictures of reality.

## Dependencies

- `P2` matter change, energy transfer, and observable forces
- `M2` mechanism, cause-effect, and process models

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `P3` only when all of the following are true:

1. uses a particle model to explain at least one observable property or change
2. uses a wave model to explain signal or energy transfer in a bounded case
3. reasons about force interactions rather than one-sided pushes or pulls
4. succeeds across more than one physical context
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `28`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- describe matter with a simplified particle model when appropriate
- explain how waves carry energy or information in a bounded system
- reason about interacting forces affecting motion or stability
- state where the model helps and where it remains simplified

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
