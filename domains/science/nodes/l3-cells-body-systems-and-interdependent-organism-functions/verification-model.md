# L3 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`L3` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across cell, body-system, and coordinated-function tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains how structures support functions in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar organism or body-system context.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `28` days.

## Verification Notes

- Labeling body parts without functional reasoning is insufficient.
- System interaction claims must stay bounded to available evidence and taught models.
- Synthetic evidence can support structural testing only and cannot stand in for human approval.
