# L3 Intervention Playbook

- For `L3-FM-01`, use image-supported cell-to-organism comparison routines that repeatedly anchor the basic-unit idea.
- For `L3-FM-02`, use structure-function sentence frames that require the learner to complete "because it helps..." explanations.
- For `L3-FM-03`, use flow maps showing how matter or gases move across at least two connected systems.
- For `L3-FM-04`, use scale-sorting tasks that force the learner to place examples at cell, organ, system, or organism level before explaining.
