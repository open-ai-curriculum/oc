# Q1 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Direct Observation

- notice observable properties
- compare two objects or events
- identify visible change

### Family B. Explanation

- explain what was observed and how the comparison was made

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when a claim goes beyond what was directly observed

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across objects, pictures, natural materials, and familiar outdoor contexts
