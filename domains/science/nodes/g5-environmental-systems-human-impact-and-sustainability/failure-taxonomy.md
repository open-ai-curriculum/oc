# G5 Failure Taxonomy

- `G5-FM-01` single-metric sustainability collapse: learner judges environmental systems through one metric while ignoring tradeoffs.
- `G5-FM-02` impact-claim advocacy drift: learner advances a position without enough system evidence or uncertainty handling.
- `G5-FM-03` human-natural separation: learner discusses human impacts and environmental systems as disconnected layers.
- `G5-FM-04` source-quality neglect: learner uses environmental claims without weighing evidence quality or limitations.
