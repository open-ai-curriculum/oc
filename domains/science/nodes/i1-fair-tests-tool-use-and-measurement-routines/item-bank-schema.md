# I1 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Fair Test And Tool Use

- identify what should stay the same
- choose a relevant tool
- follow a simple measurement routine

### Family B. Explanation

- explain what is being measured and why the test is fair

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when too many things changed or when the wrong tool was used

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across plant, motion, weather, and material-testing contexts
