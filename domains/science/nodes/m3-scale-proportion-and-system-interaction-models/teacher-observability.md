# M3 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `M3` status
- dependency posture for `M2` and `I2`
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner can use scale and interaction-aware models rather than isolated-part descriptions
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who ignores scale
- who struggles with relative change
- who leaves out key interactions in a system explanation
