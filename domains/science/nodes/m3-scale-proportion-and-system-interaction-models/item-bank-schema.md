# M3 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Scale Proportion And Interactions

- identify when scale matters
- describe a relative change
- explain interacting parts in a system

### Family B. Explanation

- explain how scale or one part's change affects the system model

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when a model ignores scale or interactions that matter

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across ecosystems, body systems, Earth systems, and physical systems
