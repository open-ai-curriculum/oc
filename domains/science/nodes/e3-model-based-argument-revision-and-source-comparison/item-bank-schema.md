# E3 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Arguments Revisions And Sources

- build a model-based argument
- revise a claim using new evidence
- compare sources or representations

### Family B. Explanation

- explain why the revision or source choice is stronger

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when an argument ignores new evidence or misuses a source

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across life, physical, and Earth/space argument contexts
