# G4 Failure Taxonomy

- `G4-FM-01` space-fact cataloging: learner lists astronomy facts without model-based explanation.
- `G4-FM-02` observation-inference collapse: learner treats indirect astronomical inference as direct observation.
- `G4-FM-03` scale-model mismatch: learner uses a model that does not fit the scale or process of the phenomenon.
- `G4-FM-04` cosmic-claim overreach: learner extends limited astronomical evidence to larger claims without sufficient support.
