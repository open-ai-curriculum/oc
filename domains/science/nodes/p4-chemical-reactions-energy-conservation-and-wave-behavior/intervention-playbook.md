# P4 Intervention Playbook

- For `P4-FM-01`, use compare-and-classify sets that separate reaction evidence from mere appearance changes.
- For `P4-FM-02`, use closed-system accounting tables that force explicit tracking of matter or energy before and after change.
- For `P4-FM-03`, use wave-behavior explanation frames that require observation plus model language.
- For `P4-FM-04`, use system-boundary marking routines before conservation arguments are accepted.
