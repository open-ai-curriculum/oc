# P4 Failure Taxonomy

- `P4-FM-01` reaction-evidence underread: learner treats chemical reactions as superficial appearance changes only.
- `P4-FM-02` conservation loss claim: learner states matter or energy vanished because it became less visible or harder to track.
- `P4-FM-03` wave-behavior vocabulary shell: learner uses terms like reflect or absorb without explanatory model control.
- `P4-FM-04` system-boundary drift: learner makes conservation or wave claims without respecting the defined system.
