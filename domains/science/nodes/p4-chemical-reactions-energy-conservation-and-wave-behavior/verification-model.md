# P4 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`P4` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across reaction, conservation, and wave-behavior tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains the evidence or mechanism in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar physical-phenomenon context.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `28` days.

## Verification Notes

- Vocabulary-only recognition of "reaction" or "wave" is insufficient.
- Conservation claims must stay bounded to the modeled system and given evidence.
- Synthetic evidence supports package testing only and cannot authorize final learner claims.
