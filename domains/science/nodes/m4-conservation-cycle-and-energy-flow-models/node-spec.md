# M4 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `M4`
- name: `conservation_cycle_and_energy_flow_models`
- domain: `science`
- support tier: `Incubation`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `science-secondary-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner uses conservation, cycle, and energy-flow models across scientific systems without collapsing different quantities or processes into one undifferentiated account.

## Dependencies

- `M3` scale, proportion, and system interaction models
- `E2` claim-evidence-reasoning and competing explanations

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `M4` only when all of the following are true:

1. builds or critiques models that preserve system boundaries and conserved quantities
2. distinguishes cycling processes from one-way flow accounts when the model requires it
3. represents energy movement or transformation without turning energy into matter
4. succeeds across more than one disciplinary system context
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `35`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- explain what is conserved and what changes form within a model
- trace matter cycles and energy flow with appropriate distinction
- critique models that hide important reservoirs, transfers, or boundaries
- use the same modeling discipline across life, Earth, and physical science contexts

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
