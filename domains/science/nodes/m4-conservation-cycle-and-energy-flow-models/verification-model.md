# M4 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`M4` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across conservation, cycle, and energy-flow model tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains modeled relationships or boundaries in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar scientific system.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `35` days.

## Verification Notes

- General system vocabulary without quantity control is insufficient.
- Conserved quantities, reservoirs, and flows must remain bounded to the model and evidence.
- Synthetic evidence supports package inspection only and cannot authorize final learner claims.
