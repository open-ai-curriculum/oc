# M4 Failure Taxonomy

- `M4-FM-01` broken conservation boundary: learner omits key inputs, outputs, or reservoirs in a supposedly bounded model.
- `M4-FM-02` cycle-flow collapse: learner treats cyclical systems as one-way processes only.
- `M4-FM-03` matter-energy fusion: learner merges conserved matter and energy-flow accounts inappropriately.
- `M4-FM-04` cross-context model drift: learner cannot preserve the same modeling rules across different sciences.
