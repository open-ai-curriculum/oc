# E2 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. CER And Explanations

- state a claim
- choose supporting evidence
- compare stronger and weaker explanations

### Family B. Explanation

- explain why the selected evidence supports the stronger claim

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when evidence or reasoning does not match the claim

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across life, physical, and Earth/space investigations
