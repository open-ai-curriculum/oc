# E2 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `E2` status
- dependency posture for `E1` and `I2`
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner can build a claim-evidence-reasoning response and compare explanations
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who mismatches claims and evidence
- who cannot explain why evidence supports a claim
- who chooses weaker explanations over stronger evidence-based ones
