# C1 Intervention Playbook

- For `C1-FM-01`, use periodic-trend explanations that require causal rather than recall-only justification.
- For `C1-FM-02`, use model-versus-reality comparison prompts for atomic and quantum representations.
- For `C1-FM-03`, use energy-level tasks that connect arrangement to observable outcomes.
- For `C1-FM-04`, use bounded-case reviews that identify where atomic trends stop being sufficient by themselves.
