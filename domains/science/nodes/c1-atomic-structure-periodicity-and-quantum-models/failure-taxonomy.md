# C1 Failure Taxonomy

- `C1-FM-01` periodic-table recall only: learner uses periodic patterns without explaining why they matter.
- `C1-FM-02` orbital-picture literalism: learner treats model diagrams as direct physical pictures rather than explanatory models.
- `C1-FM-03` energy-level disconnect: learner cannot connect energy-level reasoning to chemical behavior.
- `C1-FM-04` atomic-property overgeneralization: learner extends a trend or model beyond its justified range.
