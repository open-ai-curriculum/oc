# A2 Item Bank Schema

Each item in the `A2` bank should include:

- `item_id`
- `node_id`
- `prompt`
- `task_type`
- `answer_key`
- `scorable_traits`
- `target_failure_modes`
- `context_tags`

Allowed `task_type` values:

- `capstone_design_justification`
- `computational_analysis_integration`
- `engineering_science_critique`
- `transfer_case`
