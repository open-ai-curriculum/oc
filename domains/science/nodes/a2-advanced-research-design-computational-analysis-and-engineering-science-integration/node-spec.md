# A2 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `A2`
- name: `advanced_research_design_computational_analysis_and_engineering_science_integration`
- domain: `science`
- support tier: `Incubation`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `science-secondary-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner designs and critiques advanced research or engineering-science work that integrates interdisciplinary modeling, computational analysis, and evidence-driven optimization.

## Dependencies

- `A1` interdisciplinary phenomenon modeling and scientific synthesis
- `D4` evidence-driven optimization under constraints

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `A2` only when all of the following are true:

1. designs or critiques a capstone investigation or engineering-science workflow using coordinated scientific strands
2. uses computational or quantitative analysis as part of the reasoning rather than as an isolated add-on
3. integrates optimization, evidence critique, and model coordination in one bounded account
4. succeeds across more than one unfamiliar capstone context
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `49`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- justify a research or engineering-science design with explicit scientific and methodological reasoning
- coordinate computational analysis with empirical evidence and model limits
- critique capstone-level plans for weak integration, poor optimization logic, or unsupported inference
- synthesize interdisciplinary science and engineering constraints in a bounded design argument

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
