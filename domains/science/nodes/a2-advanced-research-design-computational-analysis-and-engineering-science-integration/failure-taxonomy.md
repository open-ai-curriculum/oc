# A2 Failure Taxonomy

- `A2-FM-01` cosmetic computation use: learner includes computation or quantitative tooling without linking it to scientific inference.
- `A2-FM-02` science-design decoupling: learner treats engineering optimization and scientific validity as unrelated concerns.
- `A2-FM-03` capstone breadth-without-coherence: learner lists many domains or datasets without a coherent bounded integration strategy.
- `A2-FM-04` unsupported final integration claim: learner overstates the strength of a capstone design or research plan beyond its evidence and method.
