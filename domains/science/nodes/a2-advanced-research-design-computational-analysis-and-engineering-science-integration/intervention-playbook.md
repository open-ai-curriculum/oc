# A2 Intervention Playbook

- For `A2-FM-01`, use computation-to-claim mapping where each analysis step must justify its scientific role.
- For `A2-FM-02`, use paired validity-and-optimization reviews that check method and design logic together.
- For `A2-FM-03`, use capstone architecture templates that force explicit linkage across domain strands, evidence, and design choices.
- For `A2-FM-04`, use final-claim audits comparing plan strength against actual integration quality and evidence support.
