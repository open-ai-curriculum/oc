# A2 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`A2` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across advanced research-design, computational-analysis, and engineering-science integration tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains or critiques a capstone science-engineering workflow in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar capstone science-engineering context.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `49` days.

## Verification Notes

- Capstone integration requires explicit coordination of scientific models, methods, and design constraints.
- Computational reasoning must be meaningfully tied to the evidence and problem structure, not added cosmetically.
- Synthetic evidence supports package inspection only and cannot authorize final learner claims.
