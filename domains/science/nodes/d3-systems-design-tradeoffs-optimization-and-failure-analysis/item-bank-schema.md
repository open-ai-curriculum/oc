# D3 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Failure Tradeoffs And Optimization

- identify why a design failed at the system level
- recognize tradeoffs among design goals
- propose a system-aware optimization

### Family B. Explanation

- explain how a change affects system performance

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when an optimization ignores a key subsystem or tradeoff

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across transport, shelter, filtration, and other system-design contexts
