# B3 Failure Taxonomy

- `B3-FM-01` teleological-evolution return: learner explains population change as organisms changing because they need to.
- `B3-FM-02` selection-drift collapse: learner treats different evolutionary mechanisms as equivalent.
- `B3-FM-03` biodiversity-as-listing: learner catalogs diversity without explaining pattern-producing processes.
- `B3-FM-04` population-evidence weakness: learner makes evolutionary claims without enough population-level evidence.
