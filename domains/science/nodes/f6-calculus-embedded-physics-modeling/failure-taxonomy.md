# F6 Failure Taxonomy

- `F6-FM-01` calculus-procedure substitution: learner performs calculus steps without explaining the physics meaning of the quantities.
- `F6-FM-02` quantity-interpretation drift: learner loses track of what changing or accumulated quantities represent physically.
- `F6-FM-03` model-scope overreach: learner extends a successful mathematical model beyond the bounded physical case.
- `F6-FM-04` math-physics disconnect: learner cannot connect advanced symbolic work to system evidence or explanation.
