# G2 Intervention Playbook

- For `G2-FM-01`, use weather-versus-climate comparison routines with explicit timescale and evidence checks.
- For `G2-FM-02`, use atmospheric-system diagrams connecting circulation, moisture, pressure, and energy.
- For `G2-FM-03`, use regional-to-global claim audits that constrain inference scope.
- For `G2-FM-04`, use multi-source climate evidence synthesis tasks that require uncertainty language.
