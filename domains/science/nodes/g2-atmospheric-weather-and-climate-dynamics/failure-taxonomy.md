# G2 Failure Taxonomy

- `G2-FM-01` weather-climate conflation: learner treats short-term weather events as direct proof of climate claims.
- `G2-FM-02` atmospheric-factor isolation: learner explains atmospheric behavior with one factor while ignoring interacting system drivers.
- `G2-FM-03` regional-overgeneralization: learner extends local atmospheric evidence to global conclusions without justification.
- `G2-FM-04` climate-evidence selectivity: learner cherry-picks atmospheric evidence instead of weighing multiple sources and uncertainties.
