# T3 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`T3` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across climate, Earth-Sun-Moon, resource, and map-scale tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains a pattern or system relationship in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar Earth or space-system context.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `28` days.

## Verification Notes

- Single-event weather talk is insufficient evidence of climate reasoning.
- Map and model interpretations must preserve representation limits and scale.
- Synthetic evidence can support package inspection only and cannot authorize final claims.
