# T3 Failure Taxonomy

- `T3-FM-01` weather-climate collapse: learner treats short-term events as direct proof of long-term climate patterns.
- `T3-FM-02` Earth-space pattern mislink: learner names Sun, Earth, or Moon objects but does not correctly connect their relationships to observed patterns.
- `T3-FM-03` representation-scale literalism: learner treats map or model size and spacing as literal without using scale.
- `T3-FM-04` resource-claim overreach: learner makes unsupported claims about resource use or Earth-system consequences beyond the data.
