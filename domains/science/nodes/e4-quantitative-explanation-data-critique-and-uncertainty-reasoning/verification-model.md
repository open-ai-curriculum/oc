# E4 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`E4` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across quantitative explanation, data critique, and uncertainty reasoning tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains or critiques using quantitative evidence in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar scientific context.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `35` days.

## Verification Notes

- Correct numbers without coherent claim calibration are insufficient.
- Critique must reference evidence quality, uncertainty, or model adequacy rather than preference.
- Synthetic evidence supports structural testing only and cannot authorize final learner claims.
