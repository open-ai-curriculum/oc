# E4 Failure Taxonomy

- `E4-FM-01` number-as-proof collapse: learner treats quantitative output as automatically self-justifying.
- `E4-FM-02` evidence-weight blindness: learner fails to distinguish stronger from weaker quantitative evidence sources.
- `E4-FM-03` uncertainty nihilism or denial: learner either dismisses all inference under uncertainty or ignores uncertainty entirely.
- `E4-FM-04` explanation-critique disconnect: learner critiques data quality but does not revise the explanation accordingly.
