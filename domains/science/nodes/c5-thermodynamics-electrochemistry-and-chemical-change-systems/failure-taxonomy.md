# C5 Failure Taxonomy

- `C5-FM-01` thermo-label shortcut: learner uses energy labels without system-feasibility reasoning.
- `C5-FM-02` redox-role confusion: learner confuses oxidation and reduction or the direction of electron transfer.
- `C5-FM-03` electrochemical-diagram memorization: learner recalls parts of a cell without explaining how they work together.
- `C5-FM-04` measurement-energy disconnect: learner cannot connect electrochemical measurements to chemical-system meaning.
