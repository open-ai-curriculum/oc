# R3 Failure Taxonomy

- `R3-FM-01` black-box instrumentation trust: learner accepts device output without explaining how the system works or is calibrated.
- `R3-FM-02` signal-noise neglect: learner ignores noise, drift, or measurement-quality issues in interpretation.
- `R3-FM-03` sensor-system disconnect: learner uses sensors without connecting them to the physical quantity and circuit model.
- `R3-FM-04` setup-validity overclaim: learner overstates the validity of a measurement setup without sufficient checks.
