# L4 Failure Taxonomy

- `L4-FM-01` ecological scale collapse: learner confuses organism, population, community, and ecosystem levels.
- `L4-FM-02` single-link ecosystem reasoning: learner reduces ecosystem change to one isolated interaction only.
- `L4-FM-03` matter-energy conflation: learner describes matter and energy as moving identically through living systems.
- `L4-FM-04` unsupported ecosystem certainty: learner overstates claims beyond the available model or evidence.
