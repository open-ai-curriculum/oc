# L4 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`L4` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across population, ecosystem, and matter-energy flow tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains an ecosystem interaction or matter-energy pathway in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar ecosystem case.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `28` days.

## Verification Notes

- Simple food-chain recall without system reasoning is insufficient.
- Matter and energy claims must remain instructionally bounded and model-based.
- Synthetic evidence can support package inspection only and cannot authorize final learner-impacting claims.
