# L4 Learner State Model

The learner-state for `L4` tracks:

- dependency security on `L2`, `M3`, and `E3`
- direct accuracy on ecological scale, interaction, and matter-energy tasks
- explanation quality for ecosystem and population reasoning
- transfer performance in unfamiliar ecosystem contexts
- delayed retention outcome

`secure` requires all verification gates to pass. Any delayed failure or dependency instability should move the learner to `unstable`.
