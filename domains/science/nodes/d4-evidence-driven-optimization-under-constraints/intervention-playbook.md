# D4 Intervention Playbook

- For `D4-FM-01`, use criteria-and-constraint scoring routines where a high single score cannot override failed requirements.
- For `D4-FM-02`, use uncertainty-aware ranking tasks that require justification before declaring a design superior.
- For `D4-FM-03`, use forced tradeoff tables listing likely gains and losses for each revision.
- For `D4-FM-04`, use design-problem alignment checks where each piece of evidence must map to a specific criterion or constraint.
