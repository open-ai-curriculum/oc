# D4 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`D4` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across optimization, tradeoff, and evidence-based design-justification tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains design tradeoffs or optimization logic in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar advanced engineering context.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `42` days.

## Verification Notes

- Preference statements without explicit criteria and constraints are insufficient.
- Design justification must reflect evidence quality, uncertainty, and competing priorities.
- Synthetic evidence supports structural testing only and cannot authorize final learner claims.
