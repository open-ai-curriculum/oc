# D4 Failure Taxonomy

- `D4-FM-01` single-metric optimization error: learner treats one measure as sufficient despite other criteria or constraints.
- `D4-FM-02` uncertainty-blind design claim: learner ignores uncertainty or test quality when ranking design options.
- `D4-FM-03` tradeoff flattening: learner frames design improvement as purely positive without acknowledging costs elsewhere.
- `D4-FM-04` evidence-constraint disconnect: learner cites evidence but does not tie it back to the actual design problem structure.
