# T2 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Surface Patterns And Water Movement

- identify an Earth-surface change
- recognize a sun or moon pattern
- describe simple water movement

### Family B. Explanation

- explain what observation supports the Earth/space claim

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when an Earth/space explanation goes beyond the available evidence

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across local landforms, sky observations, and water-system examples
