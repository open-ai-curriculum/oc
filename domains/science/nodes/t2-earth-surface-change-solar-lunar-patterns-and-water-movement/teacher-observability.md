# T2 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `T2` status
- dependency posture for `T1` and `I1`
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner can explain local Earth-systems patterns and changes using evidence
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who misreads Earth-surface change
- who misses recurring sun or moon patterns
- who cannot explain simple water movement
