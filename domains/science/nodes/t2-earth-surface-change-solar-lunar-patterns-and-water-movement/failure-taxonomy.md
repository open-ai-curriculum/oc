# T2 Failure Taxonomy

### T2-FM-01 Surface-Change Misread

- description: learner cannot reliably identify a visible Earth-surface change or process

### T2-FM-02 Sky-Pattern Instability

- description: learner misses or misstates recurring solar or lunar patterns

### T2-FM-03 Water-Movement Disconnect

- description: learner cannot explain simple water movement using direct evidence

### T2-FM-04 Hidden-Cause Or Fantasy Drift

- description: learner explains Earth/space patterns using unsupported hidden causes, personification, or fantasy
