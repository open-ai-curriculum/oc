# T2 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `T2`
- name: `earth_surface_change_solar_lunar_patterns_and_water_movement`
- domain: `science`
- support tier: `Incubation`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `science-bridge-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner explains visible changes to Earth's surface, recognizes recurring solar or lunar patterns, and describes simple water movement through familiar Earth systems using direct evidence.

## Dependencies

- `T1` weather, sky patterns, and local Earth materials
- `I1` fair tests, tool use, and measurement routines

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `T2` only when all of the following are true:

1. identifies a visible Earth-surface change or pattern over time
2. recognizes a recurring sun or moon pattern
3. describes a simple water movement process in a familiar setting
4. succeeds across more than one Earth/space context
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `21`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- describe how land or soil changed after wind, water, or repeated conditions
- recognize repeating sun or moon observations
- explain simple water movement such as flowing, collecting, or soaking in
- use records or observations rather than unsupported hidden causes

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
