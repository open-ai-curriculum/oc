# B2 Intervention Playbook

- For `B2-FM-01`, use gene-expression flow maps that force explicit intermediate reasoning from DNA to trait.
- For `B2-FM-02`, use genotype-phenotype-environment comparison tables to separate causal roles.
- For `B2-FM-03`, use inheritance-case sorts that distinguish transmission patterns from molecular explanation.
- For `B2-FM-04`, use biotechnology evidence reviews that focus on method limits and claim scope.
