# B2 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `B2`
- name: `gene_expression_inheritance_and_biotechnology`
- domain: `science`
- support tier: `Incubation`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `science-secondary-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner explains how information flows from genetic material to expressed traits and evaluates biotechnology methods without reducing inheritance to deterministic trait slogans.

## Dependencies

- `B1`
- `L5`
- `I4`

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `B2` only when all of the following are true:

1. explains gene-to-trait pathways with bounded molecular and regulatory reasoning
2. distinguishes inheritance patterns from gene expression and environmental influence
3. evaluates biotechnology claims using method and evidence quality
4. succeeds across more than one heredity or biotechnology context
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `42`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- trace information flow from DNA through expression to trait outcomes
- compare genotype, phenotype, regulation, and environment without role collapse
- evaluate biotechnology methods such as editing, amplification, or screening with evidence awareness
- avoid deterministic overclaiming about gene-trait relations

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
