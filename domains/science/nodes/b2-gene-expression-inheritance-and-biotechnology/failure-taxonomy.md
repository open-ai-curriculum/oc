# B2 Failure Taxonomy

- `B2-FM-01` dna-to-trait shortcut: learner skips expression and regulation steps when explaining inherited traits.
- `B2-FM-02` gene-determinism overreach: learner treats genes as fully determining outcomes without environmental or regulatory context.
- `B2-FM-03` inheritance-pattern confusion: learner confuses trait transmission patterns with molecular mechanisms.
- `B2-FM-04` biotech-method credibility drift: learner accepts biotechnology claims without method or evidence critique.
