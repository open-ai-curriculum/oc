# E1 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Claim And Evidence

- state what happened
- choose relevant evidence
- link a claim to an observation

### Family B. Explanation

- explain why the named evidence supports the claim

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when a claim is unsupported or uses the wrong evidence

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across plant, motion, weather, and materials contexts
