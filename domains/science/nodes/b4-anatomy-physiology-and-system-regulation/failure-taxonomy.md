# B4 Failure Taxonomy

- `B4-FM-01` organ-fact fragmentation: learner lists organs or structures without explaining coordinated function.
- `B4-FM-02` feedback-loop instability: learner cannot explain negative or positive feedback coherently.
- `B4-FM-03` static-homeostasis claim: learner treats regulation as keeping systems unchanged rather than dynamically responsive.
- `B4-FM-04` system-interaction omission: learner explains one body system while ignoring necessary cross-system interactions.
