# L2 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `L2` status
- dependency posture for `L1` and `M1`
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner explains life cycles, shared traits, and ecosystem relationships coherently
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who misorders life-cycle stages
- who confuses shared traits
- who cannot explain simple ecosystem relationships
