# L2 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `L2`
- name: `life_cycles_inheritance_and_ecosystem_relationships`
- domain: `science`
- support tier: `Incubation`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `science-bridge-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner explains that organisms change through life cycles, that offspring share some traits with parents, and that organisms relate to one another and their environments within simple ecosystems.

## Dependencies

- `L1` organisms, needs, traits, and habitats
- `M1` part-whole structure, function, and simple system models

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `L2` only when all of the following are true:

1. identifies stages or ordered changes in a life cycle
2. recognizes that some traits are shared across parent-offspring pairs
3. describes a simple organism-environment or organism-organism relationship
4. succeeds across more than one organism or ecosystem context
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `21`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- order stages of a familiar life cycle
- compare shared and differing observable traits
- explain a simple ecosystem relationship such as food, shelter, or pollination support
- use simple system language to connect living things and environments

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
