# L2 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Life Cycles Traits And Relationships

- order life-cycle stages
- identify shared traits
- describe a simple ecosystem relationship

### Family B. Explanation

- explain how a stage trait or relationship fits the living system

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when a life-cycle, trait, or ecosystem claim is unsupported

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across plants, insects, amphibians, birds, and simple local ecosystems
