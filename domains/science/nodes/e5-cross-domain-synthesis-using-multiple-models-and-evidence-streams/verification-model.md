# E5 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`E5` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across cross-domain synthesis, multi-model comparison, and evidence-integration tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains or critiques an integrated account using multiple evidence streams in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar interdisciplinary synthesis context.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `42` days.

## Verification Notes

- Surface-level interdisciplinarity without disciplined model coordination is insufficient.
- Integrated explanations must preserve uncertainty, model limits, and evidence scope.
- Synthetic evidence supports structural testing only and cannot authorize final learner claims.
