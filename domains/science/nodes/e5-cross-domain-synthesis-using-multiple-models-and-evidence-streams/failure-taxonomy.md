# E5 Failure Taxonomy

- `E5-FM-01` single-stream synthesis failure: learner claims synthesis while relying on one evidence source only.
- `E5-FM-02` model-role blur: learner combines models without stating what each contributes or where each stops.
- `E5-FM-03` vocabulary-stack pseudo-integration: learner piles up disciplinary language without evidence-backed connections.
- `E5-FM-04` cross-domain overclaim: learner extends integrated claims beyond what the coordinated evidence supports.
