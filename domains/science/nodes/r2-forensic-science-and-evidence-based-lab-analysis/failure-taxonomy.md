# R2 Failure Taxonomy

- `R2-FM-01` technical-certainty illusion: learner assumes a forensic result is certain because the method sounds technical.
- `R2-FM-02` case-narrative override: learner lets the story of the case outweigh the evidentiary limits.
- `R2-FM-03` contamination-method neglect: learner underweights sample handling or method quality in interpretation.
- `R2-FM-04` evidence-to-identity overreach: learner extends bounded lab evidence to stronger identity or causation claims than justified.
