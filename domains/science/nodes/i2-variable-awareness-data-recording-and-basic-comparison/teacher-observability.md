# I2 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `I2` status
- dependency posture for `I1` and `Q3`
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner tracks changed factors and compares recorded outcomes correctly
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who confuses what changed
- who records data inconsistently
- who cannot compare results accurately
