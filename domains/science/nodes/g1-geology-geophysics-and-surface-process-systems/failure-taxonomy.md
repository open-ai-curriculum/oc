# G1 Failure Taxonomy

- `G1-FM-01` landform-label substitution: learner names rocks or landforms without explaining the processes that produced them.
- `G1-FM-02` timescale collapse: learner confuses short-term events with long geologic-system change.
- `G1-FM-03` tectonic-process fragmentation: learner discusses plate or crust processes as disconnected facts.
- `G1-FM-04` evidence-to-process disconnect: learner cannot connect geologic evidence to a justified Earth-process explanation.
