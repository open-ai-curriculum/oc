# G1 Intervention Playbook

- For `G1-FM-01`, use landform-to-process routines that require causal Earth-system explanation.
- For `G1-FM-02`, use timescale comparison tasks that separate event, cycle, and deep-time claims.
- For `G1-FM-03`, use tectonic-system maps tying boundaries, forces, and surface outcomes together.
- For `G1-FM-04`, use rock-and-material evidence reviews that connect observations to bounded geologic interpretations.
