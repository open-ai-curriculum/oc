# P6 Failure Taxonomy

- `P6-FM-01` macro-micro disconnect: learner cannot link observable physical behavior to relevant microscopic models.
- `P6-FM-02` chemistry-physics vocabulary stack: learner mimics integration by combining terms without model-role clarity.
- `P6-FM-03` weak quantitative critique: learner critiques numbers vaguely without identifying the violated relationship or assumption.
- `P6-FM-04` multiscale overreach: learner extends cross-scale physical claims beyond what the evidence and models support.
