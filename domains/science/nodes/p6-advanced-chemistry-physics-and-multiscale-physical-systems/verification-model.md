# P6 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`P6` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across advanced chemistry, physics, and multiscale physical-system tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains multiscale physical interaction in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar advanced physical-systems context.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `42` days.

## Verification Notes

- Physical-systems synthesis must preserve scale and model-role clarity.
- Quantitative critique must remain tied to evidence quality and model adequacy.
- Synthetic evidence supports package inspection only and cannot authorize final learner claims.
