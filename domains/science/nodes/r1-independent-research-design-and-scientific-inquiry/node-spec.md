# R1 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `R1`
- name: `independent_research_design_and_scientific_inquiry`
- domain: `science`
- support tier: `Incubation`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `science-secondary-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner proposes and executes sustained scientific inquiry with defended methods, evidence, and interpretation without confusing project completion with credible scientific reasoning.

## Dependencies

- `A2`
- `I5`
- `E5`

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `R1` only when all of the following are true:

1. frames a defensible research question with bounded scope and method choice
2. uses evidence and uncertainty posture to justify interpretation and critique limitations
3. coordinates investigation design, data handling, and explanation in a sustained inquiry cycle
4. succeeds across more than one advanced inquiry or research-design context
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `42`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- justify question scope, variables, and method decisions explicitly
- evaluate data quality, uncertainty, and limitation claims in research contexts
- connect research design decisions to interpretation quality
- avoid equating polished presentation with strong inquiry logic

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
