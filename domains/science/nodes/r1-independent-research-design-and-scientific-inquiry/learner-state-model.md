# R1 Learner State Model

The learner-state for `R1` tracks:

- dependency security on `A2`, `I5`, `E5`
- direct accuracy on independent research design and scientific inquiry tasks
- explanation quality for bounded applied science specialization reasoning
- transfer performance across unfamiliar advanced science contexts
- delayed retention outcome

`secure` requires all verification gates to pass. Any delayed failure or dependency instability should move the learner to `unstable`.
