# R1 Failure Taxonomy

- `R1-FM-01` question-method mismatch: learner poses a question that is not matched by the chosen method or evidence plan.
- `R1-FM-02` project-completion masquerade: learner treats finishing a project as evidence of scientific quality.
- `R1-FM-03` limitation-understatement: learner reports findings without adequately addressing limitations or uncertainty.
- `R1-FM-04` interpretation-evidence drift: learner's interpretation extends beyond what the evidence supports.
