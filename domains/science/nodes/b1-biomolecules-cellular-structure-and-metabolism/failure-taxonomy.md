# B1 Failure Taxonomy

- `B1-FM-01` cell-part list substitution: learner names cell structures without explaining their coordinated functional roles.
- `B1-FM-02` membrane-process collapse: learner confuses diffusion, active transport, or membrane selectivity as interchangeable.
- `B1-FM-03` metabolism-as-purpose drift: learner explains metabolic processes teleologically instead of by constrained energy transformation.
- `B1-FM-04` molecule-to-cell disconnect: learner cannot connect biomolecule properties to cellular behavior.
