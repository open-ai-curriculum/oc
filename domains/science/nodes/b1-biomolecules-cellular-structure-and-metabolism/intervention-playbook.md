# B1 Intervention Playbook

- For `B1-FM-01`, use structure-to-function comparison routines that force explicit biomolecule and organelle role explanations.
- For `B1-FM-02`, use membrane transport scenario sorts that separate passive, active, and selective processes.
- For `B1-FM-03`, use energy pathway maps that connect inputs, transformations, and outputs in respiration or photosynthesis.
- For `B1-FM-04`, use cross-scale prompts linking molecular properties to whole-cell performance.
