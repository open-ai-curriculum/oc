# P5 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`P5` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across field, atomic-interaction, and quantitative-conservation tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains a quantitative physical model in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar advanced physical-science context.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `42` days.

## Verification Notes

- Physical-science explanations must link quantitative reasoning to the actual model used.
- Atomic and field models must remain bounded to evidence and representational limits.
- Synthetic evidence supports package inspection only and cannot authorize final learner claims.
