# P5 Failure Taxonomy

- `P5-FM-01` contact-only interaction collapse: learner rejects or mishandles field-based interaction models.
- `P5-FM-02` naive atomic miniature model: learner treats atomic interactions as literal visible-object stories without model discipline.
- `P5-FM-03` quantitative conservation breach: learner accepts explanations that violate conservation within the stated system.
- `P5-FM-04` math-without-model drift: learner manipulates quantities without connecting them to physical meaning or model structure.
