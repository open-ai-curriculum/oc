# T4 Learner State Model

The learner-state for `T4` tracks:

- dependency security on `T3`, `M2`, and `E3`
- direct accuracy on plate-process, water-cycle, and space-system interaction tasks
- explanation quality for geoscience and space-system reasoning
- transfer performance in unfamiliar Earth-space contexts
- delayed retention outcome

`secure` requires all verification gates to pass. Any delayed failure or dependency instability should move the learner to `unstable`.
