# T4 Failure Taxonomy

- `T4-FM-01` surface-feature naming only: learner names landforms or Earth features without explaining the generating process.
- `T4-FM-02` loop-diagram water cycle collapse: learner treats the water cycle as a fixed picture instead of a system of moving water among reservoirs.
- `T4-FM-03` single-object space explanation: learner ignores system interaction and attributes the event to one object alone.
- `T4-FM-04` unsupported Earth-system claim: learner overgeneralizes beyond the model or available evidence.
