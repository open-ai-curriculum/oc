# T4 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`T4` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across plate-process, water-cycle, and space-system interaction tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains a mechanism or system interaction in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar Earth-space system context.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `28` days.

## Verification Notes

- Naming landforms or water-cycle terms without process explanation is insufficient.
- System accounts must remain bounded to the given model and evidence.
- Synthetic evidence supports structural testing only and cannot stand in for human instructional approval.
