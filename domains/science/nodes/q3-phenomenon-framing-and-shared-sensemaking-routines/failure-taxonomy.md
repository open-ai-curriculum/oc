# Q3 Failure Taxonomy

### Q3-FM-01 Phenomenon Blur

- description: learner cannot identify the focal phenomenon or what is being figured out

### Q3-FM-02 Topic Drift

- description: learner contributes ideas that do not stay anchored to the focal science question

### Q3-FM-03 Relevance Misjudgment

- description: learner cannot distinguish relevant evidence or ideas from irrelevant contributions

### Q3-FM-04 Context Boundary

- description: learner can frame one familiar discussion but not a changed science context
