# Q3 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `Q3` status
- dependency posture for `Q2`
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner stays on the focal phenomenon during science talk
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who loses the focal question
- who contributes off-topic ideas
- who cannot judge which observations are relevant to the shared investigation
