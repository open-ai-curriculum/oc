# Q3 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Phenomenon Framing

- identify the focal phenomenon
- state what is being figured out
- keep a contribution on topic

### Family B. Explanation

- explain why an idea or observation fits the shared frame

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when a contribution drifts off the focal phenomenon

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across life, physical, and Earth/space science discussions
