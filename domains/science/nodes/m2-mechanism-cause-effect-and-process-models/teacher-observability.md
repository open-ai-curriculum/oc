# M2 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `M2` status
- dependency posture for `M1` and `E1`
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner explains processes through mechanism rather than surface labels alone
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who cannot sustain process steps
- who reverses cause and effect
- who names results without explaining the mechanism
