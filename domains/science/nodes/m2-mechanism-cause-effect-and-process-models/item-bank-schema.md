# M2 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Mechanisms Processes And Cause-Effect

- identify what causes what
- order process steps
- choose or interpret a simple mechanism model

### Family B. Explanation

- explain how the process works step by step

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when a process explanation skips the mechanism or reverses cause and effect

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across life, physical, and Earth/space processes
