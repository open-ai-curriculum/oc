# A1 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`A1` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across interdisciplinary phenomenon modeling and synthesis tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains an integrated phenomenon using coordinated domain strands in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar integrated science phenomenon.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `49` days.

## Verification Notes

- Real interdisciplinary synthesis requires explicit coordination of models and evidence, not broad thematic language alone.
- Integrated phenomenon explanations must preserve the distinct role and limit of each domain strand.
- Synthetic evidence supports package inspection only and cannot authorize final learner claims.
