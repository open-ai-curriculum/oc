# G6 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `G6`
- name: `land_water_use_agriculture_and_resource_management`
- domain: `science`
- support tier: `Incubation`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `science-secondary-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner applies environmental and biological reasoning to agriculture, land use, water use, and resource decisions without treating management questions as disconnected from system evidence and tradeoffs.

## Dependencies

- `G5`
- `B5`
- `D4`

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `G6` only when all of the following are true:

1. explains land, water, and agricultural decisions through environmental-system reasoning
2. evaluates resource-management options with criteria, constraints, and tradeoff evidence
3. connects biological productivity, water, soil, and human-use factors in bounded cases
4. succeeds across more than one advanced agriculture or resource context
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `42`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- connect agricultural and resource decisions to ecological and Earth-system constraints
- use design and management criteria explicitly when comparing options
- reason about land and water use with system-wide consequences in view
- avoid local-solution claims that ignore downstream or long-term effects

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
