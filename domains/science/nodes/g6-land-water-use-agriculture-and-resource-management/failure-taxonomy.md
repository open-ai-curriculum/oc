# G6 Failure Taxonomy

- `G6-FM-01` resource-use short-horizon: learner evaluates land or water use without long-term system consequences.
- `G6-FM-02` agriculture-system isolation: learner treats agriculture as detached from ecology, soil, water, or climate drivers.
- `G6-FM-03` management-criteria omission: learner compares options without explicit constraints or decision criteria.
- `G6-FM-04` downstream-impact blindness: learner ignores cross-system or downstream effects of a proposed management choice.
