# G6 Intervention Playbook

- For `G6-FM-01`, use resource-planning tasks that force long-term and downstream consequence checks.
- For `G6-FM-02`, use agriculture-system maps connecting soil, water, biodiversity, and production goals.
- For `G6-FM-03`, use criteria-and-constraint tables for land and water management decisions.
- For `G6-FM-04`, use case reviews that compare local gains with broader system impacts.
