# F3 Failure Taxonomy

- `F3-FM-01` temperature-energy collapse: learner treats temperature and energy transfer as interchangeable.
- `F3-FM-02` pressure-flow shortcut: learner uses pressure or flow labels without system-level explanation.
- `F3-FM-03` thermal-state oversimplification: learner ignores important state or process conditions in thermal reasoning.
- `F3-FM-04` fluid-thermal context drift: learner applies one fluid or thermal relation outside its justified context.
