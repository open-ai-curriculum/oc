# F1 Failure Taxonomy

- `F1-FM-01` equation-cue substitution: learner selects formulas from surface cues without a coherent mechanics model.
- `F1-FM-02` force-energy conflation: learner merges force, work, and energy reasoning without distinction.
- `F1-FM-03` momentum-scope confusion: learner misapplies momentum ideas outside the bounded interaction context.
- `F1-FM-04` representation-fragmentation: learner cannot coordinate graphs, measurements, and symbolic reasoning.
