# F1 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `F1`
- name: `mechanics_kinematics_energy_and_momentum`
- domain: `science`
- support tier: `Incubation`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `science-secondary-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner models motion and interaction using force, energy, and momentum relationships without reducing mechanics to equation selection by surface cue only.

## Dependencies

- `P4`
- `M4`
- `I4`

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `F1` only when all of the following are true:

1. explains motion using kinematic, force, energy, and momentum models in bounded cases
2. distinguishes when force, energy, or momentum reasoning is the better explanatory tool
3. connects representations, measurements, and model assumptions in mechanics tasks
4. succeeds across more than one advanced mechanics context
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `42`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- coordinate verbal, graphical, and mathematical mechanics representations
- explain why a given mechanics model applies in the bounded case
- distinguish interaction reasoning from formula recall
- use evidence from motion or collision data without overclaiming precision

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
