# L6 Failure Taxonomy

- `L6-FM-01` scale-isolation error: learner explains biological phenomena at one scale only when multi-scale integration is needed.
- `L6-FM-02` single-cause ecology collapse: learner reduces complex ecological dynamics to one interaction or variable.
- `L6-FM-03` selective biological evidence use: learner cherry-picks biological evidence streams instead of integrating or critiquing them.
- `L6-FM-04` systems-integration overreach: learner asserts cross-scale integration without enough evidence or model support.
