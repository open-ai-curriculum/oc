# L6 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `L6`
- name: `molecular_biology_complex_ecology_and_biological_systems_integration`
- domain: `science`
- support tier: `Incubation`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `science-secondary-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner integrates molecular, organismal, and ecological reasoning across biological systems without collapsing scale, causation, or evidence boundaries.

## Dependencies

- `L5` genetics, evolution, and homeostasis models
- `E4` quantitative explanation, data critique, and uncertainty reasoning
- `I5` quantitative investigation design, uncertainty, and method critique

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `L6` only when all of the following are true:

1. coordinates molecular and systems-level biological explanations in bounded cases
2. explains complex ecological interactions without reducing them to single-cause stories
3. uses quantitative and evidentiary critique when integrating biological evidence streams
4. succeeds across more than one advanced biological integration context
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `42`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- connect gene expression, cellular process, organism function, and ecological consequence when the evidence supports it
- critique oversimplified biological explanations that ignore scale or interaction complexity
- integrate observational, experimental, and quantitative biological evidence
- preserve model limits while synthesizing across biological levels

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
