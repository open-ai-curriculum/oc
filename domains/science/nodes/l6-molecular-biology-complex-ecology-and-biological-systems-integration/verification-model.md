# L6 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`L6` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across molecular biology, complex ecology, and biological-systems integration tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains multiscale biological interaction in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar advanced biological integration context.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `42` days.

## Verification Notes

- Cross-scale biological synthesis must remain evidence-backed and bounded.
- Complexity language without explicit interaction structure is insufficient.
- Synthetic evidence supports package inspection only and cannot authorize final learner claims.
