# L6 Learner State Model

The learner-state for `L6` tracks:

- dependency security on `L5`, `E4`, and `I5`
- direct accuracy on multiscale biology, complex ecology, and evidence-integration tasks
- explanation quality for biological systems synthesis
- transfer performance across unfamiliar advanced biological contexts
- delayed retention outcome

`secure` requires all verification gates to pass. Any delayed failure or dependency instability should move the learner to `unstable`.
