# T1 Failure Taxonomy

### T1-FM-01 Weather Observation Instability

- description: learner cannot reliably describe direct weather or sky observations

### T1-FM-02 Pattern-Over-Time Breakdown

- description: learner misses or misstates recurring weather or sky patterns across observations

### T1-FM-03 Earth-Material Property Confusion

- description: learner cannot reliably compare local Earth materials by observable properties

### T1-FM-04 Hidden-Cause Or Fantasy Drift

- description: learner explains weather, sky, or Earth-material observations using unsupported hidden causes, personification, or fantasy
