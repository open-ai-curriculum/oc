# T1 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Weather Sky And Materials

- identify a direct weather observation
- describe a sky pattern
- compare local Earth materials by observable properties

### Family B. Explanation

- explain how an observation supports a weather, sky, or material claim

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when a weather or sky explanation goes beyond the available evidence

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across different days, seasons, neighborhoods, and familiar local materials
