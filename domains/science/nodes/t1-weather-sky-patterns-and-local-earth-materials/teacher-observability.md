# T1 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `T1` status
- dependency posture for `Q1` and `E1`
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner grounds weather, sky, and Earth-material claims in observation
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who cannot describe direct weather or sky observations
- who misses recurring patterns over time
- who confuses local Earth materials or relies on fantasy explanations
