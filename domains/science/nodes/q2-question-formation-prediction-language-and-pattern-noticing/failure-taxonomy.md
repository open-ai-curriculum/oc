# Q2 Failure Taxonomy

### Q2-FM-01 Uncheckable Questioning

- description: learner asks a question that cannot be answered through observation or simple testing

### Q2-FM-02 Prediction Without Basis

- description: learner makes a prediction that is disconnected from the observed setup or pattern

### Q2-FM-03 Pattern Blindness

- description: learner misses or misstates a recurring visible pattern

### Q2-FM-04 Topic Drift

- description: learner shifts away from the focal phenomenon and asks about a different issue
