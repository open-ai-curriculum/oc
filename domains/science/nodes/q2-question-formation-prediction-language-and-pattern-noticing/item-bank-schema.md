# Q2 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Question And Prediction

- ask a bounded question
- choose or state a simple prediction
- identify what can be checked

### Family B. Explanation

- explain why the question or prediction fits the observation

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when a question is off-topic or uncheckable

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across classroom materials, organism contexts, and weather or motion examples
