# Q2 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `Q2` status
- dependency posture for `Q1`
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner asks checkable questions and makes evidence-based predictions
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who asks uncheckable questions
- who predicts without visible evidence
- who notices patterns only in one familiar context
