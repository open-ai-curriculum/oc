# D2 Teacher Observability View

Teachers should see:

- current `D2` status
- dependency posture for `D1` and `I2`
- direct accuracy
- explanation, transfer, and retention posture
- dominant failure mode
- whether the learner uses test evidence to redesign and record tradeoffs
- recommended intervention

Class-level view should highlight:

- who misreads test results
- who redesigns without using evidence
- who misses basic tradeoffs or constraint conflicts
