# D2 Item Bank Schema

## Required Item Families

### Family A. Testing Redesign And Tradeoffs

- identify what a test shows
- choose a useful redesign
- note a simple tradeoff

### Family B. Explanation

- explain why a redesign fits the test evidence

### Family C. Error Diagnosis

- identify when a redesign ignores the test result or tradeoff

### Family D. Transfer

- reason across shelter, bridge, boat, and barrier design contexts
