# B6 Failure Taxonomy

- `B6-FM-01` trait-catalog substitution: learner lists organism traits without explaining their system role.
- `B6-FM-02` habitat-disconnect reasoning: learner ignores environmental constraints when explaining organism function.
- `B6-FM-03` marine-system isolation: learner treats marine biology as detached from Earth-system processes.
- `B6-FM-04` comparative-overgeneralization: learner overextends one organism case to unrelated groups without evidence.
