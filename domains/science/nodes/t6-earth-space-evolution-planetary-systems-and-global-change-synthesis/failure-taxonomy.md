# T6 Failure Taxonomy

- `T6-FM-01` single-stream planetary explanation: learner ignores relevant Earth-space evidence streams in synthesis.
- `T6-FM-02` oversimplified global-change narrative: learner reduces global change to one trend or cause without system and uncertainty control.
- `T6-FM-03` vague Earth-space critique: learner critiques models generically without identifying the actual evidentiary or modeling flaw.
- `T6-FM-04` synthesis overreach across scales: learner extends Earth-space claims beyond what the coordinated evidence supports.
