# T6 Verification Model

## Verification Gates

`T6` requires all of the following before `secure` status can be assigned:

1. Direct performance gate: learner reaches at least `0.90` accuracy across Earth-space evolution, planetary-system, and global-change synthesis tasks.
2. Meaning gate: learner explains or critiques planetary or global-change synthesis in at least `2` of `3` prompts.
3. Transfer gate: learner succeeds in at least one unfamiliar advanced Earth-space synthesis context.
4. Retention gate: learner reaches at least `0.85` accuracy on a delayed recheck after about `42` days.

## Verification Notes

- Integration across Earth and space evidence streams must remain bounded and evidence-backed.
- Global-change claims must preserve uncertainty, timescale, and model-role discipline.
- Synthetic evidence supports package inspection only and cannot authorize final learner claims.
