# G3 Node Specification

## Node Identity

- id: `G3`
- name: `hydrology_oceanography_and_marine_earth_systems`
- domain: `science`
- support tier: `Incubation`
- current maturity state: `Drafted`
- supported profile: `science-secondary-node-package`
- evidence class: `Synthetic`

## Purpose

The learner models water movement, ocean systems, and coastal or marine Earth interactions without fragmenting hydrology from climate and Earth-system processes.

## Dependencies

- `G1`
- `G2`
- `T6`

## Exit Conditions

The learner is secure on `G3` only when all of the following are true:

1. explains hydrologic and ocean processes through interacting Earth-system models
2. connects water movement to climate, geology, and marine-system outcomes in bounded cases
3. uses marine and hydrologic evidence without overextending local observations
4. succeeds across more than one advanced oceanography or hydrology context
5. passes a delayed recheck after spacing

## Mastery Criteria

- direct accuracy threshold: `0.90`
- explanation required: `true`
- transfer required: `true`
- retention check days: `42`

## Observable Evidence

The learner can:

- connect water pathways, storage, and movement to system conditions
- relate coastal or marine outcomes to atmospheric and geologic drivers
- use observational evidence with explicit spatial and temporal bounds
- avoid treating ocean or water systems as isolated from broader Earth processes

## Claim Boundary

This node package is an operational draft and not a classroom-release claim.
