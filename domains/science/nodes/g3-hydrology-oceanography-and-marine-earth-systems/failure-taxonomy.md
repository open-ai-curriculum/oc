# G3 Failure Taxonomy

- `G3-FM-01` water-cycle simplification: learner uses an oversimplified cycle story without system drivers or storage distinctions.
- `G3-FM-02` marine-system isolation: learner treats ocean processes as detached from climate, geology, or coastlines.
- `G3-FM-03` local-observation overreach: learner generalizes from one water or coastal observation beyond its justified scale.
- `G3-FM-04` hydrology-climate disconnect: learner cannot connect hydrologic behavior to atmospheric or Earth-system conditions.
