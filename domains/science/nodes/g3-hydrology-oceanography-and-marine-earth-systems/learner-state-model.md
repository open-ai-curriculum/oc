# G3 Learner State Model

The learner-state for `G3` tracks:

- dependency security on `G1`, `G2`, `T6`
- direct accuracy on hydrology, oceanography, and marine earth systems tasks
- explanation quality for bounded earth and environmental specialization reasoning
- transfer performance across unfamiliar advanced science contexts
- delayed retention outcome

`secure` requires all verification gates to pass. Any delayed failure or dependency instability should move the learner to `unstable`.
