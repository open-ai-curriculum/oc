# G3 Intervention Playbook

- For `G3-FM-01`, use water-system maps that include storage, transfer, and driver conditions.
- For `G3-FM-02`, use marine Earth-system comparisons linking ocean, atmosphere, and geology.
- For `G3-FM-03`, use scale-bounding prompts for local, regional, and global water evidence.
- For `G3-FM-04`, use hydrology-climate integration tasks requiring cross-system explanation.
