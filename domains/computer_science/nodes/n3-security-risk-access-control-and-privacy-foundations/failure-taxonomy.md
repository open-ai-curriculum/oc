# N3 Failure Taxonomy

- `N3-FM-01` security-rule parroting: learner repeats a rule without explaining what risk it addresses.
- `N3-FM-02` access-boundary confusion: learner cannot distinguish authorized from unauthorized access in a bounded case.
- `N3-FM-03` privacy-public collapse: learner treats shared digital spaces as if all information should be openly accessible.
- `N3-FM-04` risk-practice disconnect: learner names a protective action but cannot connect it to a specific risk reduction.
