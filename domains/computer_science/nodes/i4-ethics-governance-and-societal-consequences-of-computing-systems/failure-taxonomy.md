# I4 Failure Taxonomy

- `I4-FM-01` ethics-as-opinion only: learner treats computing ethics as personal feeling without explicit criteria, stakeholders, or accountability reasoning.
- `I4-FM-02` technical-social disconnect: learner discusses social impact without connecting it to a bounded technical system choice.
- `I4-FM-03` governance-opacity: learner cannot explain what kind of review, policy, or oversight might matter in the scenario.
- `I4-FM-04` ethics-transfer weakness: learner can analyze one familiar computing-impact case only and cannot adapt the same structure to another bounded system.
