# N1 Failure Taxonomy

- `N1-FM-01` single-device belief: learner assumes one visible device is doing everything in a connected task.
- `N1-FM-02` connection-purpose confusion: learner does not understand why a connection is needed for the bounded task.
- `N1-FM-03` information-movement opacity: learner cannot describe how information gets from one part to another even at a simple level.
- `N1-FM-04` network-context fragility: learner understands one familiar connected setup only and cannot transfer to another bounded case.
