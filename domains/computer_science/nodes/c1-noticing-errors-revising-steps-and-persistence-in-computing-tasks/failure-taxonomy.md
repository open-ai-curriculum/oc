# C1 Failure Taxonomy

- `C1-FM-01` all-or-nothing revision: learner changes many steps at once and cannot track what fixed the issue.
- `C1-FM-02` error-without-location: learner notices something is wrong but cannot identify a likely source.
- `C1-FM-03` abandonment-after-failure: learner stops after an error rather than attempting a bounded revision.
- `C1-FM-04` revision-without-check: learner changes a step but does not compare the new result against the target.
