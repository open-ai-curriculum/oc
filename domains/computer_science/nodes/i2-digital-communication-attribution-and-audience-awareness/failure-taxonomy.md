# I2 Failure Taxonomy

- `I2-FM-01` audience-blind communication: learner sends or frames a message without considering who will receive it.
- `I2-FM-02` source-ownership confusion: learner does not distinguish own content from content created by others.
- `I2-FM-03` tone-context mismatch: learner uses a communication choice that does not fit the bounded audience or setting.
- `I2-FM-04` sharing-without-attribution: learner shares borrowed digital content without acknowledging the source when appropriate.
