# I3 Failure Taxonomy

- `I3-FM-01` technology-neutrality assumption: learner assumes computing systems have no built-in impact from data or design choices.
- `I3-FM-02` fairness-without-cause: learner names fairness as a concern but cannot connect it to a system feature or data choice.
- `I3-FM-03` one-sided impact reasoning: learner evaluates a system only as good or bad without bounded tradeoff analysis.
- `I3-FM-04` human-context omission: learner discusses a tool without considering who uses it and under what conditions.
