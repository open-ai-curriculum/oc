# A2 Failure Taxonomy

- `A2-FM-01` correctness-efficiency collapse: learner confuses a correct algorithm with the most effective one without comparison criteria.
- `A2-FM-02` decomposition-drift: learner breaks a problem into parts that do not fit together coherently.
- `A2-FM-03` criterion-free comparison: learner judges one solution as better without naming a bounded reason.
- `A2-FM-04` near-transfer breakdown: learner can design for one exact case only and cannot adapt the structure to a nearby problem.
