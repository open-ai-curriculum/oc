# I1 Intervention Playbook

- For `I1-FM-01`, use scenario comparisons that make digital consequences visible and discuss who is affected.
- For `I1-FM-02`, use private-shared-public sorting tasks with simple classroom technology examples.
- For `I1-FM-03`, use guided digital participation routines that model respectful action and repair.
- For `I1-FM-04`, use why-this-rule matters prompts tied to one concrete technology-use norm.
