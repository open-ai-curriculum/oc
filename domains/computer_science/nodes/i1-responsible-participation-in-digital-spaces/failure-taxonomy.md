# I1 Failure Taxonomy

- `I1-FM-01` digital-actions-have-no-consequence belief: learner treats digital actions as if they do not affect other people or the system.
- `I1-FM-02` private-shared-public confusion: learner cannot distinguish what should stay private from what may be shared.
- `I1-FM-03` respect-norm instability: learner does not apply basic respect routines in digital participation.
- `I1-FM-04` rule-repeat-without-reason: learner can repeat a norm but cannot explain why it matters in the bounded case.
