# Unit: Community, Maps, and Citizenship

## Unit Metadata

- Unit title: Community, Maps, and Citizenship
- Unit ID: `g1-ss-community-maps-unit`
- Subject: Social Studies
- Grade / grade band: Grade 1
- Estimated duration: 2 weeks
- Review status: Draft

## Standards

- Primary standards:
  - 1-SS-COMMUNITY-MAPS
  - location, place, and simple maps

## Unit Purpose

- What is the unit about?
  - Students identify community roles, use simple maps, and discuss how people participate responsibly in shared spaces.
- Why does it matter for learners?
  - Grade 1 social studies should connect civic life with place, rules, participation, and belonging.

## Desired Results

- Students should understand:
  - communities include places, roles, and shared expectations
  - maps can show where things are
- Students should be able to do:
  - describe a simple community role
  - use a simple map or picture map

## Lesson Sequence

### Lesson 1

- objective: Read a simple map of a school or community place.
- key activities:
  - look at a picture map
  - identify familiar places
  - discuss how people use shared spaces responsibly

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- use inclusive community examples and clear visuals
- allow pointing, oral language, and drawing

## Subject-Specific Quality Notes

- avoid narrow assumptions about home or community life
- keep civic expectations concrete and age-appropriate
