# Unit: Nutrition, Safety, and Emotions

## Unit Metadata

- Unit title: Nutrition, Safety, and Emotions
- Unit ID: `g1-health-safety-emotions-unit`
- Subject: Health
- Grade / grade band: Grade 1
- Estimated duration: 2 weeks
- Review status: Draft

## Standards

- Primary standards:
  - 1-HEALTH-SAFETY
  - describe feelings and responses

## Unit Purpose

- What is the unit about?
  - Students identify healthy foods, safe choices, and ways to name or respond to feelings.
- Why does it matter for learners?
  - Grade 1 health should build concrete self-care, safety, and emotional-language habits.

## Desired Results

- Students should understand:
  - people make choices that affect health and safety
  - feelings can be named and responded to with support
- Students should be able to do:
  - identify one healthy or safe choice
  - name a feeling and a helpful response

## Lesson Sequence

### Lesson 1

- objective: Name one healthy or safe choice and explain why it helps.
- key activities:
  - sort healthy or unsafe examples
  - name a feeling and a response
  - discuss one everyday choice

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- use visuals, calm routines, and concrete examples
- keep topics age-appropriate and non-clinical
