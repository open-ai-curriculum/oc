# Learner Support: My Opinion and My Reason

## Support Material Metadata

- Purpose:
  - Give students a simple structure for saying an opinion, adding a reason, and rereading their writing.
- Review status: Draft

## Alignment Notes

- This scaffold supports the unit expectation that students state an opinion and give a matching reason.
- It is designed to reduce confusion during early writing while preserving the core cognitive work of choosing an idea and supporting it.

## Student-Facing Support

- I think ________.
- One reason is ________.
- I can say it.
- I can draw it.
- I can write it.
- I can reread it and make it clearer.

## Optional Sentence Stems

- “I think ___ because ___.”
- “My reason matches my opinion because ___.”
- “I reread and added ___.”

## Accessibility Notes

- Pair the scaffold with oral rehearsal, picture support, and teacher modeling.
- Keep the language brief and repeatable.
- Allow oral use of the scaffold even if students are not reading it independently.
