## Alignment Notes

- This checklist is aligned to the unit focus on opinion writing and simple revision.
- It is meant for quick in-lesson use, not formal writing evaluation.

## Observation Items

- Student states a clear opinion about the topic or text.
- Student gives at least one reason that matches the opinion.
- Student uses drawing, dictation, or writing to express the opinion-and-reason pair.
- Student rereads the piece and notices or responds to one needed clarification.
- Student participates in oral rehearsal or discussion before writing.

## Accessibility Notes

- Observe across oral rehearsal, drawing, dictation, shared writing, and independent writing.
- Allow students to demonstrate understanding through speech or teacher-supported writing when needed.
- Use the checklist to guide instruction, not as a standalone high-stakes judgment.
