## Purpose

Provide a teacher-ready routine for modeling an opinion and a matching reason.

## Teacher Narrative

- Introduce a familiar topic.
- Model an opinion sentence and a matching reason.
- Compare it to a non-matching reason.
- Support students in orally rehearsing and then writing or dictating one opinion and reason.
