## Alignment Notes

- This scaffold supports the lesson goal of representing and solving a simple story problem.
- It reduces confusion without replacing the math reasoning.

## Student Directions

- Listen to the story.
- Show it with objects or a drawing.
- Solve it.
- Tell how you know.

## Accessibility Notes

- Use with counters, drawings, or oral explanation.
- Read directions aloud and pair with modeling.
- Keep the language short and repeatable.
