## Family Message

You can practice simple number stories in everyday moments.

Try:

- Tell a short story with snacks, toys, or steps.
- Ask your child to show the story with objects or a drawing.
- Ask, “How did you figure it out?”

## Accessibility Notes

- Talking through the thinking matters more than speed.
