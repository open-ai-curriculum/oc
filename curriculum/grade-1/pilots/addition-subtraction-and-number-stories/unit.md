## Unit Purpose

- What is the unit about?
  - Students solve simple addition and subtraction situations using objects, drawings, and equations.
- Why does it matter for learners?
  - Grade 1 students need connected work in number sense, operations, and problem representation rather than isolated facts alone.

## Desired Results

- Students should understand:
  - number stories can show joining, separating, and comparing
  - addition and subtraction can be modeled in more than one way
- Students should be able to do:
  - solve a simple number story
  - represent thinking with objects, drawings, or equations

## Lesson Sequence

### Lesson 1

- objective: Solve a number story using objects or drawings.
- key activities:
  - act out or model a join or separate story
  - solve with counters or drawings
  - explain how the answer was found

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes
