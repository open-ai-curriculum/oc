- Review status: Draft

## Alignment Notes

- This scaffold supports the unit expectation that students name a topic and give a matching fact.
- It is designed to reduce confusion during early writing while preserving the core cognitive work of communicating knowledge from a shared source.

## Student-Facing Support

- My topic is ________.
- One fact is ________.
- I can say it.
- I can draw it.
- I can write it.
- I can reread it and make it clearer.

## Optional Sentence Stems

- “This is about ___.”
- “One fact is ___.”
- “I reread and added ___.”

## Accessibility Notes

- Pair the scaffold with oral rehearsal, picture support, and teacher modeling.
- Keep the language brief and repeatable.
- Allow oral use of the scaffold even if students are not reading it independently.
