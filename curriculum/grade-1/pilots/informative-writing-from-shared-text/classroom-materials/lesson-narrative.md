## Purpose

Provide a teacher-ready routine for turning shared reading into simple factual writing.

## Teacher Narrative

- Revisit a shared informational text.
- Model naming the topic and stating one matching fact.
- Support oral rehearsal before writing or dictation.
- Have students reread and clarify one part if needed.
