# Unit: Digital Citizenship and Algorithmic Thinking

## Unit Metadata

- Unit title: Digital Citizenship and Algorithmic Thinking
- Unit ID: `g1-tech-digital-citizenship-unit`
- Subject: Technology / Computer Science
- Grade / grade band: Grade 1
- Estimated duration: 2 weeks
- Review status: Draft

## Standards

- Primary standards:
  - 1-TECH-ALGORITHM
  - following and creating simple algorithms or sequences

## Unit Purpose

- What is the unit about?
  - Students practice giving and following clear step-by-step directions and discuss safe, respectful use of digital tools.
- Why does it matter for learners?
  - Grade 1 technology learning should build early computational thinking and digital citizenship together.

## Desired Results

- Students should understand:
  - steps can be ordered to complete a task
  - digital tools should be used safely and respectfully
- Students should be able to do:
  - follow a short sequence of directions
  - give a simple set of steps for another person to follow

## Lesson Sequence

### Lesson 1

- objective: Give and follow clear steps to complete a simple task.
- key activities:
  - model an everyday sequence
  - arrange and test ordered steps
  - discuss one safe digital-use norm

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- use picture cards, oral modeling, and unplugged options
- avoid assuming home technology access
