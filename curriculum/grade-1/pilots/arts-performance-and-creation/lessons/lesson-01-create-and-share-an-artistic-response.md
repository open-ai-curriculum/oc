## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - 1-ARTS-CREATE
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will create and share an artistic response to a prompt, sound, text, or image.

## Lesson Flow

- notice and discuss a prompt
- create a response with art, music, or movement
- share one choice or feature

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- allow varied modes of creation and sharing
- use accessible materials and inclusive examples
