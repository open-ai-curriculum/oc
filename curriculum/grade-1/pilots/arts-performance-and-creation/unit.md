# Unit: Arts, Performance, and Creation

## Unit Metadata

- Unit title: Arts, Performance, and Creation
- Unit ID: `g1-arts-performance-unit`
- Subject: Arts
- Grade / grade band: Grade 1
- Estimated duration: 2 weeks
- Review status: Draft

## Standards

- Primary standards:
  - 1-ARTS-CREATE
  - create, perform, and respond to artistic work

## Unit Purpose

- What is the unit about?
  - Students make and share an artistic response using visual art, music, or movement.
- Why does it matter for learners?
  - Grade 1 arts learning should support expression, interpretation, and reflection through making and sharing.

## Desired Results

- Students should understand:
  - art can communicate ideas, feelings, and stories
- Students should be able to do:
  - create a simple artistic response
  - describe one choice they made

## Lesson Sequence

### Lesson 1

- objective: Create and share an artistic response to a prompt or text.
- key activities:
  - notice a prompt, sound, story, or image
  - create a response
  - share one feature of the work

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- provide varied materials, movement options, and oral or nonverbal sharing
- use inclusive prompts and examples

## Subject-Specific Quality Notes

- keep arts work expressive and meaningful
- avoid over-standardizing creative output
