## Alignment Notes

- This checklist is aligned to the unit focus on decoding short-vowel CVC words and rereading connected text more accurately.
- It is meant for quick in-lesson use, not formal diagnostic assessment.

## Observation Items

- Student blends sounds left to right instead of guessing from the first letter.
- Student reads target short-vowel words accurately with teacher support as needed.
- Student notices or corrects at least one inaccurate word during rereading.
- Student rereads the connected text with greater accuracy on a second attempt.
- Student can describe one decoding move or strategy used.

## Suggested Recording Format

- `consistently`
- `sometimes`

## Accessibility Notes

- Observe across oral reading, tile movement, pointing, and teacher-supported response.
- Allow students to demonstrate strategy understanding verbally or by showing the word parts they used.
- Use the checklist as a support for instruction, not as a standalone high-stakes judgment.
