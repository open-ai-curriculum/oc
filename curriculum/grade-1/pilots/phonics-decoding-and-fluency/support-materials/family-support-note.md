## Alignment Notes

- This note supports the unit focus on blending sounds in simple words and rereading short text for better accuracy.
- It is written to help families reinforce the classroom routine in a brief and realistic way.

## Family-Facing Guidance

- Ask your child to look at each letter in a short word and say the sounds slowly.
- Help your child blend the sounds into the whole word.
- Read one short sentence together, then read it again.
- Keep practice short. One or two minutes of calm practice is enough.
- Praise careful looking and trying again, not just getting the word quickly.

## Suggested Family Prompt

- “Say the sounds, blend the word, and read it again.”

## Accessibility Notes

- Keep home practice brief and low-pressure.
- Encourage families to support oral practice even if a child is not ready to read independently for long stretches.
- Avoid assuming access to special materials beyond paper, pencil, or a teacher-sent text.
