## Alignment Notes

- This scaffold supports the decoding and rereading expectations of the unit.
- It is designed to reduce confusion during practice while preserving the core cognitive work of sound-symbol mapping and rereading.

## Student-Facing Support

- Look at each letter.
- Say each sound.
- Blend the sounds into a word.
- Read the whole word again.
- Read the sentence or short text.
- Reread and see if it sounds right.

## Optional Sentence Stems

- “I said each sound and blended the word.”
- “I reread because I wanted the word to match the letters.”
- “This word has the short vowel sound ___.”

## Accessibility Notes

- Pair the routine with finger tracking, sound boxes, or letter tiles.
- Keep the language brief and repeatable.
- Allow oral use of the scaffold even if students are not reading it independently.
