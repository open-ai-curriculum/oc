# Unit: Plants, Needs, and Patterns

## Unit Metadata

- Unit title: Plants, Needs, and Patterns
- Unit ID: `g1-sci-plants-patterns-unit`
- Subject: Science
- Grade / grade band: Grade 1
- Estimated duration: 2 weeks
- Review status: Draft

## Standards

- Primary standards:
  - 1-LS1-1
  - observation and explanation of living things

## Unit Purpose

- What is the unit about?
  - Students observe plants, notice what they need, and describe simple patterns in growth or care.
- Why does it matter for learners?
  - Grade 1 science should connect living things to observation, evidence, and explanation.

## Desired Results

- Students should understand:
  - plants need resources to live and grow
  - observations help scientists describe living things
- Students should be able to do:
  - identify simple plant needs
  - record or discuss an observation

## Lesson Sequence

### Lesson 1

- objective: Observe a plant and describe what it needs.
- key activities:
  - observe a real plant or images
  - discuss plant parts and needs
  - record one observation

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- use real objects, labeled visuals, and oral discussion
- allow drawing and verbal responses

## Subject-Specific Quality Notes

- keep science tied to evidence and observation
- review diagrams and claims for accuracy
