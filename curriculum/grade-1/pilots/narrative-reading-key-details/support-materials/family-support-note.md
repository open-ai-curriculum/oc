## Family Message

Talking about a story helps your child understand and remember it.

Try these simple prompts:

- “What happened at the beginning?”
- “What happened next?”
- “What was an important detail?”
- “How do you know?”
- “Can you retell the story in order?”

## Easy At-Home Ideas

- Read a short story together and stop to ask one or two key-detail questions.
- Ask your child to retell the story using beginning, middle, and end.
- Let your child point to pictures or reread a page to help answer.
- Use your home language too. Talking about story events in any language supports comprehension.
