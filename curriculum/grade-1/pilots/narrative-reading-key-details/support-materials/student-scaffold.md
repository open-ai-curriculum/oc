## Student Directions

### 1. Listen or read

- Think about who, what, where, and what happened.

### 2. Find a key detail

- Point to a picture or sentence that helps answer the question.
- Tell why that detail matters.

### 3. Retell the story

- Say: “In the beginning…”
- Say: “In the middle…”
- Say: “At the end…”

### 4. Add one important detail

- Tell one detail that helps explain the story better.

## Optional Sentence Supports

- “A key detail is ____________________.”
- “In the beginning, ____________________.”
- “This detail matters because ____________________.”
