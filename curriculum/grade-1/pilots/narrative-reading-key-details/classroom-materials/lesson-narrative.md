## Purpose

Provide a teacher-ready routine for asking and answering questions about key story details.

## Teacher Narrative

- Read a short narrative text aloud.
- Pause to ask who, what, where, and what happened questions.
- Model the difference between a key detail and an extra detail.
- End with a beginning-middle-end retell prompt.
