## Alignment Notes

## Student Directions

### 1. Read or listen

- Think: “What is this text about?”

### 2. Answer the question

- Find a picture, heading, label, or sentence that helps.
- Tell your answer.

### 3. Say the topic

- “This text is mostly about ____________________.”

### 4. Add a key detail

- “One important detail is ____________________.”

## Optional Sentence Supports

- “The text is mostly about ____________________.”
- “A key detail is ____________________.”
- “I know because I saw ____________________.”
