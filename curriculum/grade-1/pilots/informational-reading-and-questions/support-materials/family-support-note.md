## Family Message

Talking about information books or articles helps your child learn new ideas and explain what they read.

Try these simple prompts:

- “What is this text mostly about?”
- “What is one important detail?”
- “What picture or word helped you know that?”
- “What question do you have about this topic?”

## Easy At-Home Ideas

- Read a short information book, article, or labeled page together.
- Pause to ask one or two key-detail questions.
- Ask your child to show the picture or words that helped them answer.
- Use your home language too. Talking about a topic in any language supports understanding.
