## Purpose

Provide a teacher-ready routine for asking and answering questions about key facts in an informational text.

## Teacher Narrative

- Preview the topic and illustrations.
- Read the informational text in short sections.
- Ask who, what, and how questions tied to key details.
- End by naming what the text is mostly about.
