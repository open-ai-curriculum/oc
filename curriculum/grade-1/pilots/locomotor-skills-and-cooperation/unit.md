## Standards

- Primary standards:

## Unit Purpose

- What is the unit about?
  - Students practice locomotor movement, changing speed or direction, and cooperating with partners safely.
- Why does it matter for learners?
  - Grade 1 physical education should build skill, confidence, control, and positive participation.

## Desired Results

- Students should understand:
  - movement can be controlled in different ways
  - cooperation helps people participate safely
- Students should be able to do:
  - demonstrate locomotor movement
  - respond to cues and work with a partner

## Lesson Sequence

### Lesson 1

- objective: Move with control and cooperate with a partner.
- key activities:
  - practice travel patterns
  - change speed or direction on cue
  - complete a simple partner movement task
