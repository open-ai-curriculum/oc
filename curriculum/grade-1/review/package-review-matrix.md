## Purpose

Use this matrix to review each Grade 1 package against the same dimensions.

- `partially meets expectations`

## Reviewer Prompts By Dimension

### Sequence fit

- Does this package belong in the current order?
- Does it prepare for or reinforce the next package productively?

### Grade-level fit

- Are the tasks, prompts, and supports appropriate for first grade?
- Does the package balance oral language, supported writing, and reading demands appropriately?

### Unit coherence

- Does the unit goal match the standards and lesson sequence?
- Is there a clear instructional throughline from text work to evidence-based response?

### Lesson usability

- Could a teacher enact the lesson without major guesswork?
- Are materials, routines, and pacing plausible?

### Support-material usability

- Does the teacher guide improve enactment?
- Is the observation checklist usable during instruction?
- Does the learner scaffold help without reducing the task to completion behavior?
- Is the family note realistic for home use?

### Accessibility and inclusion

- Are multiple participation paths clear?
- Are examples, prompts, and assumptions inclusive?
- Are supports reducing barriers instead of lowering rigor?

### Knowledge / content integrity

- Are the text choices, questions, examples, and writing models instructionally sound?
- Are there weak prompts, inaccuracies, avoidable ambiguities, questionable decoding routines, or hollow writing tasks?
