## Purpose

Provide a Grade 1 student-facing textbook draft assembled from the governed packet set.

## Unit 1: Reading Stories for Key Details

- stories contain important details that help readers understand what happened
- readers can answer questions and retell beginning, middle, and end

## Unit 2: Reading Informational Texts

- informational texts teach about a topic through facts and details
- readers ask and answer questions to learn more

## Unit 3: Phonics and Fluency

- readers blend sounds to decode words
- rereading helps reading become smoother and more accurate

## Unit 4: Opinion Writing

- writers tell what they think
- writers give reasons that match the opinion

## Unit 5: Informative Writing

- writers teach about a topic using facts
- facts should match what was learned from a text or lesson

## Unit 6: Addition and Subtraction Stories

- number stories can show joining and separating
- objects, drawings, and equations can represent the same story

## Unit 7: Plants and Patterns

- plants need resources to live and grow
- careful observation helps scientists describe living things

## Unit 8: Community, Maps, and Citizenship

- maps show where places are
- people share responsibilities in communities

## Unit 9: Arts, Performance, and Creation

- art can show an idea, feeling, or story
- artists can create and share with others

## Unit 10: Nutrition, Safety, and Emotions

- people make choices that affect health and safety
- feelings can be named and responded to constructively

## Unit 11: Locomotor Skills and Cooperation

- bodies can move with control in different ways
- cooperation helps people participate safely

## Unit 12: Digital Citizenship and Algorithmic Thinking

- tasks can be described in ordered steps
- digital tools should be used safely and respectfully
