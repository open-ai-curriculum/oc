# Grade 1 Workbook Draft

## Volume Metadata

- Volume title: Grade 1 Workbook Draft
- Program layer: workbook
- Grade / grade band: Grade 1
- Review status: Draft

## Purpose

Provide a real Grade 1 practice-book draft assembled from packet classroom assignments and home-practice routines.

## How To Use This Volume

- Use workbook pages after direct instruction and discussion.
- Keep the focus on evidence, explanation, and short meaningful practice.
- Mix written, oral, drawn, and performance-linked responses as needed.

## Section 1: Narrative Reading Practice

### Practice Page 1: Important Detail or Extra Detail?

- Read or hear a short story detail set.
- Mark which details are important to understanding the story.

### Practice Page 2: Beginning, Middle, End

- Place three story events in order.
- Write or say one detail for each part.

## Section 2: Informational Reading Practice

### Practice Page 3: Ask a Question About the Topic

- Read a short text or look at a text feature.
- Write or say one question about it.

### Practice Page 4: Find the Answer

- Match a question to a fact from the text.
- Underline or point to the detail that helps answer it.

## Section 3: Phonics and Fluency Practice

### Practice Page 5: Blend the Word

- Read short-vowel CVC words.
- Mark each sound and blend the whole word.

### Practice Page 6: Reread for Smoothness

- Read a short line once.
- Read it again more accurately and smoothly.

## Section 4: Opinion Writing Practice

### Practice Page 7: State an Opinion

- Write or dictate one opinion about a familiar topic.

### Practice Page 8: Add a Reason

- Add one reason that matches the opinion.
- Reread to see if the reason fits.

## Section 5: Informative Writing Practice

### Practice Page 9: Name the Topic

- Write the topic of a shared text or lesson.

### Practice Page 10: Add One Fact

- Write or dictate one matching fact about the topic.

## Section 6: Number Story Practice

### Practice Page 11: Show the Story

- Read or hear a number story.
- Solve it with objects or drawings.

### Practice Page 12: Explain the Math

- Tell or write how you solved it.

## Section 7: Plant Observation Practice

### Practice Page 13: What Does a Plant Need?

- Match plant needs to simple pictures.

### Practice Page 14: Record an Observation

- Draw or write one thing you notice about a plant.

## Section 8: Community and Map Practice

### Practice Page 15: Read the Map

- Use a simple map to find a place.

### Practice Page 16: Community Responsibility

- Write or say one way people care for shared places.

## Section 9: Arts Practice

### Practice Page 17: Create from a Prompt

- Draw or design something based on a prompt.

### Practice Page 18: Explain One Choice

- Tell or write one choice you made in your work.

## Section 10: Health Practice

### Practice Page 19: Safe or Unsafe?

- Sort simple examples into healthy/safe and not safe.

### Practice Page 20: Feelings and Responses

- Match a feeling to one helpful response.

## Section 11: PE Practice

### Practice Page 21: Move and Cooperate

- Reflect on one movement and one partner skill.

### Practice Page 22: Follow the Cue

- Show how you changed speed, direction, or stopped safely.

## Section 12: Technology and Sequencing Practice

### Practice Page 23: Put the Steps in Order

- Number a short set of steps 1, 2, 3.

### Practice Page 24: Safe Digital Choice

- Identify one safe and respectful digital-use choice.

## Home Practice Strip

- retell a story in order
- ask and answer a question about a topic
- reread a short decodable line
- tell an opinion and a reason
- share one fact about a topic
- act out a number story
- observe a plant
- talk about places in the community
- make and share art
- talk about a healthy choice
- move and follow cues
- talk through steps in order
