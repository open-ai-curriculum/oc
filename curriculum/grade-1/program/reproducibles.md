# Grade 1 Reproducible Teacher-Use Assets

## Purpose

Provide assembled teacher-use masters that can be copied or reformatted into classroom assignments, response sheets, observation tools, and family-facing supports across the full Grade 1 program.

## Included Reproducibles

### Literacy

- key-detail question strips
- beginning / middle / end retell organizer
- informational topic-and-detail organizer
- phonics blending mat
- opinion sentence frame page
- informative fact organizer

### Math

- number-story representation mat
- explain-my-strategy response strip

### Science

- plant observation log
- plant needs and evidence match sheet

### Social Studies

- simple map symbol key
- community responsibility response card

### Arts

- create-and-explain prompt sheet

### Health

- healthy or safe choice scenario card
- feelings and response organizer

### Physical Education

- locomotor cue card strip
- partner cooperation reflection sheet

### Technology

- sequencing cards
- safe digital choice response strip

## Reproducible Master: Number-Story Representation Mat

- What happened in the story?
- Show it with:
  - objects
  - a drawing
  - an equation
- My answer is: ______
- I know because: ______

## Reproducible Master: Simple Map Response Page

- Circle the place named by the teacher.
- Draw a path from one place to another.
- Tell one way people should care for this place.

## Reproducible Master: Opinion Sentence Frame Page

- My opinion is __________.
- One reason is __________.
- I know this because __________.

## Notes

- These reproducibles are draft content masters, not final classroom print files.
- They still need formatting, accessibility review, and reproduction testing.
