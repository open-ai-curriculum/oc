## Purpose

Provide simple criteria for interpreting letter-recognition evidence.

## Suggested Criteria

- Recognition:
  - `secure`: recognizes and names focus letters
  - `developing`: recognizes with prompting
  - `not yet`: does not yet recognize the focus letters
- Matching:
  - `secure`: matches uppercase and lowercase correctly
  - `developing`: matches with support
  - `not yet`: does not yet match pairs
