## Purpose

Provide a teacher-ready routine for introducing a small set of letter names and shapes.

## Teacher Narrative

- Display uppercase and lowercase focus letters.
- Name each letter and trace its shape in the air.
- Ask students to match uppercase to lowercase.
- Reinforce the lesson with song, tracing, and quick recognition prompts.

## Assessment Notes

- Look for whether students can recognize and name at least one taught letter pair with support.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use tactile, visual, and movement-based options.
- Limit the number of focus letters at one time.
