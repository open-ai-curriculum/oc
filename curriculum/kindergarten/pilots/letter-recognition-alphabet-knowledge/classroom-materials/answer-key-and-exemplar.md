## Purpose

Provide guidance on acceptable evidence in the letter-matching task.

## Correct Or Acceptable Responses

- identifies one or more focus letters
- matches uppercase and lowercase forms correctly

## Exemplar Student Responses

- points to matching letter cards and says the letter name
- traces the shape while naming it

## Common Error Patterns

- confuses visually similar letters
- identifies only one case without matching the pair
