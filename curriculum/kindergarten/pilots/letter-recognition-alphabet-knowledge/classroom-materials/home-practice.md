## Purpose

Provide a short home routine for noticing and naming letters in everyday print.

## Directions For Families

- Find one or two letters on a sign, book, or package.
- Ask your child to match the letters to the ones learned in class.
- Say the letter names together.

## Accessibility Notes

- Home language support is welcome.
- Oral noticing is enough.
