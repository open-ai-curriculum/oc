## Alignment Notes

- The scaffold preserves the lesson objective: recognize and name a small taught set of letters and match corresponding uppercase/lowercase forms.
- It supports pointing, naming, tracing, and matching without replacing the literacy target.
- It is aligned to the unit’s alphabet-knowledge goals.

## Student Directions

### 1. Look at the letter

- Find the letter your teacher names.
- Point to the letter.

### 2. Say the letter name

- Repeat the letter name.
- Show the matching uppercase or lowercase partner.

### 3. Match the pair

- Put the two matching letters together.
- Check that they belong to the same letter name.

### 4. Say the sound

- Say a common sound for the focus letter with the class.
- Listen for a word that starts with that sound.

## Optional Sentence Supports

- “This is letter ______.”
- “These match.”
- “I hear ______.”
