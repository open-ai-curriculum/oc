## How To Use

- Observe a small group each day if whole-class note taking is unrealistic.
- Note whether a student identifies the letter independently or after prompting.
- Use the notes column for follow-up planning, not permanent labeling.
- Revisit the same checklist across several lessons to monitor growth.

## Accessibility Notes

- The checklist is printable, editable, and low-bandwidth friendly.
- It supports quick notation during live instruction.
- It should be paired with multimodal participation rather than replacing it.
