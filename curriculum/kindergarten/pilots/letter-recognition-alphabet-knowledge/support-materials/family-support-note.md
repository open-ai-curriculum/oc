## Family Message

You can help your child notice letters in everyday life. Short, playful practice is enough.

Try these simple ideas:

- Ask, “Can you find this letter?”
- Say a letter name and look for it in a book, sign, or label.
- Match an uppercase and lowercase pair if you have alphabet cards or magnets.
- Say a common sound for a focus letter and think of a word that starts that way.

## Easy At-Home Ideas

- Use letters in your child’s name.
- Look for letters on food boxes, mail, or simple signs.
- Let your child point, say, match, or trace letters with a finger.
- Use your home language too; naming and noticing print is still valuable.
