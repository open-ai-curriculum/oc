## Alignment Notes

- This guide supports the unit goals around healthy habits, emotional language, and safe responses.
- It keeps the health work concrete, age-appropriate, and non-shaming.

## Teacher Preparation Notes

- Prepare feeling visuals and a few clear examples of healthy or safe choices.
- Keep the routine predictable and brief.
- Decide how students can respond without personal disclosure.

## Likely Misconceptions Or Developmental Challenges

- Students may confuse a feeling with a behavior.
- Students may repeat a rule without connecting it to a healthy choice.
- Some students may need nonverbal ways to identify feelings.

## Recommended Teacher Moves

- Offer named choices and visuals.
- Ask, “What is one safe choice?”
- Keep responses concrete and avoid probing for personal experiences.

## Observation Guidance

- Notice whether students can name one feeling.
- Track whether students can identify one healthy or safe response.
- Note who needs language, regulation, or visual support.

## Accessibility Notes

- Use visuals, calm language, and predictable routines.
- Allow pointing, gestures, and oral response.
- Avoid shaming or overly personal prompts.
