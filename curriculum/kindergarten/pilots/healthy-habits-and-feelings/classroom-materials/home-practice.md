## Purpose

Provide a short home conversation routine about feelings and healthy habits.

## Directions For Families

- Ask your child to name a feeling from the day.
- Ask what helps when they feel that way.
- Practice one healthy action such as breathing slowly or asking for help.

## Accessibility Notes

- Home language discussion is welcome.
- Keep the conversation brief and calm.
