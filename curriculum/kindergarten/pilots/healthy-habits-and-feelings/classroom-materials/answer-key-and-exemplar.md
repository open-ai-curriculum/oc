## Purpose

Provide guidance on acceptable feeling-identification and safe-response evidence.

## Correct Or Acceptable Responses

- identifies a feeling such as happy, sad, mad, or scared
- chooses a safe or healthy response such as ask for help, breathe, rest, or drink water

## Exemplar Student Responses

- "I feel sad. I can ask for help."
- points to an upset face and models a calm breath

## Common Error Patterns

- names a feeling but no response
- gives a harmful or unrelated action
