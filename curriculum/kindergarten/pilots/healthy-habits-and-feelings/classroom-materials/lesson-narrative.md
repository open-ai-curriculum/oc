## Purpose

Provide a teacher-ready routine for naming feelings and connecting them to safe, healthy responses.

## Teacher Narrative

- Show a feeling card and name the feeling aloud.
- Model one healthy or safe response such as breathing, asking for help, drinking water, or taking a calm break.
- Invite students to match a feeling to a safe response.
- Rehearse the response with words, gestures, or visuals.

## Assessment Notes

- Look for whether students can identify a feeling and connect it to a safe or healthy action.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep examples concrete and non-shaming.
- Allow gestures, pointing, and picture supports.
