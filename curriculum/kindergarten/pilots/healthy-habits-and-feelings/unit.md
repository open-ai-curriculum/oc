# Unit: Healthy Habits and Feelings

## Unit Metadata

- Unit title: Healthy Habits and Feelings
- Unit ID: `k-health-habits-feelings-unit`
- Subject: Health
- Grade / grade band: Kindergarten
- Estimated duration: 2 weeks
- Review status: Draft

## Standards

- Primary standards:
  - K-HEALTH-HABITS
  - name feelings and safe responses

## Unit Purpose

- What is the unit about?
  - Students learn simple habits that support health and practice naming feelings and safe responses.
- Why does it matter for learners?
  - Kindergarten health should support body awareness, self-care, and emotional language in concrete ways.

## Desired Results

- Students should understand:
  - healthy habits help bodies stay safe and strong
  - feelings can be named and managed with support
- Students should be able to do:
  - identify one healthy habit
  - name a feeling and one safe response

## Lesson Sequence

### Lesson 1

- objective: Name a feeling and one healthy or safe choice.
- key activities:
  - identify feelings with visuals
  - talk about simple healthy choices
  - practice one response routine

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- use visuals, repeated language, and predictable routines
- keep topics age-appropriate and non-clinical

## Subject-Specific Quality Notes

- avoid shaming language
- keep examples concrete, safe, and inclusive
