## Student Directions

### 1. Look at the book

- Point to the front cover.
- Show where the book opens.

### 2. Name what you see

- Title: “This is the title.”
- Author: “This is the author.”
- Illustrator: “This is the illustrator.”

### 3. Show where reading starts

- Point to where reading begins on the page.
- Move your finger the way the words go.

### 4. Talk about the story

- Answer: “What happened on this page?”
- Answer: “What do you notice in the picture?”
- Ask: “Why did that happen?” or “What comes next?”

## Optional Sentence Supports

- “I start reading here.”
- “The title tells me ____________________.”
- “I noticed ____________________ in the picture.”

## Accessibility Notes

- The scaffold uses short repeated prompts and can be read aloud.
- It supports pointing, gesture, oral language, and picture-based response.
- It should remain printable, editable, and usable in low-bandwidth settings.
