## Purpose

Provide a short teacher-led assessment check aligned to the lesson objective.

## Teacher Directions

Use a familiar book or displayed sample page.

Ask each student to:

1. show the front cover
2. point to where reading starts
3. answer one key-detail question about the read-aloud

## Assessment Items

- Item 1: "Show me the front cover."
- Item 2: "Show me where I start reading."
- Item 3: "Tell me one thing that happened or one thing you noticed in the book."

## Response Format

- live performance
- pointing
- gesture
- short oral response

## Accessibility Notes

- Repeat directions as needed.
- Allow pointing or picture-supported response.
- Keep the assessment brief and low-pressure.
