## Purpose

Provide teacher scoring notes for the short assessment check.

## Item Key

### Item 1: Front Cover

- correct:
  - student points to or names the front cover
- partially correct:
  - student points to the book generally but not specifically to the front cover
- not yet:
  - student cannot identify the front cover even with support

### Item 2: Reading Start

- correct:
  - student points to where reading begins on the page
- partially correct:
  - student points near the correct area with prompting
- not yet:
  - student points randomly or cannot indicate a starting place

### Item 3: Key Detail Response

- correct:
  - student gives one relevant detail from the read-aloud or picture
- partially correct:
  - student gives a loosely related detail with prompting
- not yet:
  - student gives an unrelated or no response

## Scoring Guidance

- Two or more `correct` responses suggest the student is moving toward the lesson goal.
- A mix of `partially correct` and `correct` responses suggests continued guided practice is needed.
- Multiple `not yet` responses suggest reteaching with stronger modeling and visual support.
