## Purpose

Provide a brief home-practice task that reinforces book handling, print awareness, and simple talk about a text.

## Directions For Families

Use any book, magazine, library book, or printed picture page.

Ask your child to:

- show the front cover
- show where the book opens
- point to where reading starts
- tell one thing they notice in the picture or story

## Expected Home Evidence

- child uses pointing, speech, or gesture to show book knowledge
- family engages in brief shared talk about the text

## Accessibility Notes

- No internet or special materials are required.

- Home language discussion is welcome.
