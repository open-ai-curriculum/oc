## Purpose

Provide simple criteria for interpreting classroom evidence from the lesson task.

## Suggested Criteria

### Criterion 1: Book Handling And Book-Part Knowledge

- `secure`
  - student identifies the front cover and shows how the book opens without prompting
- `developing`
  - student identifies one feature with prompting
- `not yet`
  - student does not yet orient to the book or identify parts consistently

### Criterion 2: Print Awareness

- `secure`
  - student shows where reading begins and tracks direction with little support
- `developing`
  - student shows partial understanding with prompts or modeling
- `not yet`
  - student does not yet show where reading begins

### Criterion 3: Read-Aloud Response

- `secure`
  - student answers one key-detail question using the text or picture
- `developing`
  - student gives a partially related response with support
- `not yet`
  - student does not yet connect response to the read-aloud

## Scoring Notes

- Use the criteria for planning next instruction, not for high-stakes grading.
- Multimodal evidence counts.
- A student may be stronger in book handling than in oral response, or vice versa.
