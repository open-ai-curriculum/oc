## Alignment Notes

- This guide supports the unit goals around following short sequences and using simple tools with guidance.
- It keeps the work age-appropriate and low-risk.

## Teacher Preparation Notes

- Prepare picture-step cards and an unplugged version of the task.
- Decide what parts of the lesson require no device at all.
- Keep any digital use brief and guided.

## Likely Misconceptions Or Developmental Challenges

- Students may know the routine but not the order.
- Some students may focus on the device instead of the sequence.
- Students may need strong modeling for first, next, and last.

## Recommended Teacher Moves

- Model the full sequence before students try it.
- Ask students to say the steps aloud.
- Use unplugged rehearsal before digital interaction.

## Observation Guidance

- Notice whether students keep steps in order.
- Track whether students can describe first, next, and last.
- Note who needs extra visual or partner support.

## Accessibility Notes

- Use picture cards, oral modeling, and unplugged options.
- Avoid requiring independent device fluency.
- Keep privacy and data exposure minimal.
