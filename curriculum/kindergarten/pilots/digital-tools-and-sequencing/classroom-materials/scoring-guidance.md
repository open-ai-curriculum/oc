## Suggested Criteria

  - `developing`: orders with some prompting
  - `not yet`: does not yet order steps coherently
- Following:
  - `secure`: follows the ordered steps
  - `developing`: follows with teacher help
  - `not yet`: does not yet use the ordered sequence
