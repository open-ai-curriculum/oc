# Unit: Digital Tools and Sequencing

## Unit Metadata

- Unit title: Digital Tools and Sequencing
- Unit ID: `k-tech-sequencing-unit`
- Subject: Technology / Computer Science
- Grade / grade band: Kindergarten
- Estimated duration: 2 weeks
- Review status: Draft

## Standards

- Primary standards:
  - K-TECH-SEQUENCE
  - recognize and follow simple sequences

## Unit Purpose

- What is the unit about?
  - Students use simple digital or unplugged tools and follow step-by-step sequences to complete a task.
- Why does it matter for learners?
  - Kindergarten technology learning should build responsible tool use, sequencing, and clear step-following in age-appropriate ways.

## Desired Results

- Students should understand:
  - some tasks happen in an order
  - digital tools should be used safely and with guidance
- Students should be able to do:
  - follow a short sequence
  - describe a first, next, and last step

## Lesson Sequence

### Lesson 1

- objective: Follow a simple sequence to complete a task.
- key activities:
  - act out a short sequence
  - arrange steps in order
  - use a tool or unplugged task following the steps

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- use visual step cards, oral modeling, and unplugged options
- avoid assuming independent device fluency

## Subject-Specific Quality Notes

- keep technology use purposeful and age-appropriate
- protect privacy and avoid unnecessary account or data use
