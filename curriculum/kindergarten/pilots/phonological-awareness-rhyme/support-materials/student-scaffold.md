## Student Directions

### 1. Listen to the words

- Hear two words.
- Think: “Do they sound the same at the end?”

### 2. Show what you hear

- Thumbs up if they rhyme.
- Hands flat if they do not rhyme.

### 3. Say the rhyme

- Repeat the two rhyming words.
- Join the chant or poem when the rhyming word comes.

### 4. Clap the parts

- Clap the beats in a word.
- Say the word again and count the claps.

## Optional Sentence Supports

- “These rhyme.”
- “These do not rhyme.”
