## Purpose

Provide guidance on acceptable rhyme-identification evidence.

## Correct Or Acceptable Responses

- identifies a rhyming pair by sound
- sorts picture cards into rhyme or not-rhyme with support

## Exemplar Student Responses

- "Cat and hat rhyme."
- moves two rhyming pictures together

## Common Error Patterns

- matches by first sound instead of ending sound
- guesses without attending to the oral model
