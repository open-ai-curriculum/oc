## Purpose

Provide simple criteria for interpreting rhyme-listening evidence.

## Suggested Criteria

- Listening for rhyme:
  - `secure`: identifies rhyming pairs accurately
  - `developing`: identifies with repeated modeling
  - `not yet`: does not yet distinguish rhyme reliably
- Participation:
  - `secure`: participates in oral or movement-based rhyme tasks
  - `developing`: participates with support
  - `not yet`: does not yet engage in the task
