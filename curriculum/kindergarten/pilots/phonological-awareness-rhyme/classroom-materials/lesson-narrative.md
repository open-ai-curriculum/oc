## Purpose

Provide a teacher-ready oral-language routine for hearing and identifying rhyme.

## Teacher Narrative

- Read or chant a short rhyme-rich text aloud.
- Emphasize the ending sounds in a pair of rhyming words.
- Ask students to identify which words sound the same at the end.
- Use movement or sorting to reinforce rhyming and non-rhyming pairs.

## Assessment Notes

- Look for listening accuracy rather than spelling or print-based reasoning.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep the lesson oral and playful.
- Use clear picture supports and repeated modeling.
