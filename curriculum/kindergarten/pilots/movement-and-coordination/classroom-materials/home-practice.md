## Purpose

Provide a short home movement game tied to safe stopping and balance.

## Directions For Families

- Ask your child to move in one way such as walk, tiptoe, or march.
- Call "freeze" and see if your child can stop safely.
- Try one simple balance pose.

## Accessibility Notes

- Use a small safe space.
- Adapt movements to what is comfortable.
