## Purpose

Provide guidance on acceptable movement-control evidence.

## Correct Or Acceptable Responses

- moves safely in the assigned way
- stops on cue
- holds a simple balance briefly

## Exemplar Student Responses

- freezes with body control
- balances on one foot briefly or with light support

## Common Error Patterns

- continues moving after cue
- unsafe use of space
- cannot attempt a balance pose
