## Purpose

Provide simple criteria for interpreting movement-control evidence.

## Suggested Criteria

- Cue response:
  - `secure`: responds to movement and freeze cues safely
  - `developing`: responds with occasional prompting
  - `not yet`: does not yet respond consistently
- Balance:
  - `secure`: holds a simple balance pose
  - `developing`: attempts with support
  - `not yet`: does not yet attempt or sustain balance
