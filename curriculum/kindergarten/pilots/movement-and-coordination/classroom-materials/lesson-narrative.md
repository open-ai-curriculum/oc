## Purpose

Provide a teacher-ready movement routine for safe locomotor practice, stopping, and balance.

## Teacher Narrative

- Model how to move safely in open space.
- Practice walk, hop, or march, then freeze on cue.
- Add simple balance poses with visual modeling.
- Reinforce body control and space awareness throughout.

## Assessment Notes

- Look for safe participation, response to stop cues, and ability to hold a brief balance.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Offer lower-impact movement options.
- Adapt pace, distance, and balancing demands.
