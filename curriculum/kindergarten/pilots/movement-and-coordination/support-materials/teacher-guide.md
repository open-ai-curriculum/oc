## Alignment Notes

- This guide supports the unit goals around locomotor movement, balance, and safe participation.
- It keeps the focus on control, joy, and safety rather than competition.

## Teacher Preparation Notes

- Prepare a clear movement space and visual boundaries.
- Model each movement and safe stopping routine.
- Plan alternate movement options.

## Likely Misconceptions Or Developmental Challenges

- Students may move too quickly to stop safely.
- Some students may need alternate balance positions.
- Students may focus on “winning” rather than safe participation.

## Recommended Teacher Moves

- Use short clear cues.
- Model safe starts and stops repeatedly.
- Praise control, effort, and cooperation.

## Observation Guidance

- Notice movement control and stopping on cue.
- Track balance and body awareness.
- Note who needs alternate pacing or movement options.

## Accessibility Notes

- Offer multiple movement options.
- Adapt pace, range of motion, and space needs.
- Avoid elimination-based formats.
