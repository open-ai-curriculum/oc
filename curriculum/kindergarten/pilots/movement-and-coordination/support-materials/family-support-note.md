## Family Message

You can practice safe movement in short playful ways.

Try:

- Walk, hop, or tiptoe together.
- Say “freeze” and stop safely.
- Balance for a few seconds in a safe space.

## Accessibility Notes

- Safety and body control matter more than speed.
