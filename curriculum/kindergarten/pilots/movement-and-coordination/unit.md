## Standards

- Primary standards:
  - K-PE-MOVE
  - balance and body control
  - safe participation

## Unit Purpose

- What is the unit about?
  - Students practice moving, stopping, balancing, and following safe movement routines.
- Why does it matter for learners?
  - Kindergarten physical education should build body awareness, coordination, and safe participation through joyful movement.

## Desired Results

- Students should understand:
  - bodies can move in different safe ways
  - stopping and balancing require control
- Students should be able to do:
  - perform simple locomotor movements
  - stop and balance on cue

## Lesson Sequence

### Lesson 1

- objective: Move, balance, and freeze safely.
- key activities:
  - practice walking, hopping, and stopping
  - balance in simple positions
  - follow movement cues

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- provide multiple movement options and visual modeling
- adapt range of motion and pace as needed
