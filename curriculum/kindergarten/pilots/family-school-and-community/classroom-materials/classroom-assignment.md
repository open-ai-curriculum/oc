## Purpose

Define the live classroom task students complete to identify helpers and roles.

## Student Task

- Look at helper pictures.
- Choose one helper at home, school, or in the community.
- Tell or show one way that person helps.

## Expected Evidence Of Learning

- student identifies a person with a role
- student connects the role to helping behavior
