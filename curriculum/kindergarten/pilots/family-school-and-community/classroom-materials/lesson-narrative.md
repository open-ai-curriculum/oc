## Purpose

Provide a teacher-ready discussion routine for introducing roles people play at home, school, and in the community.

## Teacher Narrative

- Show pictures of familiar helpers and ask, "How does this person help?"
- Sort examples into home, school, and community.
- Revoice student ideas with simple language about helping roles and responsibilities.
- Invite each student to name or point to one helper they know.

## Assessment Notes

- Look for whether students can identify a helper and describe one way that person helps.
- Accept pointing, naming, or short phrases.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use varied and culturally inclusive examples.
- Avoid assuming one family structure or community context.
