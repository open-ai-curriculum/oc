## Purpose

Provide guidance on acceptable responses during the helper-identification task.

## Correct Or Acceptable Responses

- identifies a real helper such as teacher, caregiver, nurse, crossing guard, or firefighter
- describes one way the helper supports people

## Exemplar Student Responses

- "My teacher helps me learn."
- "A firefighter helps keep people safe."

## Common Error Patterns

- names a person without describing the role
- gives an unrelated activity instead of a helping responsibility
