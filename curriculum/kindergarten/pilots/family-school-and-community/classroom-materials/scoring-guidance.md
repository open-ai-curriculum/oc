## Purpose

Provide simple criteria for interpreting helper-role evidence.

## Suggested Criteria

- Role identification:
  - `secure`: identifies a helper accurately
  - `developing`: identifies with picture or teacher support
  - `not yet`: does not connect a person to a helping role
- Explanation:
  - `secure`: describes how the helper contributes
  - `developing`: gives a partial explanation
  - `not yet`: cannot explain the helping role
