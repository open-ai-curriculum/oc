# Unit: Family, School, and Community

## Unit Metadata

- Unit title: Family, School, and Community
- Unit ID: `k-ss-family-community-unit`
- Subject: Social Studies
- Grade / grade band: Kindergarten
- Estimated duration: 2 weeks
- Review status: Draft

## Standards

- Primary standards:
  - K-SS-COMMUNITY
  - family, classroom, and school participation

## Unit Purpose

- What is the unit about?
  - Students learn that families, classrooms, and communities include people with different roles and responsibilities.
- Why does it matter for learners?
  - Kindergarten social studies should help students understand belonging, roles, and respectful participation in shared spaces.

## Desired Results

- Students should understand:
  - people help families, schools, and communities in different ways
  - rules and routines support shared life
- Students should be able to do:
  - name familiar community helpers or roles
  - describe one way people help at school or home

## Lesson Sequence

### Lesson 1

- objective: Identify people who help in school and community settings.
- key activities:
  - discuss familiar roles
  - match people to jobs or responsibilities
  - share one way someone helps

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- use inclusive family and community examples
- allow oral, picture-based, and gesture-supported responses

## Subject-Specific Quality Notes

- avoid narrow assumptions about family structure or community experience
- keep examples concrete and age-appropriate
