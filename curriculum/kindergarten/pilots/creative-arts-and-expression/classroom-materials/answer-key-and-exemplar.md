## Purpose

Provide guidance on what counts as acceptable artistic participation and sharing.

## Correct Or Acceptable Responses

- makes a creative response connected to the prompt
- shares one feature, idea, action, or feeling about the response

## Exemplar Student Responses

- "I made rain with blue lines."
- points to a drawing and says, "This is my song bird."

## Common Error Patterns

- creates without connection to the prompt
- cannot communicate any feature of the work even with support
