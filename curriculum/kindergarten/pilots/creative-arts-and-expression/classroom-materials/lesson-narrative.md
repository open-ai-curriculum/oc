## Purpose

Provide a teacher-ready routine for making and sharing an artistic response to a simple prompt.

## Teacher Narrative

- Introduce a prompt, song, image, or short story.
- Model one artistic response with drawing, collage, rhythm, or movement.
- Invite students to create their own response.
- End with a brief share where each student names one part of the work or what it shows.

## Assessment Notes

- Look for whether students create in response to the prompt and share one feature or idea.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Offer multiple modes for creating and sharing.
- Keep materials simple and physically accessible.
