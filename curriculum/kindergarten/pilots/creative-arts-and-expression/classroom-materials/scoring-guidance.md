## Purpose

Provide simple criteria for interpreting artistic participation and sharing.

## Suggested Criteria

- Creating:
  - `secure`: creates a response connected to the prompt
  - `developing`: creates with teacher support
  - `not yet`: does not yet engage in the creation task
- Sharing:
  - `secure`: communicates one feature or idea
  - `developing`: shares with prompting
  - `not yet`: does not yet communicate about the work
