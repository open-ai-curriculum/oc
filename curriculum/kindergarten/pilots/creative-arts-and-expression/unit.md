# Unit: Creative Arts and Expression

## Unit Metadata

- Unit title: Creative Arts and Expression
- Unit ID: `k-arts-expression-unit`
- Subject: Arts
- Grade / grade band: Kindergarten
- Estimated duration: 2 weeks
- Review status: Draft

## Standards

- Primary standards:
  - K-ARTS-CREATE
  - respond to music, art, or movement

## Unit Purpose

- What is the unit about?
  - Students create and share art, music, or movement responses to a simple idea or experience.
- Why does it matter for learners?
  - Kindergarten arts learning should support expression, noticing, and confidence through making and sharing.

## Desired Results

- Students should understand:
  - art can communicate ideas and feelings
  - people can make, notice, and talk about creative work
- Students should be able to do:
  - create a simple artistic response
  - describe something they made or noticed

## Lesson Sequence

### Lesson 1

- objective: Make and share art about a simple idea or experience.
- key activities:
  - look, listen, or move in response to a prompt
  - create with simple materials
  - share one thing about the work

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- allow varied materials, movement, and oral or nonverbal sharing
- avoid narrow cultural assumptions about art forms

## Subject-Specific Quality Notes

- keep expression meaningful rather than purely decorative
- review materials and prompts for inclusion and usability
