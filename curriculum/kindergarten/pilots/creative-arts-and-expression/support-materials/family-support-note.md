## Family Message

You can encourage creative expression in short, simple ways.

Try:

- Draw, sing, move, or build something small together.
- Ask, “What did you make?”
- Ask, “What part do you want to show me?”

## Accessibility Notes

- No internet or special materials are required.
- Everyday materials and movement are enough.
- Talking, showing, and gestures all count.
