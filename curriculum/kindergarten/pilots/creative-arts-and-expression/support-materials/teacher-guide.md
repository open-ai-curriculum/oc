## Alignment Notes

- This guide supports the unit goals around creating and sharing artistic responses.
- It keeps the work expressive rather than purely decorative.

## Teacher Preparation Notes

- Prepare simple accessible materials.
- Plan a short prompt, song, story, or image.
- Decide how students can share without requiring full verbal explanation.

## Likely Misconceptions Or Developmental Challenges

- Students may copy peers instead of making a choice.
- Some students may need alternate ways to share meaning.
- Overly narrow prompts can shut down expression.

## Recommended Teacher Moves

- Ask students to notice and make one choice.
- Accept varied artistic responses tied to the prompt.
- Revoice student descriptions without overcorrecting.

## Observation Guidance

- Notice whether students engage with the prompt.
- Track whether students make at least one creative choice.
- Note who needs support with materials, movement, or sharing.

## Accessibility Notes

- Use varied materials and modes.
- Allow movement, drawing, collage, or oral description.
- Avoid over-standardizing output.
