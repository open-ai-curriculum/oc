- Estimated duration: 25 minutes
- Review status: Draft

## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - K-ESS2-1
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will observe the weather and help record what they notice.

## Lesson Flow

- observe the weather or weather images
- describe what is seen using simple words
- add the weather to a class chart

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- use picture symbols, gestures, and repeated oral frames
- allow drawing and pointing
