# Unit: Weather and Observation

## Unit Metadata

- Unit title: Weather and Observation
- Unit ID: `k-sci-weather-observation-unit`
- Subject: Science
- Grade / grade band: Kindergarten
- Estimated duration: 2 weeks
- Review status: Draft

## Standards

- Primary standards:
  - K-ESS2-1
  - observation and pattern noticing

## Unit Purpose

- What is the unit about?
  - Students observe daily weather and describe simple patterns using words, pictures, and shared discussion.
- Why does it matter for learners?
  - Kindergarten science should build noticing, describing, and recording skills through real phenomena.

## Desired Results

- Students should understand:
  - weather can be observed and described
  - weather changes over time
- Students should be able to do:
  - notice daily weather conditions
  - describe what they see
  - help record a simple pattern over several days

## Lesson Sequence

### Lesson 1

- objective: Notice and describe daily weather.
- key activities:
  - observe outside conditions or weather images
  - talk about what is seen
  - record the weather with picture symbols

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- use visuals, gestures, repeated oral language, and shared charts
- allow pointing, drawing, and oral responses

## Subject-Specific Quality Notes

- keep the science tied to observation, not only vocabulary
- review visuals and explanations for accuracy
