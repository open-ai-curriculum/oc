## Alignment Notes

- This scaffold supports the lesson goal of noticing weather and recording what is seen.
- It preserves the science work of observing and describing.

## Student Directions

- Look outside or at the weather picture.
- Ask: What do I see?
- Pick the weather symbol.
- Say one thing about today’s weather.

## Accessibility Notes

- Use with picture cards, gestures, and oral modeling.
- Read directions aloud and pair with visuals.
- Keep the language short and concrete.
