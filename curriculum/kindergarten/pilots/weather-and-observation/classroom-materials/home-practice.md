## Purpose

Provide a brief home observation routine connected to daily weather talk.

## Directions For Families

- Look outside with your child.
- Ask, "What is the weather like today?"
- Choose one word such as sunny, cloudy, rainy, or windy.
- Talk about what helped your child know.

## Accessibility Notes

- Home language discussion is welcome.
- A window view or weather photo is enough.
