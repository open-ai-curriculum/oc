## Purpose

Provide guidance on acceptable weather observations and chart responses.

## Correct Or Acceptable Responses

- identifies an observable condition such as sun, clouds, rain, or wind
- points to or selects a matching weather icon
- contributes to the chart with teacher support

## Exemplar Student Responses

- "It is sunny."
- points to the cloud symbol
- "I see rain."

## Common Error Patterns

- names unrelated topics instead of weather
- guesses without attending to the observation source
