## Purpose

Provide a teacher-ready routine for observing daily weather and recording what students notice.

## Teacher Narrative

- Begin by asking students to look outside or at a weather image.
- Prompt with, "What do you notice in the sky? What do you feel?"
- Model one clear observation such as sunny, cloudy, rainy, or windy.
- Add the observation to a class chart and invite students to help describe the day.
- Close by comparing today's weather to another day if possible.

## Assessment Notes

- Look for whether students notice an observable condition and can describe it with a word, gesture, or picture support.
- Use the class chart as shared evidence.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Provide weather icons and repeated language frames.
- Accept pointing, drawing, speaking, or acting out weather conditions.
