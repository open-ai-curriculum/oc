## Purpose

Provide simple criteria for interpreting weather-observation evidence.

## Suggested Criteria

- Observation:
  - `secure`: notices and describes a relevant weather condition
  - `developing`: notices with prompting or picture support
  - `not yet`: cannot connect response to the observed weather
- Recording:
  - `secure`: helps match the weather to the chart
  - `developing`: participates with teacher support
  - `not yet`: does not yet engage with the shared record
