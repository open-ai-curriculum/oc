## Purpose

Define the live classroom task students complete to notice and record weather.

## Student Task

- Observe the weather or a weather photo.
- Choose a word, symbol, or picture to describe the weather.
- Help place the day's weather on a class chart.

## Expected Evidence Of Learning

- student notices one observable weather condition
- student contributes to the class record with support
