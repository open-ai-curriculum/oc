## How To Use

- Observe a small group each day if whole-class note taking is unrealistic.
- Record whether the student responds with speaking, gesture, picture placement, or a combination.
- Use the notes column for follow-up planning rather than permanent labels.
- Reuse the tool across several lessons to monitor growth in retell strength.

## Accessibility Notes

- The checklist is printable, editable, and low-bandwidth friendly.
- It supports quick notation during live instruction.
- It should be paired with multimodal student participation rather than replacing it.
