## Student Directions

### 1. Look at the story pictures

- Find the picture that happened first.
- Find what happened next.
- Find what happened last.

### 2. Tell the story

- Say: “First…”
- Say: “Next…”
- Say: “Last…”

### 3. Show what happened

- Point to the picture.
- Move the picture into order.
- Act out part of the story if that helps.
