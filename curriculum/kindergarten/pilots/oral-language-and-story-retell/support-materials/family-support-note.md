## Family Message

Talking about a story helps your child remember and understand it. A short conversation is enough.

Try these simple prompts:

- “What happened first?”
- “What happened next?”
- “What happened at the end?”
- “What was your favorite part?”
- “Can you show me with a picture or your hands?”

## Easy At-Home Ideas

- Retell a story from a book you read together.
- Talk about a favorite picture and what was happening there.
- Let your child answer with speaking, pointing, acting, or picture choice.
- Use your home language too. Retelling in any language supports comprehension and oral language growth.
