## Suggested Criteria

- Sequencing:
  - `secure`: retells events in order
  - `developing`: retells partially in order with support
  - `not yet`: does not yet sequence events
- Communication:
  - `secure`: communicates events clearly through one or more modes
  - `developing`: communicates with heavy prompting
  - `not yet`: does not yet communicate story events
