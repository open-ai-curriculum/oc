## Purpose

Provide a short home routine for talking through story events in order.

## Directions For Families

- Read or remember a familiar story together.
- Ask what happened first, next, and last.
- Let your child point, act, or tell the events.

## Accessibility Notes

- Home language retell is welcome.
- Oral storytelling is enough.
