## Purpose

Provide guidance on acceptable retell evidence.

## Correct Or Acceptable Responses

- orders major events correctly
- retells with words, pictures, gestures, or props

## Exemplar Student Responses

- "First he went home. Next he found the dog. Last they ate."
- points to ordered cards while retelling

## Common Error Patterns

- tells favorite parts out of order
- gives isolated details without sequence
