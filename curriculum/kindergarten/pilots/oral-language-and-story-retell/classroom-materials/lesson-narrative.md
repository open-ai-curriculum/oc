## Purpose

Provide a teacher-ready oral-language routine for retelling a familiar story in order.

## Teacher Narrative

- Revisit a familiar story and display key event cards.
- Model retelling the story with first, next, and last.
- Invite students to place event cards in order and retell with partners.
- Revoice student language to strengthen sequencing and story vocabulary.

## Assessment Notes

- Look for sequencing, oral participation, and use of picture or gesture support to retell key events.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Accept partial retells, gestures, puppets, and visuals.
- Use a highly familiar story with clear events.
