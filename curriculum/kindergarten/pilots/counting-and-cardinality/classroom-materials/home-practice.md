## Purpose

Provide a short home routine that reinforces counting a small set and saying how many.

## Directions For Families

- Find up to 10 small objects such as blocks, spoons, or socks.
- Ask your child to count each object once.
- Ask, "How many are there?"
- If you have two sets, ask which group has more.

## Accessibility Notes

- Any household objects work.
- Home language counting and discussion are welcome.
