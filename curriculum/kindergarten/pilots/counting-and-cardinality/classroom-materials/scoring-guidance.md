## Purpose

Provide simple criteria for interpreting counting evidence.

## Suggested Criteria

- Counting process:
  - `secure`: counts each object once
  - `developing`: counts with minor errors or prompting
  - `not yet`: cannot sustain one-to-one counting
- Naming the total:
  - `secure`: states the final number as the total
  - `developing`: counts correctly but needs prompting to state total
  - `not yet`: does not connect the final number to quantity
