## Purpose

Provide guidance on what counts as correct evidence during the live counting task.

## Correct Or Acceptable Responses

- touches or moves each object once while counting
- says the final number as the total
- compares two sets with correct informal language when asked

## Exemplar Student Responses

- "One, two, three, four. There are four."
- "This one has more."

## Common Error Patterns

- skips an object or counts one twice
- restarts counting without naming the total
- says a random number unrelated to the set
