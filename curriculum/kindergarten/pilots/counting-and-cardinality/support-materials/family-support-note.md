## Family Message

You can practice counting during everyday moments.

Try:

- Count 3 to 10 objects together.
- Touch each object one time.
- Ask, “How many are there?”
- Compare two small groups and ask which has more.

## Accessibility Notes

- No internet or special materials are required.
- Families can count toys, snacks, or household objects.
- Pointing and saying the numbers out loud both help.
