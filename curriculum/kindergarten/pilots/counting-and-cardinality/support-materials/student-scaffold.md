## Alignment Notes

- This scaffold supports the lesson goal of counting a set accurately and saying how many.
- It reduces confusion without replacing the math thinking.

## Student Directions

- Touch each object one time.
- Say the numbers in order.
- Say the last number again.
- Ask: “How many?”
- Look at two groups.
- Tell which has more, less, or the same.

## Accessibility Notes

- Use with real objects, pictures, or counters.
- Read directions aloud and pair with gestures.
- Keep the language short and repeatable.
