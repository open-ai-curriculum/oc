# Unit 3 Assessment Calibration

## Assessment Recording Form

Record each student on:

- shows a number in two ways
- identifies parts and whole
- finds a missing part with objects
- explains a representation

For each area record:

- secure
- developing
- not yet
- support used

## Interpretation Notes

- accept object movement and pointing as valid evidence
- do not penalize expressive-language brevity when quantity reasoning is clear
