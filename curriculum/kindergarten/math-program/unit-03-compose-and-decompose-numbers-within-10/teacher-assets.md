# Unit 3 Teacher Assets

## Weekly Teacher Overview

### Week 9

- priority:
  - show small numbers in more than one way
- emphasize:
  - same total, different parts

### Week 10

- priority:
  - move from object models to drawings and ten-frames
- emphasize:
  - represent the same quantity across formats

### Week 11

- priority:
  - explain two ways and find missing parts
- emphasize:
  - have students justify with objects first

### Week 12

- priority:
  - consolidate part-whole routines and gather evidence
- emphasize:
  - use familiar mats and prompts

## Preparation Notes

- prepare part-whole mats and ten-frames
- keep counters available for every lesson
- preselect numbers that are developmentally manageable

## Family Communication Asset

This month students are showing numbers in different ways and talking about parts and whole. Families can help by asking, “Can you show 5 in another way?”

## Intervention And Extension

### Intervention

- return to numbers within 5
- use objects before drawings

### Extension

- compare two representations of the same number
- ask students to explain how they know the total stayed the same
