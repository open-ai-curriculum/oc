# Unit 4 Teacher Assets

## Weekly Teacher Overview

### Week 13

- priority:
  - identify, name, and sort common shapes
- emphasize:
  - describe shapes by what they have, not only how they look

### Week 14

- priority:
  - connect shapes to the environment and introduce positional language
- emphasize:
  - use real objects and student movement

### Week 15

- priority:
  - compose larger shapes and describe positions more precisely
- emphasize:
  - build, move, and explain with concrete materials

### Week 16

- priority:
  - review and gather benchmark evidence
- emphasize:
  - use familiar shape sets and stable positional prompts

## Preparation Notes

- prepare shape cards and cutouts in multiple orientations
- use a consistent positional-language visual chart
- stage build trays or mats before composition lessons

## Family Communication Asset

This month students are learning about shapes and position. Families can help by naming shapes around the home and using words like above, below, beside, and behind.

## Intervention And Extension

### Intervention

- reduce the number of shapes in play
- return to real objects before picture cards

### Extension

- ask students to justify a shape sort rule
- challenge students to build a larger picture from multiple smaller shapes
