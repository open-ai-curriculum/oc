# Unit 5 Teacher Assets

## Weekly Teacher Overview

### Week 17

- priority:
  - launch addition as joining and putting together
- emphasize:
  - act it out before drawing or recording

### Week 18

- priority:
  - deepen add-to situations and explain what changes
- emphasize:
  - total after joining, not just recounting mechanically

### Week 19

- priority:
  - compare addition stories and connect them to teacher-supported equations
- emphasize:
  - same total can be represented in different story forms

### Week 20

- priority:
  - consolidate addition routines and gather evidence
- emphasize:
  - use familiar props and stable prompts

## Preparation Notes

- prepare simple story mats and story props
- keep counters and ten-frames available every day
- preselect story contexts that are familiar and concrete

## Family Communication Asset

This month students are learning to show addition stories by putting groups together. Families can help by acting out small joining stories with toys or snacks and asking how many there are altogether.

## Intervention And Extension

### Intervention

- return to smaller totals and act out the story physically
- keep the two groups visually separated before joining

### Extension

- compare two stories that lead to the same total
- have students explain an addition story using both counters and a drawing
