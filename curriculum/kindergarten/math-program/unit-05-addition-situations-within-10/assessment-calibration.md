# Unit 5 Assessment Calibration

## Assessment Recording Form

Record each student on:

- models an addition story
- shows the total after joining
- explains what happened
- connects a story to a drawing or ten-frame

For each area record:

- secure
- developing
- not yet
- support used

## Interpretation Notes

- accept acting, pointing, and teacher-recorded language as valid evidence
- do not treat symbolic equation writing as required for secure conceptual understanding
