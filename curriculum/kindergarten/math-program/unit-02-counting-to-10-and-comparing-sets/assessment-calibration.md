# Unit 2 Assessment Calibration

## Assessment Recording Form

Record each student on:

- counts within 10 accurately
- matches numeral to quantity
- compares two sets
- shows quantity on a ten-frame

For each area record:

- secure
- developing
- not yet
- support used

## Decision Rules

- secure:
  - student completes the task with routine teacher language only
- developing:
  - student completes the task with visible prompts or reduced complexity
- not yet:
  - student cannot demonstrate the skill even with routine supports

## Interpretation Notes

- note whether arrangement changes counting accuracy
- distinguish numeral-recognition difficulty from quantity understanding
