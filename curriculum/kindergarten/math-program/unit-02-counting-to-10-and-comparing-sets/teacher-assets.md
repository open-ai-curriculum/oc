# Unit 2 Teacher Assets

## Weekly Teacher Overview

### Week 5

- priority:
  - extend count sequences from 5 to 10
- emphasize:
  - keep one-to-one correspondence stable as the count grows

### Week 6

- priority:
  - connect numerals, quantities, and comparison within 10
- emphasize:
  - compare by matching or counting

### Week 7

- priority:
  - represent 10 on a ten-frame and sort/count categories
- emphasize:
  - ten-frame as quantity support, not speed drill

### Week 8

- priority:
  - review and gather benchmark evidence
- emphasize:
  - use familiar materials and routines

## Preparation Notes

- prepare ten-frames and numeral cards to 10
- build sets with varied arrangements
- use consistent compare mats across the unit

## Family Communication Asset

This month students are counting to 10, matching numbers to quantities, and comparing two groups. Families can help by counting small sets at home and asking which group has more, less, or the same number.

## Intervention And Extension

### Intervention

- return to sets within 5 if one-to-one counting breaks down
- use lined or framed spaces before scattered sets

### Extension

- ask students to compare and explain two sets with the same total arranged differently
- ask students to show the same number on a ten-frame and with loose objects
