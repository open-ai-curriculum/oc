# Unit 9 Assessment Calibration

## Assessment Recording Form

Record each student on:

- counts and compares quantities with stable routines
- represents addition, subtraction, and teen numbers with familiar tools
- explains at least one math idea with pictures, objects, or teacher-recorded language
- shows readiness evidence across center review and benchmark tasks

For each area record:

- secure
- developing
- not yet
- support used

## Interpretation Notes

- accept multimodal evidence across benchmark, conference, center, and reflection settings
- do not let one weaker domain erase stronger evidence from another
- prioritize consistent conceptual understanding over speed or worksheet completion
