# Unit 9 Lesson Teacher Narratives

## KMATH-129 Review Counting And Comparison

Revisit early counting and comparison routines with materials students know well. Watch for stable one-to-one correspondence and clear quantity language.

## KMATH-130 Review Compose And Decompose

Use part-whole tasks and ten-frames to see whether students still recognize that the same number can be made in different ways.

## KMATH-131 Review Shapes And Position

Return to shape naming, attributes, and positional language through quick build-and-describe tasks.

## KMATH-132 Review Addition Stories

Use familiar joining stories so students can model and explain addition without new context demands.

## KMATH-133 Review Subtraction Stories

Ask students to act out and solve take-away situations, keeping the starting amount and change visible.

## KMATH-134 Review Teen Numbers

Rebuild teen numbers as one ten and some ones to confirm that place-value meaning remains stable late in the year.

## KMATH-135 Review Measurement And Data

Blend direct comparison and simple graph interpretation using familiar classroom materials and categories.

## KMATH-136 Solve A Mixed Problem Set With Support

Present a short set of mixed tasks and allow students to choose tools. This shows transfer, not just repetition.

## KMATH-137 Explain A Math Idea With Pictures And Words

Invite students to choose one math idea they know and explain it with a drawing, objects, and oral language.

## KMATH-138 Final Benchmark Practice

Practice the final benchmark routine with calm, familiar prompts and clear expectations.

## KMATH-139 Final Benchmark Window

Collect benchmark evidence while keeping teacher language minimal and supports consistent.

## KMATH-140 Transition Review Conference

Meet briefly with students to notice strengths, supports still needed, and Grade 1 readiness patterns.

## KMATH-141 Center Review: Counting And Comparison

Use a center rotation focused on counting, matching numerals, and set comparison. Record what students do without heavy prompting.

## KMATH-142 Center Review: Operations

Use addition and subtraction story centers with objects, drawings, and oral explanation.

## KMATH-143 Center Review: Shapes And Measurement

Use a geometry and measurement center to review shape attributes, direct comparison, and simple data talk.

## KMATH-144 End-Of-Year Math Reflection

Close the year by asking students to reflect on what they can do in math now and how they show their thinking.
