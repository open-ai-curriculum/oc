# Unit 9: Cumulative Review And Grade 1 Transition

## Purpose

This package is the ninth fully authored unit in the Kindergarten Math commercialization build.

It consolidates the year's major math ideas through mixed review, center-based practice, benchmark evidence, and transition-oriented readiness tasks for Grade 1.
