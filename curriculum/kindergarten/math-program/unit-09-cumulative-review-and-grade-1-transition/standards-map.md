# Unit 9 Standards Map

## Primary Alignment

- `CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.CC.A.1`
  - Count to 100 by ones and by tens.
- `CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.CC.B.4`
  - Understand the relationship between numbers and quantities.
- `CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.OA.A.1`
  - Represent addition and subtraction with objects, fingers, mental images, drawings, sounds, acting situations, verbal explanations, expressions, or equations.
- `CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.OA.A.2`
  - Solve addition and subtraction word problems, and add and subtract within 10.
- `CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.NBT.A.1`
  - Compose and decompose numbers from 11 to 19 into ten ones and some further ones.
- `CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.MD.A.2`
  - Directly compare two objects with a measurable attribute in common.
- `CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.MD.B.3`
  - Classify objects into given categories, count the numbers in each category, and sort the categories by count.
- `CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.G.B.4`
  - Analyze and compare two- and three-dimensional shapes.

## Supporting Alignment

- `CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.CC.B.5`
  - Count to answer how many.
- `CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.OA.A.3`
  - Decompose numbers less than or equal to 10 into pairs in more than one way.
- `CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.G.A.2`
  - Correctly name shapes regardless of their orientations or size.

## Lesson Coverage

- `KMATH-129` to `KMATH-136`
  - mixed review across counting, composition, geometry, operations, teen numbers, and measurement/data
- `KMATH-137` to `KMATH-140`
  - explanation tasks, final benchmark practice, benchmark window, and transition conference
- `KMATH-141` to `KMATH-144`
  - center review and end-of-year readiness reflection

## Review Notes

- Final specialist review should confirm that cumulative tasks maintain domain balance and avoid overrepresenting literacy demand in readiness judgments.
