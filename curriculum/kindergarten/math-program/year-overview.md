# Kindergarten Math Year Overview

## Purpose

Define the full-year Kindergarten Math spine for the repository's commercial-grade build.

## Annual Model

- 36 instructional weeks
- daily math block
- recurring review and center routines
- 4 benchmark windows including end-of-year synthesis

## Year Arc

### Unit Band 1. Counting, Quantities, And Math Routines

Weeks 1-5

- count to 5 and then 10
- one-to-one correspondence
- answer "how many?"
- compare small sets
- sort and classify objects
- launch math talk, tool use, and recording routines

### Unit Band 2. Numbers To 10 And Composition

Weeks 6-10

- count forward within 10
- represent quantities in different ways
- compose and decompose small numbers
- compare groups as more, less, and equal
- use ten-frame and picture-set routines

### Unit Band 3. Shapes, Position, And Attributes

Weeks 11-14

- describe and compare shapes
- sort by attributes
- use positional language
- build and compose shapes

### Unit Band 4. Addition And Subtraction Situations Within 10

Weeks 15-20

- model put-together, take-apart, add-to, and take-from situations
- act out and draw stories
- connect concrete representations to equations with teacher support

### Unit Band 5. Teen Numbers And Base-Ten Foundations

Weeks 21-25

- count beyond 10
- understand teen numbers as ten and some ones
- represent teen quantities with concrete materials

### Unit Band 6. Measurement, Data, And Comparison

Weeks 26-30

- compare length, weight, and other measurable attributes informally
- collect simple class data
- interpret and discuss simple data displays

### Unit Band 7. Review, Problem Solving, And Transition To Grade 1

Weeks 31-36

- cumulative review
- mixed problem situations
- shape and number review
- final benchmark and Grade 1 readiness notes

## Program Priorities

- concrete representation before abstraction
- repeated math-talk routines
- visual and tactile models
- daily opportunities to count, compare, explain, and represent
- strong connection between student action and teacher interpretation
