# Kindergarten Math Unit Structure

## Purpose

Define the full-year unit structure for the Kindergarten Math commercialization build.

## Unit Model

The annual Kindergarten Math program will use 9 units across 36 weeks.

## Annual Unit Structure

### Unit 1. Launching Math Routines And Counting To 5

- weeks: 1-4
- lesson target: 16-20
- emphasis:
  - counting objects
  - one-to-one correspondence
  - math tools and discussion routines

### Unit 2. Counting To 10 And Comparing Sets

- weeks: 5-8
- lesson target: 16-20
- emphasis:
  - count to 10
  - compare groups
  - sort, classify, and explain

### Unit 3. Compose And Decompose Numbers Within 10

- weeks: 9-12
- lesson target: 16-20
- emphasis:
  - parts and wholes
  - represent the same quantity in multiple ways
  - ten-frame readiness

### Unit 4. Shapes And Positional Reasoning

- weeks: 13-16
- lesson target: 16-20
- emphasis:
  - identify and describe shapes
  - build and sort by attributes
  - use positional language

### Unit 5. Addition Situations Within 10

- weeks: 17-20
- lesson target: 16-20
- emphasis:
  - put-together and add-to situations
  - drawings, counters, and stories

### Unit 6. Subtraction Situations Within 10

- weeks: 21-24
- lesson target: 16-20
- emphasis:
  - take-apart and take-from situations
  - missing-part thinking with support

### Unit 7. Teen Numbers And Base-Ten Foundations

- weeks: 25-28
- lesson target: 16-20
- emphasis:
  - ten and some ones
  - counting beyond 10
  - teen-number representation

### Unit 8. Measurement And Data

- weeks: 29-32
- lesson target: 16-20
- emphasis:
  - compare measurable attributes
  - collect and represent simple data

### Unit 9. Cumulative Review And Grade 1 Transition

- weeks: 33-36
- lesson target: 12-16
- emphasis:
  - review and consolidation
  - mixed problem solving
  - readiness evidence

## Unit Artifact Expectations

Each future math unit should eventually include:

- `unit.md`
- `standards-map.md`
- `teacher-assets.md`
- `lesson-teacher-narratives.md`
- `manipulative-plan.md`
- `assessment-calibration.md`
- `reproducibles.md`
- `lessons/`
