# Unit 8 Lesson Teacher Narratives

## KMATH-113 Compare Two Objects By Length

Use two visibly different objects and align them at one end. Keep the comparison direct and physical.

## KMATH-114 Compare Two Objects By Weight

Invite students to hold or balance two classroom-safe objects and describe which feels heavier or lighter.

## KMATH-115 Compare Two Objects By Another Measurable Attribute

Use another shared attribute such as height or capacity-like comparison with visible materials. Keep the language informal and concrete.

## KMATH-116 Use Informal Language For Measurement Comparison

Rehearse words such as longer, shorter, heavier, lighter, and taller through repeated comparison routines.

## KMATH-117 Sort Data From A Class Question

Ask a familiar class question and have students place themselves, cards, or objects into categories to create live data.

## KMATH-118 Build A Simple Picture Graph

Turn sorted data into a visible picture graph. Each picture should clearly stand for one response.

## KMATH-119 Ask And Answer A Question About Class Data

Keep graph questions concrete: "Which has more?" "Which has fewer?" "How many chose ___?"

## KMATH-120 Compare Categories In A Simple Graph

Invite students to compare category sizes by counting and by visual inspection of the graph.

## KMATH-121 Measure With Nonstandard Units

Demonstrate how to place the same unit end to end without gaps or overlaps. Keep the task physical.

## KMATH-122 Compare Measurement Results

Discuss why one object uses more or fewer nonstandard units. Connect the count to the object's attribute.

## KMATH-123 Review Data Routines

Run a quick cycle of ask, sort, count, and graph using familiar categories.

## KMATH-124 Review Measurement Routines

Revisit direct comparison and nonstandard measurement with different classroom objects.

## KMATH-125 Explain What The Graph Shows

Ask students to describe the graph in complete ideas using pointing, oral language, or teacher-recorded responses.

## KMATH-126 Unit 8 Performance Check

Use one comparison task and one graph question to gather stable evidence from concrete materials.

## KMATH-127 Practice Mixed Review

Blend comparison, graph, and measurement routines in familiar formats to prepare for the unit check.

## KMATH-128 Unit 8 Check

Record whether students can compare, sort, graph, and interpret with routine prompts and accessible materials.
