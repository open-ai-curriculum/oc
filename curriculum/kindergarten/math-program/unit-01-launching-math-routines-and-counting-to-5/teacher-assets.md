# Unit 1 Teacher Assets

## Weekly Teacher Overview

### Week 1

- priority:
  - launch math space, counting tools, and number words to 5
- emphasize:
  - touch one object at a time
  - say the number words in order
  - answer “how many?” after counting

### Week 2

- priority:
  - connect counting to sorting and matching quantity
- emphasize:
  - sort by one rule at a time
  - show quantity in multiple concrete ways
  - begin short explanations of how many

### Week 3

- priority:
  - strengthen counting when objects are scattered and compare equal/not equal
- emphasize:
  - organize sets before counting when needed
  - compare by matching or counting, not only by visual guess

### Week 4

- priority:
  - consolidate routines and gather evidence
- emphasize:
  - use the same materials used during instruction
  - note support levels during the unit check

## Preparation Notes

- prepare daily count collections in bags or trays
- keep comparison mats and numeral cards ready
- use one consistent counting mat across the unit
- preselect sort categories that are obvious and concrete

## Family Communication Asset

This month students are learning how math time works by counting small groups, answering “how many?”, and comparing two sets. Families can help by counting snacks, toys, or steps and asking which group has more or less.

## Intervention And Extension

### Intervention

- reduce the set size to 3
- arrange objects in a line before scattering them
- let students move objects into a counted space

### Extension

- ask students to show the same quantity in two ways
- ask how they know two sets are equal
