# Lesson: Compare Two Small Sets As More Or Less

## Lesson Metadata

- Lesson title: Compare Two Small Sets As More Or Less
- Lesson ID: `KMATH-004`
- Unit: `k-math-unit-01`
- Subject: Mathematics
- Grade / grade band: Kindergarten
- Estimated duration: 25 minutes
- Review status: Draft

## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.CC.C.6
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will compare two small sets and say which has more or less.

## Lesson Flow

- opening:
  - review count and total
- core learning:
  - compare two sets by matching or counting
- check for understanding:
  - identify which set has more or less

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- keep set sizes small and concrete
- allow rearranging objects before comparing
