# Lesson: Count Up To 3 Objects And Say How Many

## Lesson Metadata

- Lesson title: Count Up To 3 Objects And Say How Many
- Lesson ID: `KMATH-001`
- Unit: `k-math-unit-01`
- Subject: Mathematics
- Grade / grade band: Kindergarten
- Estimated duration: 25 minutes
- Review status: Draft

## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.CC.A.1
  - CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.CC.B.4
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will count up to 3 objects and say how many are in the set.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - counters or small classroom objects
  - counting mat to 3
- Teacher preparation:
  - prepare several sets of 1 to 3 objects

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Model touching and counting 3 objects slowly.

### Core Learning Experience

- Students count small sets with teacher guidance.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Ask, “How many?” after each set is counted.

### Check for Understanding

- Students count one set independently or with routine support.

### Closing

- Review that the last number tells how many.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- use spaced objects and allow touching or moving objects
- accept oral or teacher-recorded responses

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson formatively assesses counting small sets with one-to-one correspondence.
