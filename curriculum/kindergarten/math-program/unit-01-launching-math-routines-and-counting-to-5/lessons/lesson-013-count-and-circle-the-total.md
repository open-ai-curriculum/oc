## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:

- Lesson objective:
  - Students will count a set and circle the numeral that matches the total.

## Lesson Flow

- opening:
  - review saying the total after counting
- core learning:
  - count a set and choose the matching numeral
- check for understanding:
  - complete one count-and-circle item
