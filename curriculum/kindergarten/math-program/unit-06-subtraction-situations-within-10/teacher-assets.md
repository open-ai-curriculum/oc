# Unit 6 Teacher Assets

## Weekly Teacher Overview

### Week 21

- priority:
  - launch subtraction as taking away and finding what is left
- emphasize:
  - visible change before symbolic recording

### Week 22

- priority:
  - deepen subtraction reasoning and compare story structures
- emphasize:
  - separate subtraction stories from addition stories clearly

### Week 23

- priority:
  - connect subtraction stories to teacher-supported number sentences
- emphasize:
  - preserve story meaning while recording

### Week 24

- priority:
  - consolidate subtraction routines and gather evidence
- emphasize:
  - use familiar materials and stable prompts

## Preparation Notes

- prepare take-away story mats and counters
- keep concrete props and drawings available
- use small totals and clear story action

## Family Communication Asset

This month students are learning subtraction stories by taking some away and finding what is left. Families can help by acting out small take-away stories with toys or snacks.

## Intervention And Extension

### Intervention

- use very small starting totals
- act out and remove objects physically before drawing

### Extension

- compare an addition and subtraction story with the same numbers
- ask students to explain how the total changed
