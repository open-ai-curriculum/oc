# Unit 6 Assessment Calibration

## Assessment Recording Form

Record each student on:

- models a subtraction story
- shows how many are left
- explains what changed
- connects a story to a drawing or equation with support

For each area record:

- secure
- developing
- not yet
- support used

## Interpretation Notes

- accept acting, pointing, and teacher-recorded language as valid evidence
- do not require equation writing for secure conceptual understanding
