# Unit 7 Standards Map

## Primary Alignment

- `CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.CC.A.1`
  - Count to 100 by ones and by tens.
- `CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.CC.A.2`
  - Count forward beginning from a given number within the known sequence.
- `CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.CC.A.3`
  - Write numbers from 0 to 20 and represent a number of objects with a written numeral.
- `CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.CC.B.4`
  - Understand the relationship between numbers and quantities.
- `CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.NBT.A.1`
  - Compose and decompose numbers from 11 to 19 into ten ones and some further ones.

## Supporting Alignment

- `CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.CC.B.5`
  - Count to answer how many.
- `CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.K.OA.A.3`
  - Decompose numbers less than or equal to 10 into pairs in more than one way.

## Lesson Coverage

- `KMATH-097` to `KMATH-102`
  - counting beyond 10 and building specific teen numbers
- `KMATH-103` to `KMATH-108`
  - matching teen numerals, explaining ten and some ones, comparing teen quantities
- `KMATH-109` to `KMATH-112`
  - benchmark-style practice, performance task, and unit check

## Review Notes

- Final specialist review should confirm the balance between counting-sequence work and explicit `K.NBT.A.1` place-value work.
