# Unit 7 Manipulative Plan

## Core Materials

- full and partial ten-frames
- linking cubes
- bundled sticks or base-ten-style ten groups
- numeral cards 11-19
- comparison mats

## Use Guidelines

- keep one full ten visible in every teen-number lesson
- vary the extra ones while keeping the ten stable
- place numeral cards beside concrete models only after students build the quantity first
