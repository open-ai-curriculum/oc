## Module-Level Notes

- Reading Foundations emphasis is strongest in book handling, page orientation, and concept of word.
- Reading Literature emphasis appears through read-aloud comprehension and retell.
- Speaking and Listening runs through every lesson as a central kindergarten literacy mode.
- Language expectations remain embedded and oral rather than separate grammar instruction.
