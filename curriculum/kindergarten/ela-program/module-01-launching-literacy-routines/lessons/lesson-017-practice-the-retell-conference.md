## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - sequence cards
  - retell stem strip
  - teacher conference clipboard
- Teacher preparation:
  - set up a model conference routine

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Explain that today students will practice showing what they know in a short teacher conference.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model the conference steps:
  - look at the cards
  - put them in order
  - tell first, next, last

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students practice in pairs or small groups.
- Teacher coaches one small group at a time.

### Check for Understanding

- Students complete a supported sequence-and-retell routine.

### Closing

- Reassure students that conferences are a way to show learning, not to trick them.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep the conference model predictable.
- Allow gestures and pointing during retell.
- Use the same story and cards students already know.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson prepares students for the end-of-module summative conference.
- Evidence matches the objective because students rehearse the exact performance format.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep the routine low-stress.
- Focus on sequence and basic meaning, not full expressive sentences.
