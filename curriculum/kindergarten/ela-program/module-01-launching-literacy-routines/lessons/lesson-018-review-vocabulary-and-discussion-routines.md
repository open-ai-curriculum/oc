## Standards and Objective

  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.SL.K.2
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.L.K.6
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will review module vocabulary and discussion routines by using words from the story during partner talk and whole-group sharing.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - vocabulary picture cards
  - discussion stems
  - familiar story pages
- Teacher preparation:
  - choose four to six high-utility module words

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Review the vocabulary cards and have students repeat the words.

### Core Learning Experience

- Revisit story pages connected to the words.
- Model using a word in a simple oral sentence.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students use partner talk to complete a stem such as "I see ___" or "The ___ is ___."
- Share a few examples with the whole group.

### Check for Understanding

- Students use at least one module word in a supported oral exchange.

### Closing

- Review that words from books help us talk and think about stories.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Pair each word with a picture.
- Allow students to point if oral language is limited.
- Repeat stems and keep them short.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses oral vocabulary use and discussion participation.
- Evidence matches the objective because students use the words during live talk.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Prioritize words worth reusing across lessons.
- Avoid isolated memorization without story connection.
