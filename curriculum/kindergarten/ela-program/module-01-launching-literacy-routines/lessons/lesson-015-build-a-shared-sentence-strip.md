## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.1
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.L.K.1
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will help build and reread a short shared sentence strip, noticing words and spaces.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - sentence strip with words on cards
  - pointer
  - picture from a familiar story
- Teacher preparation:
  - prepare a simple sentence such as "We read a book."

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Show the familiar picture and say the sentence aloud.

### Core Learning Experience

- Build the sentence strip with students one word at a time.
- Read the strip chorally while pointing to each word.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Mix up the words and rebuild them together.
- Ask students where one word ends and the next begins.

### Check for Understanding

- Students help place one word and identify one space between words.

### Closing

- Read the sentence one final time and celebrate that the class made shared print together.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use a very short sentence.
- Pair spoken rehearsal with the printed sentence.
- Let students move the cards physically when useful.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses early print awareness and shared language-to-print connection.
- Evidence matches the objective because students manipulate and reread real print.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep the sentence meaningful and familiar.
- Do not overemphasize isolated terminology over participation in shared print.
