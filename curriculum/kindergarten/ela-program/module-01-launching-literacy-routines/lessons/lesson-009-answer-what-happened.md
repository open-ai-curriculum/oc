## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RL.K.1
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.SL.K.3
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will answer a simple what happened question about a familiar read-aloud using story evidence.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - familiar read-aloud text
  - two or three event pictures
- Teacher preparation:
  - prepare one clear question tied to a visible event

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Remind students that story pictures and words help us answer questions.

### Core Learning Experience

- Reread the relevant part of the text.
- Ask, "What happened when ___?"

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students look at event pictures and select the one that matches.
- Partners explain their choice with support.

### Check for Understanding

- Students answer the question orally, by pointing, or with a picture choice.

### Closing

- Reflect that readers go back to the story to answer.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use only one question focus.
- Provide visual answer options.
- Accept one-word responses and gestures.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses comprehension anchored to key events.
- Evidence matches the objective because students use the story and images.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep answer options plausible but not confusing.
- Avoid generic "favorite part" questions when the target is key details.
