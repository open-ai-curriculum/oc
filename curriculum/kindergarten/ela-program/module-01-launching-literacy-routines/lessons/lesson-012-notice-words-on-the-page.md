## Standards and Objective

  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.1.B
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.1.C
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will notice that spoken words match written words in a short shared text and that spaces separate words.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - short patterned chart text
  - pointer
  - word-cut strip or sticky note markers
- Teacher preparation:
  - select a repetitive line students can echo

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Echo-read the chart text once for familiarity.

### Core Learning Experience

- Point under each word as the line is reread.
- Show that spaces help readers see where one word ends and the next begins.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students clap once for each spoken word in the short line.
- Compare the claps to the pointed words on the chart.

### Check for Understanding

- Students show one space between words and track the line with support.

### Closing

- Repeat: words have spaces between them.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep the line short and repeated.
- Use clear spacing and large font.
- Support with oral echo and movement.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses beginning concept of word.
- Evidence matches the objective through direct interaction with shared text.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Use meaningful repeated text, not nonsense strings.
- Do not turn the lesson into isolated word counting only.
