## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - familiar book
  - sample page for print tracking
  - sequence cards
  - teacher recording sheet
- Teacher preparation:
  - set up conference stations or staggered small-group checks

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Explain the conference tasks in simple language and model one example.

### Core Learning Experience

- Students rotate through short conference checks with the teacher:
  - show front cover and title
  - show where reading begins
  - place sequence cards in order
  - answer one story question

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- While students wait, they complete quiet partner retell or book-handling review routines.

### Check for Understanding

- Teacher records evidence for each student on the conference sheet.

### Closing

- Celebrate the learning students can now show about books, stories, and listening.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep prompts consistent and concrete.
- Allow gesture, pointing, oral language, and teacher restatement.
- Provide familiar materials only.
