## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - familiar anchor book
  - quick review cards
  - big-book or chart text
- Teacher preparation:
  - prepare a fast-paced review sequence

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Tell students they will show what they already know about books and print.

### Core Learning Experience

- Move through a quick review:
  - front cover
  - title
  - where reading begins
  - where the eyes move

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students rotate through fast response prompts using gestures, pointing, and oral answers.
- Reread one short familiar line of shared text.

### Check for Understanding

- Students demonstrate at least two print concepts accurately.

### Closing

- Name how much the class already knows about how books work.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use short bursts and clear visuals.
- Provide movement breaks between prompts.
- Accept multimodal responses.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Use actual books and text, not abstract quiz-only items.
