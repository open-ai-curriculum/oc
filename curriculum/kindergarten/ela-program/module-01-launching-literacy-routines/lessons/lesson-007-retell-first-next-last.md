## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RL.K.2
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.SL.K.2
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will retell a familiar story using first, next, and last with picture support.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - familiar narrative text
  - three large sequence cards
  - sentence stem cards
- Teacher preparation:
  - choose a story with clear three-part sequence

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Review that stories happen in an order we can tell again.

### Core Learning Experience

- Reread the story briefly.
- Model placing three sequence cards in order.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students help place events under first, next, and last.
- Partners practice a short retell using the stems.

### Check for Understanding

- Students orally or gesturally retell one event in sequence.

### Closing

- Say the sequence words together and point to the cards.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use clear picture cards and repeated modeling.
- Permit pointing or acting out events.
- Keep oral language load short and scaffolded.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses early retell sequencing.
- Evidence matches the objective because students sequence events from a real story.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Focus on key events, not every minor detail.
- Keep the story familiar enough for success.
