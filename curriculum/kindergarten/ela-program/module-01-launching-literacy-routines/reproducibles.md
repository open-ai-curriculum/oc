# Module 1 Reproducible Masters

## Purpose

This file defines the draft reproducible masters for Module 1.

These assets support book handling, print concepts, retell routines, discussion, and end-of-module conferencing.

## Included Reproducibles

- book-parts picture card set
- front-cover / back-cover sorting cards
- first / next / last sequence cards
- story-question response strip
- concept-of-word sentence strip
- end-of-module literacy conference form
- family reading note

## Reproducible Master: Book-Parts Picture Card Set

Cards:

- front cover
- back cover
- title
- author
- illustrator
- page
- word

Teacher use:

- display during whole-group modeling
- use as a matching or naming routine in small group

## Reproducible Master: Front-Cover / Back-Cover Sorting Cards

Directions:

- Cut apart the book images.
- Ask students to place each card under:
  - front cover
  - back cover

## Reproducible Master: First / Next / Last Sequence Cards

Directions:

- Place the three picture cards in story order.
- Ask:
  - What happened first?
  - What happened next?
  - What happened last?

## Reproducible Master: Story-Question Response Strip

- I noticed: __________
- I wonder: __________
- My question: __________

Teacher note:

- students may point, draw, dictate, or speak while the teacher records

## Reproducible Master: Concept-Of-Word Sentence Strip

Sentence frame:

- We read a book.

Teacher use:

- cut into word cards for word-by-word reconstruction
- use pointer tracking before and after reconstruction

## Reproducible Master: Family Reading Note

This week we are practicing how books work.

Try this at home:

- point to the front cover
- name the title
- ask what happened first, next, and last
- let your child point, act, or tell the story in any language
