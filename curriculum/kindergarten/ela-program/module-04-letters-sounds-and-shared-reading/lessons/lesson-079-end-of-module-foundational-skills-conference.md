## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:

  - letter cards
  - picture cards
  - shared text
  - labeling task
- Teacher preparation:
  - prepare a four-part conference routine

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Explain the conference steps in simple language and model one example.

### Core Learning Experience

- Students rotate through teacher checks:
  - name/match taught letters
  - connect one letter to a common sound and picture
  - track a short line of text
  - contribute one letter or sound to a label

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Other students complete familiar review tasks while waiting.

### Check for Understanding

- Teacher records performance evidence for each student.

### Closing

- Celebrate the class's growth in letters, sounds, and shared reading.
