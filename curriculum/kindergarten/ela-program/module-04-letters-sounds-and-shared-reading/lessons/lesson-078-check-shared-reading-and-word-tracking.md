## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - familiar chart text
  - pointer
- Teacher preparation:
  - mark a short line for students to track

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Review how readers track shared text together.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model one final tracking example.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students come up individually or in pairs to point to a line, a word, and a space.

### Check for Understanding

- Students show where the line begins, track across, and identify one word or space.

### Closing

- Review that shared reading helps us follow text carefully.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep the text highly familiar.
- Use large print and clear spacing.
- Allow air-tracking first if needed.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson is a focused formative check on shared-reading routines.
- Evidence matches the objective through direct print interaction.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Avoid using unfamiliar text for the check.
- Focus on target print behaviors, not speed.
