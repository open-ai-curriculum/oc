## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.3.A
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.3.B
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will connect taught focus letters to their common sounds using pictures and oral examples.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - focus-letter cards
  - picture cards
- Teacher preparation:
  - prepare one clear picture example per letter

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Review letter names before introducing the sound connections.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model the letter, its common sound, and a matching picture.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students match pictures to the taught letters and repeat the sound cue.

### Check for Understanding

- Students correctly connect one picture to a focus letter/sound.

### Closing

- Review the letter-sound pairings together.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use familiar vocabulary and concrete pictures.
- Keep the number of focus sounds limited.
- Accept pointing or repeating as evidence.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses supported letter-sound correspondence.
- Evidence matches the objective through direct matching and oral response.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Avoid letters with multiple competing common sounds in the same lesson.
- Use picture examples that are linguistically stable.
