## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.1.A-C
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.W.K.2
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will reread shared labels and revise them by checking sounds and words together.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - labels from Lesson 074
  - matching pictures
- Teacher preparation:
  - choose one label to revise lightly with the class

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Revisit the labels created in the previous lesson.

### Core Learning Experience

- Reread the labels and model checking whether the letters still match the picture and sound.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students help confirm or revise one part of a label.
- Point to the words and reread together.

### Check for Understanding

- Students identify one place where a label matches the picture or needs teacher-guided revision.

### Closing

- Review that writers reread and check their work.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep revision minimal and highly supported.
- Use previously created labels only.
- Accept oral and gestural responses.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses early rereading and checking in shared writing.
- Evidence matches the objective through direct participation in label review.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep revision supportive, not corrective in a discouraging way.
- Focus on sound/meaning match, not conventions overload.
