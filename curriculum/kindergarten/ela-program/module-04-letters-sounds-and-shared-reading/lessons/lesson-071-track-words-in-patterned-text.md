## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.1.C
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will track words in a short patterned text and notice repeated words.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - patterned shared text
  - pointer
- Teacher preparation:
  - select a text with a repeated line

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Reread the patterned text together.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model pointing to each word in the repeated line.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students help track one repeated line and notice where the same word appears again.

### Check for Understanding

- Students point to one repeated word in the text.

### Closing

- Review that some words show up again and again in shared reading.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use clear spacing and large print.
- Keep the text short and predictable.
- Allow air-tracking before pointer work.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses concept of word and repeated-word noticing.
- Evidence matches the objective through direct work in patterned text.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep the shared text meaningful and not overly dense.
- Focus on repeated words in context, not isolated memorization.
