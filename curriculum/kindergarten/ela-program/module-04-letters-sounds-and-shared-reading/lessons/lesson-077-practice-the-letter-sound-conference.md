## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Explain the conference routine and show each step.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model:
  - name the letter
  - say the sound
  - match the picture
  - track one line of text

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students rehearse in pairs or small groups while the teacher coaches.

### Check for Understanding

- Students complete a supported practice conference sequence.

### Closing

- Reassure students that the conference is a way to show learning clearly.
