## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.W.K.2
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.L.K.1.A
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will help create simple labels for familiar pictures using oral rehearsal and taught sounds.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - picture cards
  - chart paper
  - letter cards or markers
- Teacher preparation:
  - choose 2-3 simple label words

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Show a familiar picture and name it together.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model stretching the first sound and choosing the first letter.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students contribute sounds, letters, or known word parts while the teacher records.
- Reread each label together.

### Check for Understanding

- Students contribute at least one letter or sound to a shared label.

### Closing

- Review that writers can use sounds to help make labels.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep labels short and familiar.
- Use shared writing, not independent writing.
- Accept pointing to letters as contribution.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses early encoding contribution in shared writing.
- Evidence matches the objective through direct participation in label construction.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Maintain developmental realism.
- The teacher should carry most of the writing load while students contribute targeted pieces.
