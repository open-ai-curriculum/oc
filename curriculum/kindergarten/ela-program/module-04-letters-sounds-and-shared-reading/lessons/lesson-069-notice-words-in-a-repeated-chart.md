- Lesson objective:
  - Students will notice individual words and spaces in a short repeated chart text.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - patterned chart text
  - pointer
  - word highlight markers
- Teacher preparation:
  - prepare a short repeated line students know orally

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Reread the familiar chart together.

### Core Learning Experience

- Point to each word and briefly stop at the spaces.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students help identify where one word ends and another begins.
- Highlight one repeated word in more than one line.

### Check for Understanding

- Students point to one word and one space in the chart.

### Closing

- Review that shared text is made of words with spaces between them.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use large-print chart text.
- Keep the text predictable and familiar.
- Use color or markers sparingly for support.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses concept of word in shared text.
- Evidence matches the objective through direct print interaction.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep the chart meaningful and rereadable.
- Do not overcomplicate with too many print terms at once.
