## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.1.D
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.3.A
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will find taught focus letters in shared text and classroom print.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - shared text or chart
  - focus-letter cards
- Teacher preparation:
  - choose a text where the taught letters appear visibly

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Review the focus letters quickly.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model scanning the text for one focus letter.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students help locate and mark other examples.
- Compare uppercase and lowercase appearances when possible.

### Check for Understanding

- Students locate at least one taught letter in actual print.

### Closing

- Review that the letters we learn show up in real reading.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use uncluttered shared text.
- Limit the number of targets.
- Allow pointing and teacher confirmation.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses application of letter knowledge in context.
- Evidence matches the objective through direct location of target letters in print.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep the print manageable and familiar.
- Avoid treating this as full decoding.
