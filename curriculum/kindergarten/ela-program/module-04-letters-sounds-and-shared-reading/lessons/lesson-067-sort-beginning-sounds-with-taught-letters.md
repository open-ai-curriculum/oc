## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.2.D
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.3.A
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will sort familiar pictures by beginning sound using the taught letters as anchors.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - picture cards
  - focus-letter headers
- Teacher preparation:
  - choose two or three letter/sound categories

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Review the target letters and their sounds.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model placing one picture under the correct sound-letter header.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students sort additional pictures and explain their choices with support.

### Check for Understanding

- Students place at least one picture correctly by beginning sound.

### Closing

- Review that sounds we hear at the start of words can help us sort them.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use clear pictures and limited categories.
- Allow gesture, placement, and oral explanation.
- Repeat the sound cues often.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses the bridge between oral sound awareness and letter use.
- Evidence matches the objective through actual sort decisions.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep the focus on beginning sound only.
- Use familiar classroom vocabulary.
