## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.W.K.2
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.L.K.1.A
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will help build a simple dictated word by connecting an oral word to one or more taught letters.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - magnetic or paper letters
  - picture card
- Teacher preparation:
  - choose a simple word aligned to taught sounds

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Show the picture and say the word together.

### Core Learning Experience

- Stretch the word orally and model identifying the first sound.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students help select the first letter and, when appropriate, another known sound-letter match.
- Teacher assembles the word with heavy support.

### Check for Understanding

- Students contribute one accurate sound-letter decision.

### Closing

- Reread the built word and connect it back to the picture.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use concrete pictures and oral rehearsal.
- Limit the word complexity.
- Let students point to letters rather than write them by hand.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses early sound-to-print contribution in a shared format.
- Evidence matches the objective through direct letter selection tied to an oral word.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep the task shared and supported.
- Do not treat this as formal spelling mastery.
