## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.1.A
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.1.B
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will track a short shared-reading text with a pointer from left to right.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - short chart text
  - pointer
- Teacher preparation:
  - choose a short predictable text with large print

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Echo-read the chart once for familiarity.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model pointing under each word as the line is reread.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students air-track the line and then help point during a second read.

### Check for Understanding

- Students demonstrate where the line begins and track across with support.

### Closing

- Review that shared reading helps us follow words together.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use oversized print and clear spacing.
- Allow full-arm tracking before finger tracking.
- Keep the text short.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses print directionality in shared reading.
- Evidence matches the objective because students act on the actual text.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep the shared text meaningful and familiar.
- Do not overload with multiple new print concepts.
