## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.3.C
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will notice one or two routine high-frequency words in familiar shared text.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - familiar chart text
  - word cards for one or two routine words
- Teacher preparation:
  - select recurring words from the shared text

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Reread the shared text and listen for a word that keeps showing up.

### Core Learning Experience

- Highlight one recurring word and compare it across lines.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students help locate the recurring word in the text.
- Match the standalone word card to the chart version.

### Check for Understanding

- Students point to the target routine word when it appears in shared text.

### Closing

- Review that some words appear so often that readers begin to know them quickly.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Limit the routine word set to one or two words.
- Use large consistent word cards.
- Keep the word tied to a familiar text, not isolated flashcards only.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses noticing of routine words in context.
- Evidence matches the objective through repeated-word location in shared text.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep the work contextualized in meaningful text.
- Do not turn the lesson into broad memorization demands.
