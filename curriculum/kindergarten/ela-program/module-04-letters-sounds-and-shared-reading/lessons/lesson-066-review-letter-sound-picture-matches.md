## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.3.A
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.3.B
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will review taught letter-sound correspondences by matching letters, sounds, and picture examples.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - focus-letter cards
  - picture cards
- Teacher preparation:
  - select picture examples already used in instruction

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Review the focus letters and their common sounds.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model a three-part match: letter, sound, picture.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students complete additional matches in pairs or small groups.

### Check for Understanding

- Students successfully complete one full letter-sound-picture match.

### Closing

- Review that letters connect to sounds we hear in words.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use familiar picture vocabulary.
- Keep the set small.
- Accept pointing and oral repetition.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses cumulative letter-sound matching.
- Evidence matches the objective because students directly connect all three elements.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Use stable examples only.
- Avoid mixing newly introduced letters into the review.
