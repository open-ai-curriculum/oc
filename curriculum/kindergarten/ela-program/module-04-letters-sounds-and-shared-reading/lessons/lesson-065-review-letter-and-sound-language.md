## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - letter cards
  - picture cards
  - oral sentence stems

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Review the words letter, sound, word, and picture.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model simple oral sentences using the review words.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students use the stems with partners and in whole group.

### Check for Understanding

- Students complete at least one supported oral statement using module vocabulary.

### Closing

- Review how talking about letters and sounds helps literacy learning.
