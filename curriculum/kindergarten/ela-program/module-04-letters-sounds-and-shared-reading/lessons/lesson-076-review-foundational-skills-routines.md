## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - letter cards
  - picture cards
  - shared text
- Teacher preparation:
  - plan a short three-part review circuit

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Tell students they will show many different reading-helping skills today.

### Core Learning Experience

- Move through quick review stations:
  - name a letter
  - match a sound
  - track shared text

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students rotate through the short routines with teacher support.

### Check for Understanding

- Students successfully complete at least two of the three routine types.

### Closing

- Review how many reading-helping skills the class now knows.
