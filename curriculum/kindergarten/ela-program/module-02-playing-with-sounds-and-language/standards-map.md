## Module-Level Notes

- Reading Foundations is the center of the module, but the work remains oral and multimodal.
- Speaking and Listening is active in every lesson because oral rehearsal and participation remain essential.
- Language work is embedded through vocabulary, naming, and repeated oral sentence frames rather than isolated worksheet grammar.
