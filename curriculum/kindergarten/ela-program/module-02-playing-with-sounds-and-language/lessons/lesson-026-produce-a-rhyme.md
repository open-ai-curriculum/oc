  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.2.A

- Lesson objective:
  - Students will orally produce a simple rhyming word for a familiar word in a supported routine.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - rhyme picture cards
  - chant frame
- Teacher preparation:
  - select high-success rhyme stems

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Review that rhyme means the end sounds alike.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model producing a rhyme for a familiar word using the chant frame.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students try producing a rhyme with partner or teacher support.
- Picture cards help cue possible answers.

### Check for Understanding

- Students produce or choose a rhyming word for one familiar word.

### Closing

- Repeat one successful rhyme set together.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use known words and picture support.
- Accept approximate oral responses when instructionally useful and clarify gently.
- Keep the task brief.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses supported rhyme production.
- Evidence matches the objective because students generate a rhyming response.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Do not press for nonsense production if it confuses students.
- Keep examples culturally neutral and concrete.
