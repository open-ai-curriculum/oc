## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.1.D
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.3.A
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will review taught letters and find them in a shared classroom text or chart.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - classroom chart or enlarged familiar text
  - focus-letter cards
- Teacher preparation:
  - choose a chart where the focus letters appear visibly

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Review the focus letters with quick flashcard practice.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model scanning the chart for one focus letter.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students help find additional occurrences of the focus letters.
- Mark or circle letters together.

### Check for Understanding

- Students locate one taught letter in the shared text or chart.

### Closing

- Review that letters show up inside real print, not just on flashcards.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use large-print shared text.
- Limit the number of target letters.
- Allow pointing as evidence.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses application of taught letter knowledge in context.
- Evidence matches the objective because students locate letters in real print.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep the text familiar so the task is about noticing letters, not decoding the whole line.
- Avoid visually dense pages.
