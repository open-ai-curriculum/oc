## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - conference recording sheet
  - rhyme cards
  - syllable words
  - beginning-sound picture sort
  - taught-letter cards
- Teacher preparation:
  - prepare a brief four-part conference sequence

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Explain the conference tasks in simple language and model one example.

### Core Learning Experience

- Students rotate through teacher checks:
  - identify a rhyme
  - clap syllables
  - sort or identify a beginning sound
  - name and match a taught letter

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students not in conference complete familiar review stations or partner sound-play routines.

### Check for Understanding

- Teacher records evidence for each student in the conference sheet.

### Closing

- Celebrate the class's growth in hearing sounds and knowing letters.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes
