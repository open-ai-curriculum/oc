## Standards and Objective

  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.2.B
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.SL.K.1
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will review rhyme and syllables while joining a short repeated oral text.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - short chant or poem
  - picture cards for target words
- Teacher preparation:
  - identify where to stop for rhyme and syllable prompts

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Echo-read the repeated text with rhythm.

### Core Learning Experience

- Pause to identify a rhyming pair and clap syllables in one or two familiar words from the text.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students join the chant, clap syllables, and respond to rhyme prompts.

### Check for Understanding

- Students demonstrate both one rhyme and one syllable response during the routine.

### Closing

- Reflect that we can hear many language patterns in the same text.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use repetition and predictable phrasing.
- Support with gestures and picture cards.
- Accept varied movement responses.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses cumulative oral sound-play skills.
- Evidence matches the objective because the skills are applied in a live repeated text routine.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep the oral text engaging and brief.
- Avoid making the text a decoding task yet.
