## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - rhyme cards
  - focus-letter cards
  - small-group mats
- Teacher preparation:
  - prepare two short activity stations

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Review the two activity types students will rotate through.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model one rhyme station and one letter station.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students rotate through the stations with teacher support.
- One station focuses on rhyme matches; the other on letter-name and letter-pair review.

### Check for Understanding

- Students successfully complete one rhyme and one letter routine.

### Closing

- Review that both sound listening and letter knowledge are part of becoming readers.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep station tasks highly modeled.
- Limit materials at each station.
- Use partner support.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses cumulative performance across two early foundational routines.
- Evidence matches the objective through hands-on and oral demonstration.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep stations short and tightly structured for kindergarten stamina.
- Avoid making the tasks competitive.
