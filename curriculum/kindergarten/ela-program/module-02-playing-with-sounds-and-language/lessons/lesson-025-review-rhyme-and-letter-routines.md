## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Repeat the review chant for sounds and letters.

### Core Learning Experience

- Cycle through rhyme, letter names, and matching routines.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students respond in groups and with partners.
- Use quick checks such as "show me the match" and "clap when you hear the rhyme."

### Check for Understanding

- Students accurately participate in at least two routine types.

### Closing

- Review what the class now knows about rhymes and letters.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Alternate whole-body, oral, and visual responses.
- Use repetition and praise for participation.
