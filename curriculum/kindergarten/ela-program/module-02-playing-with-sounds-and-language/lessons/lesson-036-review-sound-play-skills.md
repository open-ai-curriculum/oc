## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - mixed sound-play cards
  - review chant
- Teacher preparation:
  - plan a three-part review circuit

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Explain that today the class will show all the sound-play skills learned so far.

### Core Learning Experience

- Move through a quick sequence:
  - hear a rhyme
  - clap a syllable
  - sort a beginning sound

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students rotate through the review sequence with teacher support.

### Check for Understanding

- Students participate accurately in at least two of the three review types.

### Closing

- Celebrate the class as strong sound listeners.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep each review turn brief.
- Alternate movement and listening.
- Use familiar visuals only.
