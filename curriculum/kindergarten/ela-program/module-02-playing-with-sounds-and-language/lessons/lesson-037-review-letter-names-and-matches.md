## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - focus-letter cards
  - match mats
- Teacher preparation:
  - prepare quick individual or small-group checks

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Sing the letter review chant.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model a quick match and name routine.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students match letters, name them, and explain which ones belong together.

### Check for Understanding

- Students correctly identify and match taught letters during review.

### Closing

- Review that the class knows many more letters now than at the start of the module.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use large-print cards and uncluttered layouts.
- Keep the card set limited to taught letters.
- Allow nonverbal demonstration plus teacher confirmation.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson formatively assesses cumulative alphabet launch skills.
- Evidence matches the objective because students must match and name the actual letters.

## Subject-Specific Notes
