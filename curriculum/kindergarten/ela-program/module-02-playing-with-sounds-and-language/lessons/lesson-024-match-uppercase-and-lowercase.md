- Lesson objective:
  - Students will match uppercase and lowercase versions of taught letters with support.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - matching letter cards
  - pocket chart or floor-mat for sorting
- Teacher preparation:
  - prepare one small set of taught pairs

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Revisit the focus letters and show that the same letter can look two ways.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model matching one uppercase letter to its lowercase partner.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students help match the remaining taught letters.
- Partners play a quick memory or find-the-match routine.

### Check for Understanding

- Students correctly match at least one uppercase/lowercase pair.

### Closing

- Review that the letters belong together even if they look a little different.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use large cards with simple fonts.
- Limit the number of pairs.
- Allow physical movement of cards.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses matching of taught letter pairs.
- Evidence matches the objective through direct manipulation of letter cards.

## Subject-Specific Notes
