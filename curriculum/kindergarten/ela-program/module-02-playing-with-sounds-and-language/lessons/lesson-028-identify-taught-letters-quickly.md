## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.1.D
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.3.A
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will identify a small taught set of letters by name with increasing speed and confidence.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - focus-letter cards
  - pocket chart
- Teacher preparation:
  - prepare a quick warm-up routine using only taught letters

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Review the focus letters through a chant.

### Core Learning Experience

- Hold up letters one at a time and model naming them smoothly.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students respond chorally, in pairs, and individually.
- Use "find it," "show it," and "match it" routines.

### Check for Understanding

- Students correctly identify multiple taught letters during the routine.

### Closing

- Celebrate the growing alphabet knowledge of the class.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep the taught set small.
- Use large clear cards.
- Accept pointing before oral naming if needed.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson formatively assesses letter-name recognition.
- Evidence matches the objective because students must identify the taught cards directly.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep confidence high by avoiding untaught distractors.
- Use varied response modes to prevent fatigue.
