## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.2.D
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will notice and compare the beginning sounds in familiar spoken words.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - picture cards
  - sound sort mats
- Teacher preparation:
  - choose two clear contrasting beginning sounds

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Say two familiar words slowly and ask if they start the same or different.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model listening to the beginning sound and comparing words.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students sort pictures by beginning sound with teacher support.
- Repeat the target sounds together.

### Check for Understanding

- Students identify or sort one picture by beginning sound.

### Closing

- Review that we can listen to the start of a word.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Use picture-rich materials and repeated oral models.
- Keep the number of sound categories low.
- Accept gesture and picture placement as evidence.

## Assessment Notes

- Evidence matches the objective because students compare actual spoken words.

## Subject-Specific Notes
