## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.4
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.3.C
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will review a familiar controlled text and the routine words it contains.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - familiar controlled text
  - routine word cards
- Teacher preparation:
  - mark the target routine words in the text

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Preview the text and routine words quickly.

### Core Learning Experience

- Reread the text and pause at the routine words.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students match the word cards to the text and reread them in context.

### Check for Understanding

- Students identify target routine words during a supported reread.

### Closing

- Review that noticing frequent words helps reading feel smoother.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep the word set small and familiar.
- Use strong teacher modeling.
- Accept pointing as evidence.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses review of controlled-text reading and routine-word recognition.
- Evidence matches the objective through direct text interaction.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep the routine words anchored to meaning and context.
- Avoid isolated flashcard-only work here.
