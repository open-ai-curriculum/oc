## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.4
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will reread a short controlled text with greater confidence and smoother teacher-supported phrasing.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - controlled text from Lesson 106
  - pointer
- Teacher preparation:
  - mark one or two places for strategic prompting

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Briefly revisit the text picture and key words.

### Core Learning Experience

- Reread the text together, stopping less often than the first day.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students reread chorally, then in pairs or echo format.

### Check for Understanding

- Students show more confident participation and word tracking during reread.

### Closing

- Reflect that rereading helps readers sound smoother and feel more confident.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep the text and task familiar.
- Use echo reading for support.
- Accept choral success as meaningful evidence.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses supported rereading of controlled text.
- Evidence matches the objective through actual rereading performance.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Rereading should build accuracy and confidence, not speed pressure.
- Keep teacher prompts focused and minimal.
