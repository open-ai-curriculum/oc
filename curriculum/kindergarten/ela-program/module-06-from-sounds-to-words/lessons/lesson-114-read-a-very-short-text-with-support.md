## Standards and Objective

  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.4
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will read a very short familiar controlled text with heavy teacher support and growing confidence.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - short controlled text
  - pointer
- Teacher preparation:
  - choose a familiar text aligned to current patterns

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Preview the text and review one or two key known words.

### Core Learning Experience

- Teacher models the first read, then rereads with the class.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students echo-read, choral-read, and then try a brief partner-supported reread.

### Check for Understanding

- Students show active supported participation in reading the text.

### Closing

- Reflect that rereading short texts helps readers grow stronger.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep the text extremely short and familiar.
- Use echo reading and choral reading.
- Do not require independent full-text reading.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses early supported text reading.
- Evidence matches the objective through direct participation in reading.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Choose semantically coherent text.
- Keep the task teacher-mediated and low-stakes.
