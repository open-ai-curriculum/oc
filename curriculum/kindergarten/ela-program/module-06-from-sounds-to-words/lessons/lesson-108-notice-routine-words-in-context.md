## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.3.C
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will notice one or two routine high-frequency words in the controlled text and related shared text.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - controlled text
  - routine word cards
- Teacher preparation:
  - choose one or two routine words that recur in class texts

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Reread the text and listen for a word that appears again.

### Core Learning Experience

- Highlight one target routine word in the text and match it to a standalone card.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students help locate the word again in a line of text.
- Use quick matching and pointing routines.

### Check for Understanding

- Students identify the target routine word in context.

### Closing

- Review that some words become familiar because we see them often.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Limit the number of routine words.
- Keep them tied to real text.
- Use large consistent word cards.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses noticing high-frequency routine words in context.
- Evidence matches the objective through real text identification.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep the routine words contextualized.
- Do not turn this into isolated memorization drills.
