## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.W.K.2
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will help build and reread a short shared sentence using known sounds, words, and oral rehearsal.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - chart paper
  - marker
  - prompt picture
- Teacher preparation:
  - choose a simple sentence linked to the controlled text or a shared experience

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Discuss the picture or shared experience in one short sentence.

### Core Learning Experience

- Orally rehearse the sentence several times.
- Model writing the first word or sound with student help.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students contribute additional sounds, letters, or routine words while the teacher records.
- Reread the sentence after each addition.

### Check for Understanding

- Students contribute at least one meaningful element to the shared sentence.

### Closing

- Celebrate that the class used reading knowledge to help write.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep the sentence short and familiar.
- Use oral rehearsal before any writing step.
- Accept pointing and verbal contribution in place of handwriting.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses shared sentence construction and rereading.
- Evidence matches the objective through direct participation in building the sentence.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep the writing burden appropriately scaffolded.
