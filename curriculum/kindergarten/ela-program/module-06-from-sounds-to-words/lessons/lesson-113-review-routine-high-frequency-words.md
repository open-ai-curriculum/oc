## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.3.C
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will review a small routine set of high-frequency words in familiar text and cards.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - routine word cards
  - familiar controlled/shared texts
- Teacher preparation:
  - select one or two review words only

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Review the routine words in the familiar text.

### Core Learning Experience

- Match the word cards to the text and reread them in context.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students point, match, and reread the target words with support.

### Check for Understanding

- Students identify one routine word in both card and text form.

### Closing

- Review that some words become familiar because we see them often in reading.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep the word set minimal.
- Keep the words tied to familiar text.
- Use large clear cards.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses high-frequency word notice in context.
- Evidence matches the objective through direct identification and rereading.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Maintain context; do not detach words from meaning.
- Avoid overloading the lesson with too many target words.
