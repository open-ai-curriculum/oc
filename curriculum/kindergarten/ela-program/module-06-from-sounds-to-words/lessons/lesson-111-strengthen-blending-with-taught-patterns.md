## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Review oral blending with one familiar word.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model building and reading a new controlled word from taught patterns.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students help blend and build additional words.

### Check for Understanding

- Students successfully blend and read at least one additional controlled word.

### Closing

- Review that known sounds and letters can help us unlock more words.
