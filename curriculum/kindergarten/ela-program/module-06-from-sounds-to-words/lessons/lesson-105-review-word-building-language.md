## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - letter cards
  - picture cards
  - oral stems
- Teacher preparation:
  - prepare simple review prompts

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Review the terms sound, letter, word, blend, and read.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model short oral statements using the review vocabulary.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students use partner talk and whole-group responses to describe what they are doing in word work.

### Check for Understanding

- Students complete at least one oral statement about a word-building routine.

### Closing

- Review that strong readers talk about how words work.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep the stems short.
- Accept approximated oral language when meaning is clear.

## Subject-Specific Notes
