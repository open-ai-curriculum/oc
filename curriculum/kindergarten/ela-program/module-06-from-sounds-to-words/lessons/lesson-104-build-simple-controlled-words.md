## Standards and Objective

  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.3.A
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.3.B
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will build simple controlled words with taught letters and reread them with support.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - letter cards
  - sound boxes
  - picture cues
- Teacher preparation:
  - prepare a small set of buildable taught-pattern words

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Review one word build from the previous lesson.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model building and rereading a simple word with movable letters.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students help build additional words and say them together.

### Check for Understanding

- Students contribute to building and rereading one controlled word.

### Closing

- Review that readers can use letters and sounds to work through simple words.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep the words short and concrete.
- Use picture support.
- Allow pointing and oral participation rather than handwriting.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses supported word-building from taught patterns.
- Evidence matches the objective through direct word construction and rereading.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep meaning visible through pictures and oral context.
- Avoid semantically empty examples when possible.
