## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.W.K.2
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.L.K.1.A
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will help dictate a simple sentence from a shared experience using oral rehearsal and taught letters/words.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - chart paper
  - marker
  - shared picture or experience prompt
- Teacher preparation:
  - choose a simple sentence frame

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Discuss the shared picture or event in one short oral sentence.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model saying the sentence slowly and listening for the first word and first sound.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students help say the sentence, identify a word, or suggest a first letter while the teacher records.

### Check for Understanding

- Students contribute one word, sound, or letter to the shared sentence.

### Closing

- Reread the completed sentence together.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep the sentence short and meaningful.
- Use oral rehearsal before writing.
- Allow students to contribute by pointing, saying, or selecting.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses shared sentence construction and oral-to-print connection.
- Evidence matches the objective through direct sentence-building contribution.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Teacher should carry most of the writing load.
- Focus on connecting talk to print, not sentence conventions mastery.
