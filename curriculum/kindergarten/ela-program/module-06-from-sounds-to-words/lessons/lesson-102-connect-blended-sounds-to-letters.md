## Standards and Objective

  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.2.E
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.RF.K.3.A
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will connect blended sounds to taught letters while building a simple word with support.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - letter cards
  - picture cards
  - sound boxes or mats
- Teacher preparation:
  - prepare 2-3 simple buildable words using taught letters

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Review the oral blending routine from the prior lesson.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model blending a word orally, then placing the matching letters in order.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students help place letters into sound boxes.
- Reread the built word together.

### Check for Understanding

- Students contribute at least one correct letter in the word build.

### Closing

- Review that letters help show the sounds we blend.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep words short and familiar.
- Use large movable letters.
- Allow pointing and oral confirmation instead of requiring writing.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses the first bridge from oral blending to letter representation.
- Evidence matches the objective through direct word-building participation.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Avoid building words with untaught patterns.
- Keep the emphasis on sound-letter matching, not speed.
