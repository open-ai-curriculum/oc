  - sound boxes

  - plan a short four-part review circuit

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Explain that the class will show many different word-reading skills today.

### Core Learning Experience

- Review:
  - blend a word
  - segment a word
  - read a controlled word
  - reread a shared sentence

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students rotate through the routines with teacher support.

### Check for Understanding

- Students successfully complete at least two routine types.

### Closing

- Review how sound, letter, reading, and writing work together.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Alternate oral and hands-on responses.
- Provide strong modeling throughout.
