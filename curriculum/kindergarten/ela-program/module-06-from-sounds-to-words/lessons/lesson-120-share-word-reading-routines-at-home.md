## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.SL.K.4
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.SL.K.6
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will rehearse one or two simple word-reading and word-building routines they can share with a family member or caregiver.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - take-home routine card
  - mini word cards
  - picture cue
- Teacher preparation:
  - prepare one short family-facing explanation of the routine

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Tell students they will practice showing someone at home how they can blend sounds and read a simple word.

### Core Learning Experience

- Model a home routine:
  - say the sounds
  - blend the word
  - point to the letters

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students practice with partners and review the take-home card.

### Check for Understanding

- Students demonstrate one take-home routine successfully with support.

### Closing

- Celebrate the end of Module 6 and the class's reading growth.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep the home routine oral and visual.
- Use family-inclusive language.
- Provide options that do not require special home materials.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson checks transfer of simple word-reading routines beyond the classroom.
- Evidence matches the objective because students rehearse the exact routine they will share.

## Subject-Specific Notes

- Keep the home routine short and realistic.
- Avoid turning family practice into burdensome homework.
