## Standards and Objective

- Standards addressed:
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.W.K.2
  - CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.L.K.1.A
- Lesson objective:
  - Students will apply sound-letter knowledge by helping write and reread a short shared sentence using taught words and patterns.

## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - chart paper
  - marker
  - picture or prompt card
- Teacher preparation:
  - choose a sentence that uses one or more known words or patterns

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Review one familiar controlled word and one routine word from prior lessons.

### Core Learning Experience

- Orally rehearse a short sentence that uses those known pieces.

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Students help contribute words, sounds, and letters while the teacher writes.
- Reread the sentence after construction.

### Check for Understanding

- Students identify at least one place where a known sound or word appears in the sentence.

### Closing

- Review that reading knowledge can help us write sentences too.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Keep the sentence short and highly supported.
- Use oral rehearsal and pointing throughout.
- Accept verbal and gestural contributions.

## Assessment Notes

- This lesson assesses application of taught reading knowledge in shared writing.
- Evidence matches the objective through direct sentence contribution and rereading.
