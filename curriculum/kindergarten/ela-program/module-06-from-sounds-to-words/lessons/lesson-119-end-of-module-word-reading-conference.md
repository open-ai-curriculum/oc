## Materials and Preparation

- Materials needed:
  - conference sheet
  - sound boxes
  - letter cards
  - controlled word cards
  - shared sentence prompt
- Teacher preparation:
  - set up a brief four-part conference sequence

## Lesson Flow

### Opening

- Explain the conference steps simply and model one example.

### Core Learning Experience

- Students rotate through teacher checks:
  - blend a simple word
  - segment a simple word
  - read or identify a controlled word
  - contribute to a shared sentence

### Guided Practice / Discussion / Investigation

- Other students complete familiar review routines while waiting.

### Check for Understanding

- Teacher records evidence for each student on the conference sheet.

### Closing

- Celebrate the class's growth from sounds into real word reading and writing routines.

## Accessibility and Inclusion Notes

- Allow gesture, pointing, oral response, and teacher-supported writing contribution.
- Keep prompts brief and concrete.
