# Kindergarten ELA Standards Bands

## Purpose

Map the kindergarten ELA program to major standards bands so the annual architecture has traceable alignment.

## Standards-Band Groups

### Reading Foundations

Primary kindergarten emphasis:

- print concepts
- phonological awareness
- alphabet knowledge
- letter-sound correspondences
- high-frequency words in supported contexts
- early decoding and word recognition

Representative CCSS bands:

- RF.K.1
- RF.K.2
- RF.K.3
- RF.K.4 as developmentally appropriate early fluency support

### Reading Literature And Reading Informational

Primary kindergarten emphasis:

- asking and answering questions about key details
- retelling familiar stories
- identifying topic, key details, and basic text features in informational texts
- comparing and connecting ideas in supported talk

Representative CCSS bands:

- RL.K.1 through RL.K.7
- RI.K.1 through RI.K.7

### Speaking And Listening

Primary kindergarten emphasis:

- collaborative conversations
- understanding read-alouds and oral presentations
- speaking clearly about familiar texts and topics
- using drawings, gestures, and oral language to communicate

Representative CCSS bands:

- SL.K.1 through SL.K.6

### Language

Primary kindergarten emphasis:

- conventions in oral and emergent written language
- vocabulary growth
- question words, nouns, verbs, and sentence work in developmentally appropriate contexts

Representative CCSS bands:

- L.K.1 through L.K.6

### Writing

Primary kindergarten emphasis:

- drawing, dictating, and writing
- composing opinion, informative, and narrative responses at kindergarten level
- shared and interactive writing

Representative CCSS bands:

- W.K.1 through W.K.8
