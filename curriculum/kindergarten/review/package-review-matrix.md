## Reviewer Prompts By Dimension

### Sequence fit

- Does this package belong in the current order?
- Is it prerequisite knowledge, concurrent work, or later reinforcement?

### Grade-level fit

- Are the tasks, prompts, and supports appropriate for kindergarten?
- Does the package avoid pushing too quickly into abstract or worksheet-heavy work?

### Unit coherence

- Does the unit goal match the standards and lesson sequence?
- Is there a visible instructional throughline?

### Lesson usability

- Could a teacher enact the lesson without major guesswork?
- Are materials, routines, and timing plausible?

### Support-material usability

- Where supports exist, does the teacher guide improve enactment?
- Where supports exist, are observation tools usable during instruction?
- Where supports exist, do learner or family supports help without replacing the task?
- Where supports do not exist, is that an acceptable exception or a repository gap?

### Accessibility and inclusion

- Are multimodal response paths clear?
- Are examples, texts, and assumptions inclusive?
- Are barriers reduced rather than shifted onto the learner?

### Knowledge / content integrity

- Are the examples, selected texts, phenomena, movement prompts, and routines instructionally sound?
- Are there inaccuracies, weak examples, or developmentally awkward moves?
