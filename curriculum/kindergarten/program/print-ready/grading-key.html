Calibration and Access Notes

5. Counting and CardinalityCounts each object once, names the total, and compares two small sets.Counts with minor errors or prompting.Does not sustain one-to-one counting or connect the final number to quantity.
6. Weather and ObservationIdentifies a weather condition, matches a symbol or record, and contributes one visible observation.Notices with prompting or uses only one part of the record task.Response does not align to the observed weather.
