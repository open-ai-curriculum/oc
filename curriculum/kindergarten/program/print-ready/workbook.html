Home Practice Strip

  • Read a book and show where reading starts.
  • Retell a familiar story.
  • Play a rhyme game.
  • Notice letters around you.
  • Count small objects.
  • Look at the weather.
  • Name a helper.
  • Make and share something creative.
  • Name a feeling and a safe choice.
  • Move and freeze.
  • Talk about steps in order.
  • Use tools carefully with a grown-up.
