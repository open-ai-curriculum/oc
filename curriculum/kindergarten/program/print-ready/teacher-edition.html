Chapter 6: Weather and Observation

Lesson Focus

Students observe the weather and help record what they notice.

Core Teaching Moves

  • Observe outside or use a weather image.
  • Compare today with another recent day.
  • Encourage evidence-based weather statements.
Asset References

Use daily weather chart and weather icon cards from reproducibles.

Exemplar Evidence

Student says “Cloudy” and records clouds in the journal box.
