# Kindergarten Reproducible Teacher-Use Assets

## Purpose

Provide assembled teacher-use masters that can be copied or reformatted into classroom handouts, centers, observation sheets, and family-facing supports.

## Included Reproducibles

### Literacy

- book-parts picture card set
- first / next / last retell cards
- rhyme picture match cards
- uppercase / lowercase letter cards

### Math

- count-and-touch mat
- compare-two-sets strip
- numeral card strip for taught numbers

### Science

- daily weather chart
- weather icon cards
- observation drawing frame

### Social Studies

- helper sort cards
- home / school / community picture mat

### Arts

- create-from-a-prompt card set
- share-my-work response strip

### Health

- feelings faces cards
- calm-choice poster

### Physical Education

- move / freeze cue cards
- balance station visuals

### Technology

- first / next / last sequencing cards
- safe tool-use reminder card

## Reproducible Master: Count-and-Touch Mat

- Draw or place up to 5 objects in the box.
- Touch each one as you count.
- Say the last number again to tell how many.

## Reproducible Master: Helper Sort

- Cut out helper pictures.
- Place each helper under:
  - home
  - school
  - community

## Reproducible Master: Feelings and Safe Choice Strip

- I feel: __________
- I can:
  - breathe
  - ask for help
  - take a calm break
