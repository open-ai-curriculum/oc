# Accessibility and Inclusion Research

## Purpose

This section will define the operational accessibility and inclusive-design baseline for curriculum artifacts produced by the project.

It should cover:

- UDL 3.0 implications for curriculum design
- WCAG 2.2 implications for digital delivery
- accessible documents and media
- offline and printable access
- multilingual and diverse learner support
