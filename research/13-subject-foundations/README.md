# Subject Foundations Research

## Purpose

This section establishes subject-specific research foundations for future curriculum development.

The repository already has strong general guidance on design, governance, accessibility, review, and interoperability. This section begins the next layer: discipline-specific instructional foundations.

## Current Scope

Initial subject foundations have been added for:

- mathematics
- literacy and language arts
- science
- civics and history

Math and literacy now also include deeper design and assessment/task guidance.

These are starting points, not complete production blueprints.

## Why This Section Exists

A project can have excellent general process guidance and still produce weak curriculum if it lacks subject-specific instructional logic.

This section is intended to reduce that risk by documenting:

- key design considerations by discipline
- relevant standards ecosystems
- common quality risks
- immediate implications for future unit and lesson development
